20. Entropy-regularized reinforcement learning

Entropy-regularized reinforcement learning records the objects, main results, proof mechanisms, and boundary cases needed for the reinforcement learning note.

Definition

Definition. Entropy-regularized RL adds an entropy bonus to reward, encouraging stochastic policies and smoothing the Bellman optimality operator.

Proposition

Proposition. The entropy-regularized optimal policy has a softmax form, and the optimal value satisfies a soft Bellman equation using log-sum-exp instead of max.

Proof

Proof. Optimize the regularized one-step objective over action distributions. Convex duality between negative entropy and log-sum-exp gives the softmax and soft value expression.

Remark

Remark. Entropy regularization links RL to convex duality, control-as-inference, and linearly solvable control.
