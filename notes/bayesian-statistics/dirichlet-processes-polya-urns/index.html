24. Dirichlet processes and Pólya urn schemes

This page records the main definitions, propositions, and proof ideas for Dirichlet processes and Pólya urn schemes.

Definition

Definition. \(G\sim\operatorname{DP}(\alpha G_0)\) if every finite partition has Dirichlet-distributed masses.

Proposition

Proposition. If \(X_i\mid G\sim G\), then \[ G\mid X_1,\ldots,X_n\sim\operatorname{DP}\left(\alpha G_0+\sum_{i=1}^n\delta_{X_i}\right). \]

Proof

Proof. On each finite partition, this is ordinary Dirichlet-multinomial conjugacy.

Remarks

The predictive law has atoms at observed values, which explains clustering.
