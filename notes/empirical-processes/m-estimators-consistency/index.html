25. M-estimators and consistency

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/z-estimators/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/rates-of-m-estimators/" %}

M-estimators optimize empirical criteria. Consistency follows when empirical criteria converge uniformly to a population criterion with a well-separated optimum.

Definition. An approximate M-estimator \(\hat\theta\) satisfies \(P_nm_{\hat\theta}\ge \sup_{\theta\in\Theta}P_nm_\theta-o_p(1)\).

Core facts

  • A GC condition for \(\{m_\theta:\theta\in\Theta\}\) makes \(P_nm_\theta\) uniformly close to \(Pm_\theta\).
  • Identification requires \(Pm_{\theta_0}\) to dominate \(Pm_\theta\) away from \(\theta_0\).
  • Compactness can be replaced by local arguments and tightness of \(\hat\theta\).

Use

This page provides the consistency half of M-estimation; rates and limit distributions need local empirical-process refinements.

25. M-estimateurs et consistance

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/z-estimators/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/rates-of-m-estimators/" %}

Les M-estimateurs optimisent des critères empiriques. La consistance suit d'une convergence uniforme vers un critère population bien séparé.

Définition. Un M-estimateur approximatif \(\hat\theta\) vérifie \(P_nm_{\hat\theta}\ge \sup_{\theta\in\Theta}P_nm_\theta-o_p(1)\).

Faits clés

  • Une condition GC pour \(\{m_\theta:\theta\in\Theta\}\) rend \(P_nm_\theta\) uniformément proche de \(Pm_\theta\).
  • L'identification demande que \(Pm_{\theta_0}\) domine \(Pm_\theta\) loin de \(\theta_0\).
  • La compacité peut être remplacée par des arguments locaux et la tension de \(\hat\theta\).

Usage

Cette page donne la partie consistance; les vitesses et lois limites demandent des raffinements locaux.

25. M-估计量与一致性

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/z-estimators/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/rates-of-m-estimators/" %}

M-估计量优化经验准则。当经验准则一致收敛到具有良好分离最优点的总体准则时，可得一致性。

定义. 近似 M-估计量 \(\hat\theta\) 满足 \(P_nm_{\hat\theta}\ge \sup_{\theta\in\Theta}P_nm_\theta-o_p(1)\)。

核心事实

  • \(\{m_\theta:\theta\in\Theta\}\) 的 GC 条件使 \(P_nm_\theta\) 与 \(Pm_\theta\) 一致接近。
  • 识别性要求 \(Pm_{\theta_0}\) 在远离 \(\theta_0\) 时严格优于 \(Pm_\theta\)。
  • 紧性假设可由局部论证和 \(\hat\theta\) 的 tightness 替代。

用途

这一页给出 M-估计的一致性部分；收敛速度和极限分布需要局部经验过程精化。
