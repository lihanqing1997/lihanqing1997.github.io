7. Glivenko-Cantelli theorems

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/empirical-distribution-functions/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/donsker-and-gaussian-limits/" %}

Glivenko-Cantelli theory is the uniform law of large numbers. It is the main empirical-process tool for consistency.

Definition. A class \(\mathcal F\) is \(P\)-Glivenko-Cantelli if \(\|P_n-P\|_{\mathcal F}=\sup_{f\in\mathcal F}|P_nf-Pf|\to0\) in outer probability.

Uniform laws of large numbers

  • Finite classes are GC by Hoeffding plus a union bound.
  • Bracketing entropy and uniform entropy turn finite-class arguments into infinite-class theorems.
  • The theorem is distribution-dependent unless the entropy bound is uniform in \(P\).

M-estimator consistency

For consistency of an M-estimator, prove that the criterion class is GC and that the population criterion has a well-separated maximizer.

Bracketing Glivenko-Cantelli criterion

Theorem. A standard sufficient condition is an integrable envelope \(F\) with \(PF<\infty\) and bracketing numbers satisfying \(N_{[]}(\varepsilon,\mathcal F,L_1(P))<\infty\) for every \(\varepsilon>0\). Stronger entropy conditions give rates for the convergence.

Consistency argument

For M-estimation, take \(\mathcal F=\{m_\theta:\theta\in\Theta\}\). GC says the random surface \(P_nm_\theta\) is uniformly close to the deterministic surface \(Pm_\theta\). Then identification of the maximum of \(Pm_\theta\) turns into consistency of the empirical maximizer.

7. Théorèmes de Glivenko-Cantelli

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/empirical-distribution-functions/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/donsker-and-gaussian-limits/" %}

La théorie de Glivenko-Cantelli est la loi uniforme des grands nombres. Elle fournit l'outil principal pour la consistance.

Définition. Une classe \(\mathcal F\) est \(P\)-Glivenko-Cantelli si \(\|P_n-P\|_{\mathcal F}=\sup_{f\in\mathcal F}|P_nf-Pf|\to0\) en probabilité extérieure.

Lois uniformes des grands nombres

  • Les classes finies sont GC par Hoeffding et union bound.
  • L'entropie par bracketing et l'entropie uniforme généralisent l'argument aux classes infinies.
  • Le résultat dépend de \(P\) sauf si les bornes d'entropie sont uniformes en \(P\).

Consistance des M-estimateurs

Pour la consistance d'un M-estimateur, on montre que la classe de critères est GC et que le critère population a un maximiseur bien séparé.

Critère de Glivenko-Cantelli par bracketing

Théorème. Une condition suffisante standard est une enveloppe intégrable \(F\) avec \(PF<\infty\) et \(N_{[]}(\varepsilon,\mathcal F,L_1(P))<\infty\) pour tout \(\varepsilon>0\). Des conditions d'entropie plus fortes donnent des vitesses.

Argument de consistance

Pour un M-estimateur, prendre \(\mathcal F=\{m_\theta:\theta\in\Theta\}\). GC dit que la surface aléatoire \(P_nm_\theta\) est uniformément proche de \(Pm_\theta\); l'identification donne ensuite la consistance.

7. 格里文科-坎泰利定理

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/empirical-distribution-functions/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/donsker-and-gaussian-limits/" %}

格里文科-坎泰利理论就是统一大数定律。它是证明一致性的主要经验过程工具。

定义. 若 \(\|P_n-P\|_{\mathcal F}=\sup_{f\in\mathcal F}|P_nf-Pf|\to0\) 依外概率成立，则称 \(\mathcal F\) 为 \(P\)-格里文科-坎泰利类。

统一大数定律

  • 有限类由霍夫丁不等式加 union bound 得到 GC 性质。
  • 括号化熵和一致熵把有限类论证推广到无限类。
  • 除非熵界对 \(P\) 一致，否则结论通常依赖于分布 \(P\)。

M-估计量一致性

证明 M-估计量一致性时，先证明准则函数类是 GC，再证明总体准则的最大点被良好分离。

括号化格里文科-坎泰利准则

定理. 一个标准充分条件是存在可积 envelope \(F\)，满足 \(PF<\infty\)，并且对每个 \(\varepsilon>0\)，\(N_{[]}(\varepsilon,\mathcal F,L_1(P))<\infty\)。更强的熵条件还可给出速度。

一致性论证

在 M-估计中取 \(\mathcal F=\{m_\theta:\theta\in\Theta\}\)。GC 表明随机曲面 \(P_nm_\theta\) 与确定性曲面 \(Pm_\theta\) 一致接近；总体最大点的识别性进一步给出经验最大点的一致性。
