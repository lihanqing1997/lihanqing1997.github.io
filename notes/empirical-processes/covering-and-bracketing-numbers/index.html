10. Covering and bracketing numbers

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/stochastic-equicontinuity-and-applications/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/symmetrization-and-rademacher-processes/" %}

Entropy measures how many simple representatives are needed to approximate a function class. It is the basic quantitative notion of size.

Definition. \(N(\varepsilon,\mathcal F,L_2(Q))\) is the smallest number of \(L_2(Q)\)-balls of radius \(\varepsilon\) covering \(\mathcal F\). \(N_{[]}(\varepsilon,\mathcal F,L_2(P))\) counts brackets \([\ell,u]\) with \(\|u-\ell\|_{P,2}<\varepsilon\).

Core facts

  • Covering numbers approximate functions by centers; bracketing approximates them from above and below.
  • Uniform entropy takes a supremum over finitely discrete probability measures \(Q\).
  • Finite entropy at every scale supports GC results; integrable square-root entropy supports Donsker results.

Use

Most empirical-process proofs reduce to bounding one of these entropy functions at the right scale.

Criterion to remember

Guide. Covering and bracketing are related but not identical. Brackets are stronger because they order functions pointwise; hence bracketing entropy often gives easier measurability and LLN arguments, while covering entropy is more flexible for symmetrized processes.

Example and proof guide

A bracket \([\ell,u]\) represents all \(f\) satisfying \(\ell\le f\le u\). Its statistical width is measured by \(\|u-\ell\|_{P,2}\), not by pointwise width. Thus a bracket may be wide on a very small-probability set and still be useful.

10. Nombres de recouvrement et de bracketing

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/stochastic-equicontinuity-and-applications/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/symmetrization-and-rademacher-processes/" %}

L'entropie mesure combien de représentants simples sont nécessaires pour approximer une classe de fonctions. C'est la notion quantitative de taille.

Définition. \(N(\varepsilon,\mathcal F,L_2(Q))\) est le nombre minimal de boules \(L_2(Q)\) de rayon \(\varepsilon\) couvrant \(\mathcal F\). \(N_{[]}(\varepsilon,\mathcal F,L_2(P))\) compte les crochets \([\ell,u]\) tels que \(\|u-\ell\|_{P,2}<\varepsilon\).

Faits clés

  • Les recouvrements approximent par centres; le bracketing approxime par bornes supérieure et inférieure.
  • L'entropie uniforme prend un supremum sur les mesures discrètes finies \(Q\).
  • Une entropie finie à chaque échelle soutient GC; une racine d'entropie intégrable soutient Donsker.

Usage

La plupart des preuves se ramènent à borner l'une de ces fonctions d'entropie à la bonne échelle.

Critère à retenir

Guide. Recouvrement et bracketing sont liés mais distincts. Le bracketing est plus fort car il ordonne les fonctions ponctuellement; l'entropie de recouvrement est souvent plus flexible pour les processus symétrisés.

Exemple et guide de preuve

Un crochet \([\ell,u]\) contient les \(f\) tels que \(\ell\le f\le u\). Sa largeur statistique est \(\|u-\ell\|_{P,2}\), non sa largeur ponctuelle; il peut donc être large sur un ensemble rare.

10. 覆盖数与 Bracketing 数

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/stochastic-equicontinuity-and-applications/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/symmetrization-and-rademacher-processes/" %}

熵衡量需要多少简单代表函数才能逼近一个函数类，是函数类大小的基本定量概念。

定义. \(N(\varepsilon,\mathcal F,L_2(Q))\) 是用半径 \(\varepsilon\) 的 \(L_2(Q)\) 球覆盖 \(\mathcal F\) 所需的最小数量。\(N_{[]}(\varepsilon,\mathcal F,L_2(P))\) 统计满足 \(\|u-\ell\|_{P,2}<\varepsilon\) 的 brackets \([\ell,u]\)。

核心事实

  • 覆盖数用中心逼近函数；bracketing 用上下界同时夹住函数。
  • 一致熵对有限离散概率测度 \(Q\) 取 supremum。
  • 每个尺度熵有限有助于 GC；平方根熵可积有助于 Donsker。

用途

多数经验过程证明最终都归结为在正确尺度上控制这些熵函数。

需要记住的准则

指南. 覆盖和 bracketing 相关但不同。Bracketing 更强，因为它逐点夹住函数；它常让可测性和 LLN 更容易，而覆盖熵在对称化过程里更灵活。

例子与证明思路

一个 bracket \([\ell,u]\) 包含所有满足 \(\ell\le f\le u\) 的函数。其统计宽度是 \(\|u-\ell\|_{P,2}\)，不是逐点宽度；所以它可以在小概率集合上很宽而仍然有用。
