24. Z-estimators

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/functional-delta-method/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/m-estimators-consistency/" %}

Z-estimators solve empirical estimating equations. Empirical processes control the uniform remainder in the linearization.

Definition. A Z-estimator satisfies \(P_n\psi_{\hat\theta}=o_p(n^{-1/2})\). If \(P\psi_{\theta_0}=0\) and \(P\psi_\theta\) is differentiable with derivative \(A\), then linearization targets \(A(\hat\theta-\theta_0)\).

Core facts

  • The stochastic term is \(\mathbb G_n\psi_{\theta_0}\).
  • Equicontinuity justifies replacing \(\mathbb G_n\psi_{\hat\theta}\) by \(\mathbb G_n\psi_{\theta_0}\).
  • The final expansion is usually \(\sqrt n(\hat\theta-\theta_0)=-A^{-1}\mathbb G_n\psi_{\theta_0}+o_p(1)\).

Use

This page is the empirical-process layer beneath score equations, GMM, estimating equations, and semiparametric influence-function expansions.

Les Z-estimateurs résolvent des équations d'estimation empiriques. Les processus empiriques contrôlent le reste uniforme de la linéarisation.

Définition. Un Z-estimateur vérifie \(P_n\psi_{\hat\theta}=o_p(n^{-1/2})\). Si \(P\psi_{\theta_0}=0\) et si \(P\psi_\theta\) est différentiable de dérivée \(A\), la linéarisation vise \(A(\hat\theta-\theta_0)\).

Faits clés

  • Le terme stochastique est \(\mathbb G_n\psi_{\theta_0}\).
  • L'équicontinuité justifie de remplacer \(\mathbb G_n\psi_{\hat\theta}\) par \(\mathbb G_n\psi_{\theta_0}\).
  • L'expansion finale est souvent \(\sqrt n(\hat\theta-\theta_0)=-A^{-1}\mathbb G_n\psi_{\theta_0}+o_p(1)\).

Usage

24. Z-估计量

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/functional-delta-method/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/m-estimators-consistency/" %}

Z-估计量通过求解经验估计方程定义。经验过程控制线性化中的一致余项。

定义. Z-估计量满足 \(P_n\psi_{\hat\theta}=o_p(n^{-1/2})\)。若 \(P\psi_{\theta_0}=0\)，且 \(P\psi_\theta\) 可微、导数为 \(A\)，线性化目标就是 \(A(\hat\theta-\theta_0)\)。

核心事实

  • 随机项是 \(\mathbb G_n\psi_{\theta_0}\)。
  • 渐近等连续性允许把 \(\mathbb G_n\psi_{\hat\theta}\) 替换为 \(\mathbb G_n\psi_{\theta_0}\)。
  • 最终展开通常为 \(\sqrt n(\hat\theta-\theta_0)=-A^{-1}\mathbb G_n\psi_{\theta_0}+o_p(1)\)。

用途

这一页是 score 方程、GMM、估计方程和半参数影响函数展开背后的经验过程层。
