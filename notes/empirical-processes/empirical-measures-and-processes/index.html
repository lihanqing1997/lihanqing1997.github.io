5. Empirical measures and processes

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/bounded-function-spaces/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/empirical-distribution-functions/" %}

The empirical measure turns data into a random probability measure. Empirical processes are its centered and rescaled fluctuations.

Definition. For measurable \(f\), \(P_nf=\int f\,\mathrm{d}P_n=n^{-1}\sum_{i=1}^nf(X_i)\), \(Pf=\int f\,\mathrm{d}P\), and \((P_n-P)f=P_nf-Pf\).

Core facts

  • For fixed \(f\), the LLN and CLT control \(P_nf\); empirical-process theory asks for control uniformly over \(f\in\mathcal F\).
  • The centered object \(\mathbb G_n f=\sqrt n(P_n-P)f\) separates random fluctuation from deterministic approximation.
  • Indicator classes recover empirical distribution functions; smooth or loss-function classes lead to estimators.

Use

This notation is used everywhere: uniform consistency is about \(P_n-P\), while weak limits and stochastic expansions are about \(\mathbb G_n\).

5. Mesures et processus empiriques

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/bounded-function-spaces/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/empirical-distribution-functions/" %}

La mesure empirique transforme les données en une mesure de probabilité aléatoire. Le processus empirique est sa fluctuation centrée et normalisée.

Définition. Pour \(f\) mesurable, \(P_nf=\int f\,\mathrm{d}P_n=n^{-1}\sum_{i=1}^nf(X_i)\), \(Pf=\int f\,\mathrm{d}P\), et \((P_n-P)f=P_nf-Pf\).

Faits clés

  • Pour un \(f\) fixé, LLN et TCL contrôlent \(P_nf\); la théorie empirique demande un contrôle uniforme sur \(\mathcal F\).
  • L'objet centré \(\mathbb G_n f=\sqrt n(P_n-P)f\) sépare fluctuation aléatoire et approximation déterministe.
  • Les classes indicatrices donnent les fonctions de répartition empiriques; les classes de pertes donnent des estimateurs.

Usage

Cette notation est utilisée partout : la consistance uniforme concerne \(P_n-P\), tandis que les limites faibles et expansions stochastiques concernent \(\mathbb G_n\).

5. 经验测度与经验过程

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/bounded-function-spaces/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/empirical-distribution-functions/" %}

经验测度把数据转化为随机概率测度。经验过程则是它中心化并缩放后的波动。

定义. 对可测 \(f\)，\(P_nf=\int f\,\mathrm{d}P_n=n^{-1}\sum_{i=1}^nf(X_i)\)，\(Pf=\int f\,\mathrm{d}P\)，且 \((P_n-P)f=P_nf-Pf\)。

核心事实

  • 固定 \(f\) 时，LLN 与 CLT 控制 \(P_nf\)；经验过程理论要求对整个 \(\mathcal F\) 一致控制。
  • 中心化对象 \(\mathbb G_n f=\sqrt n(P_n-P)f\) 把随机波动与确定性逼近分开。
  • 指标函数类给出经验分布函数；光滑函数类或损失函数类通向估计量。

用途

这个记号贯穿全篇：一致性研究 \(P_n-P\)，弱极限和随机展开研究 \(\mathbb G_n\)。
