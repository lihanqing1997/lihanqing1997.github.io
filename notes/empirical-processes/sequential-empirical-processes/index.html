20. Sequential empirical processes

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/bootstrap-empirical-processes/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/two-sample-and-permutation-processes/" %}

Sequential empirical processes add a time index to the function index. They describe partial-sample behavior and are central for change-point and monitoring problems.

Definition. \(\alpha_n(t,f)=n^{-1/2}\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor nt\rfloor}\{f(X_i)-Pf\}\), \(0\le t\le1\), \(f\in\mathcal F\).

Time-function indexed limits

  • For fixed \(f\), the limit is Brownian motion in the time coordinate with variance \(P(f-Pf)^2\).
  • For classes \(\mathcal F\), the limit is a Gaussian process indexed by both \(t\) and \(f\).
  • Tightness combines time regularity with empirical-process equicontinuity in \(f\).

CUSUM statistics

Sequential processes are the empirical-process backbone of cumulative-sum tests, change-point alternatives, and online diagnostics.

Sequential covariance

Theorem. Under Donsker conditions, the limit of \(\alpha_n(t,f)\) is a Gaussian process with covariance \[ \operatorname{Cov}\{\alpha(s,f),\alpha(t,g)\}=(s\wedge t)\{Pfg-PfPg\}. \] Centering by the full sample produces Brownian-bridge-type versions useful for change-point tests.

Two-index tightness

CUSUM statistics take suprema over time, and sometimes over \(f\) as well. The empirical-process task is therefore two-dimensional: control oscillation in \(t\) and in the function index simultaneously.

20. Processus empiriques séquentiels

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/bootstrap-empirical-processes/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/two-sample-and-permutation-processes/" %}

Les processus empiriques séquentiels ajoutent un indice de temps à l'indice fonctionnel. Ils décrivent les sous-échantillons et les problèmes de rupture.

Définition. \(\alpha_n(t,f)=n^{-1/2}\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor nt\rfloor}\{f(X_i)-Pf\}\), \(0\le t\le1\), \(f\in\mathcal F\).

Limites indexées par temps et fonction

  • Pour un \(f\) fixé, la limite est un mouvement brownien dans le temps avec variance \(P(f-Pf)^2\).
  • Pour une classe \(\mathcal F\), la limite est gaussienne en \((t,f)\).
  • La tension combine régularité en temps et équicontinuité empirique en \(f\).

Statistiques CUSUM

Ils forment le support empirique des tests CUSUM, alternatives de rupture et diagnostics séquentiels.

Covariance séquentielle

Théorème. Sous des conditions de Donsker, la limite de \(\alpha_n(t,f)\) a covariance \[ \operatorname{Cov}\{\alpha(s,f),\alpha(t,g)\}=(s\wedge t)\{Pfg-PfPg\}. \] Le centrage par l'échantillon complet donne des versions de type pont brownien.

Tension à deux indices

Les statistiques CUSUM prennent des suprema en temps, parfois aussi en \(f\). Il faut donc contrôler les oscillations dans les deux indices.

20. 序贯经验过程

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/bootstrap-empirical-processes/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/two-sample-and-permutation-processes/" %}

序贯经验过程在函数索引外再加入时间索引，用于描述部分样本行为以及 change-point 和监控问题。

定义. \(\alpha_n(t,f)=n^{-1/2}\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor nt\rfloor}\{f(X_i)-Pf\}\)，其中 \(0\le t\le1\)、\(f\in\mathcal F\)。

时间-函数索引极限

  • 固定 \(f\) 时，时间坐标上的极限是方差为 \(P(f-Pf)^2\) 的 Brownian motion。
  • 对函数类 \(\mathcal F\)，极限是由 \((t,f)\) 同时索引的 Gaussian 过程。
  • 紧性需要结合时间方向的正则性和函数方向的经验过程等连续性。

CUSUM 统计量

序贯过程是 CUSUM 检验、change-point 备择和在线诊断的经验过程基础。

序贯协方差

定理. 在 Donsker 条件下，\(\alpha_n(t,f)\) 的极限 Gaussian 过程协方差为 \[ \operatorname{Cov}\{\alpha(s,f),\alpha(t,g)\}=(s\wedge t)\{Pfg-PfPg\}. \] 若用全样本中心化，则得到 Brownian-bridge 型过程，适合 change-point 检验。

双索引紧性

CUSUM 统计量对时间取 supremum，有时还对 \(f\) 取 supremum。因此经验过程任务是二维的：同时控制时间索引和函数索引上的振荡。
