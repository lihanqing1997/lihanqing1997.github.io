Stochastic Equicontinuity and Applications

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/entropy-and-maximal-inequalities/" %}

Definition

A process \(\mathbb G_n f_\theta\) is stochastically equicontinuous at \(\theta_0\) if, for every \(\varepsilon,\eta>0\), there exists \(\delta>0\) such that

\[ \limsup_nP\left( \sup_{\|\theta-\theta_0\|<\delta} |\mathbb G_n(f_\theta-f_{\theta_0})|>\varepsilon \right)<\eta. \]

It says that small parameter changes produce small empirical-process changes.

Local replacements

Proposition. If \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\) in probability and \(\mathbb G_nf_\theta\) is stochastically equicontinuous at \(\theta_0\), then

\[ \mathbb G_n f_{\hat\theta_n}-\mathbb G_n f_{\theta_0}=o_p(1). \]

Proof. Given \(\varepsilon,\eta>0\), choose \(\delta\) from stochastic equicontinuity. Since \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\), the event \(\|\hat\theta_n-\theta_0\|<\delta\) has probability tending to one. On this event the desired difference is bounded by the local supremum, whose probability of exceeding \(\varepsilon\) is eventually below \(\eta\).

If \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\), stochastic equicontinuity often gives

\[ \mathbb G_n f_{\hat\theta_n} = \mathbb G_n f_{\theta_0}+o_p(1). \]

This is the key step allowing random estimated functions to be replaced by fixed functions in asymptotic expansions.

M-estimators

For \(P_nm_\theta\), write

\[ P_nm_{\hat\theta}-P_nm_{\theta_0} = (P_n-P)(m_{\hat\theta}-m_{\theta_0}) +P(m_{\hat\theta}-m_{\theta_0}). \]

The deterministic term supplies curvature; the stochastic term must be controlled uniformly. This is how empirical process theory proves rates and asymptotic normality beyond smooth likelihood models.

Bootstrap processes

Multiplier bootstrap replaces \(\mathbb G_n f\) by

\[ \mathbb G_n^\xi f=\frac1{\sqrt n}\sum_{i=1}^n\xi_i\{f(X_i)-P_nf\}, \]

where \(\xi_i\) are independent weights with mean zero and variance one. Under suitable Donsker or high-dimensional conditions, this conditional process mimics the distribution of \(\mathbb G_n\).

Where it appears

Stochastic equicontinuity appears in semiparametric efficiency, quantile regression, empirical likelihood, bootstrap validity, nonparametric function estimation, and modern uniform inference. Its role is always the same: control random fluctuations uniformly over a local class.

Équicontinuité Stochastique et Applications

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/entropy-and-maximal-inequalities/" %}

Définition

Le processus \(\mathbb G_n f_\theta\) est stochastiquement équicontinu en \(\theta_0\) si, pour tout \(\varepsilon,\eta>0\), il existe \(\delta>0\) tel que

\[ \limsup_nP\left( \sup_{\|\theta-\theta_0\|<\delta} |\mathbb G_n(f_\theta-f_{\theta_0})|>\varepsilon \right)<\eta. \]

De petites variations du paramètre produisent de petites variations du processus.

Remplacements locaux

Proposition. Si \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\) en probabilité et si \(\mathbb G_nf_\theta\) est stochastiquement équicontinu en \(\theta_0\), alors

\[ \mathbb G_n f_{\hat\theta_n}-\mathbb G_n f_{\theta_0}=o_p(1). \]

Preuve. Choisir \(\delta\) par équicontinuité. L'événement \(\|\hat\theta_n-\theta_0\|<\delta\) a probabilité tendant vers un; sur cet événement, la différence est bornée par le supremum local.

Si \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\), on obtient souvent

\[ \mathbb G_n f_{\hat\theta_n} = \mathbb G_n f_{\theta_0}+o_p(1). \]

M-estimateurs

Pour \(P_nm_\theta\),

\[ P_nm_{\hat\theta}-P_nm_{\theta_0} = (P_n-P)(m_{\hat\theta}-m_{\theta_0}) +P(m_{\hat\theta}-m_{\theta_0}). \]

Le terme déterministe donne la courbure, et le terme stochastique doit être contrôlé uniformément.

Bootstrap multiplicateur

Le bootstrap multiplicateur remplace \(\mathbb G_n f\) par

\[ \mathbb G_n^\xi f=\frac1{\sqrt n}\sum_{i=1}^n\xi_i\{f(X_i)-P_nf\}, \]

où les \(\xi_i\) sont indépendants, centrés et de variance un.

Applications

Cette notion apparaît en efficacité semiparamétrique, régression quantile, vraisemblance empirique, bootstrap, estimation non paramétrique et inférence uniforme.

随机等连续性与应用

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/entropy-and-maximal-inequalities/" %}

定义

若对任意 \(\varepsilon,\eta>0\)，存在 \(\delta>0\)，使得

\[ \limsup_nP\left( \sup_{\|\theta-\theta_0\|<\delta} |\mathbb G_n(f_\theta-f_{\theta_0})|>\varepsilon \right)<\eta, \]

则称过程 \(\mathbb G_n f_\theta\) 在 \(\theta_0\) 处随机等连续。它表示参数的小变化只会造成经验过程的小变化。

局部替换

命题. 若 \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\) 依概率成立，并且 \(\mathbb G_nf_\theta\) 在 \(\theta_0\) 处随机等连续，则

\[ \mathbb G_n f_{\hat\theta_n}-\mathbb G_n f_{\theta_0}=o_p(1). \]

证明. 给定 \(\varepsilon,\eta>0\)，由随机等连续性选取 \(\delta\)。由于 \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\)，事件 \(\|\hat\theta_n-\theta_0\|<\delta\) 的概率趋于一。在该事件上，目标差值由局部 supremum 控制，而该 supremum 超过 \(\varepsilon\) 的概率最终小于 \(\eta\)。

若 \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\)，随机等连续性常给出

\[ \mathbb G_n f_{\hat\theta_n} = \mathbb G_n f_{\theta_0}+o_p(1). \]

这允许我们在渐近展开中把随机估计函数替换为固定函数。

M-估计中的应用

对 \(P_nm_\theta\)，有分解

\[ P_nm_{\hat\theta}-P_nm_{\theta_0} = (P_n-P)(m_{\hat\theta}-m_{\theta_0}) +P(m_{\hat\theta}-m_{\theta_0}). \]

确定性项给出曲率，随机项需要一致控制。经验过程理论正是通过这一点证明更一般估计量的速度和渐近正态性。

Multiplier bootstrap

Multiplier bootstrap 用

\[ \mathbb G_n^\xi f=\frac1{\sqrt n}\sum_{i=1}^n\xi_i\{f(X_i)-P_nf\} \]

替代 \(\mathbb G_n f\)，其中 \(\xi_i\) 独立、均值为零、方差为一。在适当条件下，该条件过程可以模拟 \(\mathbb G_n\) 的分布。

出现在哪里

随机等连续性出现在半参数效率、分位数回归、经验似然、bootstrap 有效性、非参数函数估计和现代统一推断中。它的核心作用始终是：在局部函数类上一致控制随机波动。
