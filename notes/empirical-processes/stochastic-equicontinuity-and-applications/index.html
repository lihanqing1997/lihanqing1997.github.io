9. Asymptotic equicontinuity

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/donsker-and-gaussian-limits/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/covering-and-bracketing-numbers/" %}

Asymptotic equicontinuity is the tightness mechanism for indexed processes. It says that close functions have close empirical fluctuations with high probability.

Definition. With semimetric \(\rho\), \(\mathbb G_n\) is asymptotically equicontinuous if \(\lim_{\delta\downarrow0}\limsup_nP^*(\sup_{\rho(f,g)<\delta}|\mathbb G_n f-\mathbb G_n g|>\varepsilon)=0\) for every \(\varepsilon>0\).

Core facts

  • Finite-dimensional convergence plus asymptotic equicontinuity gives weak convergence in many empirical-process settings.
  • The natural semimetric is often \(\rho_P(f,g)=\{P(f-g)^2\}^{1/2}\).
  • Maximal inequalities and chaining are the standard ways to prove equicontinuity.

Use

In estimator proofs, equicontinuity turns \(\mathbb G_n f_{\hat\theta}\) into \(\mathbb G_n f_{\theta_0}+o_p(1)\) whenever \(\hat\theta\) is close to \(\theta_0\).

9. Équicontinuité asymptotique

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/donsker-and-gaussian-limits/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/covering-and-bracketing-numbers/" %}

L'équicontinuité asymptotique est le mécanisme de tension pour les processus indexés. Elle affirme que des fonctions proches ont des fluctuations empiriques proches.

Définition. Pour une semi-métrique \(\rho\), \(\mathbb G_n\) est asymptotiquement équicontinu si \(\lim_{\delta\downarrow0}\limsup_nP^*(\sup_{\rho(f,g)<\delta}|\mathbb G_n f-\mathbb G_n g|>\varepsilon)=0\).

Faits clés

  • Convergence fini-dimensionnelle et équicontinuité asymptotique donnent souvent la convergence faible du processus entier.
  • La semi-métrique naturelle est souvent \(\rho_P(f,g)=\{P(f-g)^2\}^{1/2}\).
  • Les inégalités maximales et le chaînage sont les outils standard pour la prouver.

Usage

Dans les preuves d'estimateurs, elle permet de remplacer \(\mathbb G_n f_{\hat\theta}\) par \(\mathbb G_n f_{\theta_0}+o_p(1)\) lorsque \(\hat\theta\) est proche de \(\theta_0\).

9. 渐近等连续性

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/donsker-and-gaussian-limits/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/covering-and-bracketing-numbers/" %}

渐近等连续性是索引过程中的紧性机制。它说明相近函数的经验波动也以高概率相近。

定义. 给定半度量 \(\rho\)，若对任意 \(\varepsilon>0\)，\(\lim_{\delta\downarrow0}\limsup_nP^*(\sup_{\rho(f,g)<\delta}|\mathbb G_n f-\mathbb G_n g|>\varepsilon)=0\)，则称 \(\mathbb G_n\) 渐近等连续。

核心事实

  • 有限维收敛加渐近等连续性通常推出整个经验过程的弱收敛。
  • 自然半度量常是 \(\rho_P(f,g)=\{P(f-g)^2\}^{1/2}\)。
  • 最大不等式和 chaining 是证明渐近等连续性的标准工具。

用途

在估计量证明中，只要 \(\hat\theta\) 接近 \(\theta_0\)，渐近等连续性就允许把 \(\mathbb G_n f_{\hat\theta}\) 换成 \(\mathbb G_n f_{\theta_0}+o_p(1)\)。
