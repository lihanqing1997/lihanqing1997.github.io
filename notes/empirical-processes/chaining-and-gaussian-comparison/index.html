14. Chaining and Gaussian comparison

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/entropy-of-smooth-classes/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/entropy-and-maximal-inequalities/" %}

Chaining bounds a supremum by approximating the index set at successively finer scales. It is the conceptual engine behind entropy integrals.

Definition. For a sub-Gaussian process \(Z_t\), a Dudley-type bound has the form \(E\sup_{s,t\in T}|Z_s-Z_t|\lesssim \int_0^{\operatorname{diam}(T)}\sqrt{\log N(\varepsilon,T,d)}\,\mathrm{d}\varepsilon\).

Core facts

  • The entropy integral sums oscillations from coarse to fine approximations.
  • Generic chaining sharpens Dudley bounds by optimizing admissible sequences rather than fixed nets.
  • Gaussian comparison transfers bounds between processes with ordered covariance metrics.

Use

Chaining explains why entropy appears under a square root in CLT and maximal-inequality conditions.

14. Chaînage et comparaison gaussienne

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/entropy-of-smooth-classes/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/entropy-and-maximal-inequalities/" %}

Le chaînage borne un supremum en approximant l'ensemble d'indices à des échelles de plus en plus fines.

Définition. Pour un processus sous-gaussien \(Z_t\), une borne de Dudley s'écrit \(E\sup_{s,t\in T}|Z_s-Z_t|\lesssim \int_0^{\operatorname{diam}(T)}\sqrt{\log N(\varepsilon,T,d)}\,\mathrm{d}\varepsilon\).

Faits clés

  • L'intégrale d'entropie additionne les oscillations des approximations grossières aux fines.
  • Le chaînage générique affine Dudley en optimisant les suites admissibles.
  • La comparaison gaussienne transfère des bornes entre processus dont les métriques de covariance sont ordonnées.

Usage

Le chaînage explique pourquoi l'entropie apparaît sous une racine carrée dans les conditions de TCL et d'inégalité maximale.

14. Chaining 与 Gaussian 比较

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/empirical-processes/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/empirical-processes/entropy-of-smooth-classes/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/empirical-processes/entropy-and-maximal-inequalities/" %}

Chaining 通过在逐渐变细的尺度上逼近索引集来控制 supremum，是熵积分背后的核心思想。

定义. 对 sub-Gaussian 过程 \(Z_t\)，Dudley 型界为 \(E\sup_{s,t\in T}|Z_s-Z_t|\lesssim \int_0^{\operatorname{diam}(T)}\sqrt{\log N(\varepsilon,T,d)}\,\mathrm{d}\varepsilon\)。

核心事实

  • 熵积分把从粗到细各尺度的振荡累加起来。
  • Generic chaining 通过优化 admissible sequences 来细化 Dudley 界。
  • Gaussian 比较把界从一个协方差度量有序的过程转移到另一个过程。

用途

Chaining 解释了为什么 CLT 和最大不等式条件中熵常以平方根形式出现。
