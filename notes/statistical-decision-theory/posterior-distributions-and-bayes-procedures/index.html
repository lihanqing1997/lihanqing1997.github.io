23. Posterior distributions and Bayes procedures

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/tests-and-confidence-sets-gaussian/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/sequential-experiments/" %}

Le Cam treats posterior distributions through joint laws and decision risks. Bernstein-von Mises becomes an approximation of posterior experiments by Gaussian shifts.

Posterior risk minimization

Definition. Given posterior \(\Pi(\mathrm{d}\theta\mid X)\), a Bayes action minimizes \(\int L(\theta,a)\,\Pi(\mathrm{d}\theta\mid X)\). The estimator depends on the loss, not only on the posterior distribution.

Bernstein-von Mises approximation

Theorem. In regular finite-dimensional models with suitable priors, the posterior of \(\sqrt n(\theta-\hat\theta_n)\) is asymptotically \(N(0,I_{\theta_0}^{-1})\) in probability under \(P_{\theta_0}\).

LAN and prior stability

Proof. The LAN expansion makes the likelihood locally Gaussian, while prior mass and continuity replace the prior by its value at \(\theta_0\). Normalizing the local likelihood gives the Gaussian posterior limit.

Decision-theoretic consequence

Posterior means, medians, and credible ellipsoids inherit frequentist first-order behavior only through this local experiment approximation.

23. Distributions postérieures et procédures bayésiennes

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/tests-and-confidence-sets-gaussian/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/sequential-experiments/" %}

Le Cam traite les distributions postérieures par les lois jointes et les risques de décision.

Minimisation du risque postérieur

Définition. Une action bayésienne minimise \(\int L(\theta,a)\,\Pi(\mathrm{d}\theta\mid X)\). Elle dépend de la perte choisie.

Approximation de Bernstein-von Mises

Théorème. Dans les modèles réguliers, la loi postérieure de \(\sqrt n(\theta-\hat\theta_n)\) est asymptotiquement \(N(0,I_{\theta_0}^{-1})\).

LAN et stabilité de l'a priori

Preuve. LAN rend la vraisemblance localement gaussienne, et la continuité de l'a priori le remplace localement par une constante.

Conséquence décisionnelle

Les procédures bayésiennes héritent leur comportement fréquentiste de cette approximation locale de l'expérience.

23. 后验分布与贝叶斯程序

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/tests-and-confidence-sets-gaussian/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/sequential-experiments/" %}

勒卡姆通过联合分布与决策风险处理后验分布。伯恩斯坦-冯·米塞斯可看作后验实验被高斯 shift 近似。

后验风险最小化

定义. 给定后验 \(\Pi(\mathrm{d}\theta\mid X)\)，贝叶斯行动最小化 \(\int L(\theta,a)\,\Pi(\mathrm{d}\theta\mid X)\)。估计量取决于损失函数。

伯恩斯坦-冯·米塞斯近似

定理. 在正则有限维模型和适当先验下，\(\sqrt n(\theta-\hat\theta_n)\) 的后验分布在 \(P_{\theta_0}\) 下渐近为 \(N(0,I_{\theta_0}^{-1})\)。

LAN 与先验稳定性

证明. LAN 使似然局部高斯；先验在 \(\theta_0\) 附近有正质量并连续时，可局部由常数替代。归一化局部似然得到高斯后验极限。

决策论后果

后验均值、中位数与可信椭球的频率一阶性质，都来自这个局部实验近似。
