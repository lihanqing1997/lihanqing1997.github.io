14. Local Asymptotic Normality

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/gaussian-shift-experiments/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/local-asymptotic-minimax-theory/" %}

LAN is the bridge from a smooth statistical model to a Gaussian shift experiment. It is a statement about the likelihood-ratio process, not only about one estimator.

LAN expansion

Definition. A model is LAN at \(\theta_0\) with information \(I_{\theta_0}\) if, for fixed \(h\),

\[ \log\frac{\mathrm{d}P_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}^{(n)}}{\mathrm{d}P_{\theta_0}^{(n)}} =h^\top\Delta_n-\frac12h^\top I_{\theta_0}h+o_{P_{\theta_0}}(1), \quad \Delta_n\Rightarrow N(0,I_{\theta_0}). \]

Convergence to Gaussian shift

Theorem. The LAN expansion implies weak convergence of the local experiments \(\{P_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}^{(n)}:h\in K\}\) to the Gaussian shift experiment with information \(I_{\theta_0}\), for each finite \(K\).

Likelihood-ratio vector proof

Proof. For \(h_1,\ldots,h_k\), the LAN expansion expresses all likelihood ratios as continuous functions of \(\Delta_n\) plus deterministic quadratic terms. The multivariate CLT gives the joint limit.

Information as local curvature

The matrix \(I_{\theta_0}\) is simultaneously the covariance of the central sequence and the curvature of the log likelihood in local coordinates.

14. Normalité asymptotique locale

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/gaussian-shift-experiments/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/local-asymptotic-minimax-theory/" %}

LAN relie un modèle régulier à une expérience de décalage gaussienne.

Expansion LAN

Définition. Le modèle est LAN en \(\theta_0\) si

\[ \log\frac{\mathrm{d}P_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}^{(n)}}{\mathrm{d}P_{\theta_0}^{(n)}} =h^\top\Delta_n-\frac12h^\top I_{\theta_0}h+o_{P_{\theta_0}}(1), \quad \Delta_n\Rightarrow N(0,I_{\theta_0}). \]

Convergence vers le décalage gaussien

Théorème. L'expansion LAN implique la convergence faible des expériences locales vers le décalage gaussien d'information \(I_{\theta_0}\).

Preuve par vecteurs de vraisemblance

Preuve. Les rapports pour \(h_1,\ldots,h_k\) sont des fonctions continues de \(\Delta_n\) plus des termes quadratiques déterministes.

Information comme courbure locale

La matrice \(I_{\theta_0}\) est à la fois covariance de la suite centrale et courbure locale de la vraisemblance.

14. 局部渐近正态性

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/gaussian-shift-experiments/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/local-asymptotic-minimax-theory/" %}

LAN 把光滑统计模型连接到 Gaussian shift 实验。它是关于似然比过程的陈述，而不只是关于某个估计量。

LAN 展开

定义. 模型在 \(\theta_0\) 处 LAN，若对固定 \(h\)，

\[ \log\frac{\mathrm{d}P_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}^{(n)}}{\mathrm{d}P_{\theta_0}^{(n)}} =h^\top\Delta_n-\frac12h^\top I_{\theta_0}h+o_{P_{\theta_0}}(1), \quad \Delta_n\Rightarrow N(0,I_{\theta_0}). \]

收敛到 Gaussian shift

定理. LAN 展开推出局部实验 \(\{P_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}^{(n)}\}\) 在有限坐标上弱收敛到信息矩阵为 \(I_{\theta_0}\) 的 Gaussian shift 实验。

似然比向量证明

证明. 对 \(h_1,\ldots,h_k\)，LAN 把所有似然比写成 \(\Delta_n\) 的连续函数加确定性二次项。多元 CLT 给出联合极限。

信息作为局部曲率

矩阵 \(I_{\theta_0}\) 同时是中心序列的协方差，也是局部坐标中 log-likelihood 的曲率。
