28. I.I.D. Experiments and Quadratic Mean Differentiability

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/empirical-measures-and-vc-classes/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/consistent-and-root-n-estimation/" %}

For i.i.d. models, differentiability in quadratic mean is the clean condition that turns single-observation Hellinger geometry into LAN.

Quadratic mean differentiability

Definition. A dominated model \(p_\theta\) is differentiable in quadratic mean at \(\theta_0\) if there is score \(\dot\ell_{\theta_0}\) such that

\[ \int\left(\sqrt{p_{\theta_0+h}}-\sqrt{p_{\theta_0}} -\frac12h^\top\dot\ell_{\theta_0}\sqrt{p_{\theta_0}}\right)^2\mathrm{d}\mu=o(\|h\|^2). \]

DQM implies LAN

Theorem. For i.i.d. observations, DQM and nonsingular Fisher information imply LAN with central sequence \(\Delta_n=n^{-1/2}\sum_i\dot\ell_{\theta_0}(X_i)\).

Square-root density expansion

Proof. The DQM expansion controls Hellinger error for local shifts \(h/\sqrt n\). Summing independent score terms gives the central sequence CLT and the quadratic likelihood term.

Tangent Hilbert space

The closure of score directions in \(L_2^0(P_{\theta_0})\) is the local tangent space. Fisher information is the squared norm of tangent vectors.

28. Expériences i.i.d. et différentiabilité en moyenne quadratique

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/empirical-measures-and-vc-classes/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/consistent-and-root-n-estimation/" %}

Dans les modèles i.i.d., la différentiabilité en moyenne quadratique transforme la géométrie de Hellinger en LAN.

Différentiabilité en moyenne quadratique

Définition. La DQM donne une expansion \(L_2\) des racines de densités autour de \(\theta_0\).

DQM implique LAN

Théorème. Pour des observations i.i.d., DQM et information non singulière impliquent LAN avec \(\Delta_n=n^{-1/2}\sum_i\dot\ell_{\theta_0}(X_i)\).

Expansion des racines de densités

Preuve. L'erreur de Hellinger est contrôlée pour les déplacements \(h/\sqrt n\), puis le TCL des scores donne la limite.

Espace tangent hilbertien

L'adhérence des directions de score dans \(L_2^0(P_{\theta_0})\) est l'espace tangent local.

28. I.I.D. 实验与二次均值可微性

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/empirical-measures-and-vc-classes/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/consistent-and-root-n-estimation/" %}

对 i.i.d. 模型，二次均值可微性是把单个观测的 Hellinger 几何转化为 LAN 的干净条件。

二次均值可微性

定义. 若根密度在 \(L_2\) 中有一阶展开，则称模型在 \(\theta_0\) 处二次均值可微。其精确形式给出 score \(\dot\ell_{\theta_0}\)。

DQM 推出 LAN

定理. 对 i.i.d. 观测，DQM 与非奇异 Fisher 信息推出 LAN，中心序列为 \(\Delta_n=n^{-1/2}\sum_i\dot\ell_{\theta_0}(X_i)\)。

根密度展开证明

证明. DQM 控制局部位移 \(h/\sqrt n\) 的 Hellinger 误差；独立 score 项求和后，由 CLT 得到中心序列与二次似然项。

Hilbert 切空间

score 方向在 \(L_2^0(P_{\theta_0})\) 中的闭包是局部切空间，Fisher 信息是切向量范数平方。
