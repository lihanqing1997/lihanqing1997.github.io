12. Le Cam's Lemmas

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/weak-convergence-of-experiments/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/gaussian-shift-experiments/" %}

Le Cam's lemmas organize changes of measure in local asymptotics. They tell how likelihood-ratio limits transform distributions under nearby alternatives.

First lemma and contiguity

Lemma. If \(L_n=\mathrm{d}Q_n/\mathrm{d}P_n\) converges in distribution under \(P_n\) to \(L\) with \(E L=1\), then \(Q_n\triangleleft P_n\). If also \(L>0\) almost surely in the right sense, mutual contiguity follows.

Third lemma

Theorem. If \((T_n,L_n)\Rightarrow (T,L)\) under \(P_n\), then under \(Q_n\) the limit law of \(T_n\) is tilted by \(L\):

\[ E_Q\varphi(T)=E_P\{L\varphi(T)\}. \]

Proof by bounded test functions

Proof. For bounded \(\varphi\), \(E_{Q_n}\varphi(T_n)=E_{P_n}L_n\varphi(T_n)\). Joint convergence and uniform integrability of the likelihood-ratio coordinate pass this identity to the limit.

Local power calculations

In LAN, the third lemma shifts the normal limit of a statistic under \(P_{\theta_0}\) to its limit under \(P_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}\).

12. Lemmes de Le Cam

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/weak-convergence-of-experiments/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/gaussian-shift-experiments/" %}

Les lemmes de Le Cam organisent les changements de mesure en asymptotique locale.

Premier lemme et contiguïté

Lemme. Si \(L_n=\mathrm{d}Q_n/\mathrm{d}P_n\) converge sous \(P_n\) vers \(L\) avec \(E L=1\), alors \(Q_n\triangleleft P_n\).

Troisième lemme

Théorème. Si \((T_n,L_n)\Rightarrow (T,L)\) sous \(P_n\), alors sous \(Q_n\) la loi limite est inclinée par \(L\):

\[ E_Q\varphi(T)=E_P\{L\varphi(T)\}. \]

Preuve par fonctions tests bornées

Preuve. On écrit \(E_{Q_n}\varphi(T_n)=E_{P_n}L_n\varphi(T_n)\), puis on passe à la limite avec la convergence jointe.

Calculs de puissance locale

Dans LAN, le troisième lemme décale la limite normale d'une statistique sous les alternatives locales.

12. Le Cam 引理

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/weak-convergence-of-experiments/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/gaussian-shift-experiments/" %}

Le Cam 引理组织局部渐近中的变测度问题，说明似然比极限如何改变近邻备择下的分布。

第一引理与 contiguity

引理. 若 \(L_n=\mathrm{d}Q_n/\mathrm{d}P_n\) 在 \(P_n\) 下收敛到 \(L\)，且 \(E L=1\)，则 \(Q_n\triangleleft P_n\)。

第三引理

定理. 若 \((T_n,L_n)\Rightarrow (T,L)\) 在 \(P_n\) 下成立，则在 \(Q_n\) 下 \(T_n\) 的极限分布由 \(L\) 倾斜：

\[ E_Q\varphi(T)=E_P\{L\varphi(T)\}. \]

由有界测试函数证明

证明. 对有界 \(\varphi\)，有 \(E_{Q_n}\varphi(T_n)=E_{P_n}L_n\varphi(T_n)\)。联合收敛与似然比坐标的一致可积性把等式传到极限。

局部功效计算

在 LAN 中，第三引理把 \(P_{\theta_0}\) 下的正态极限转换为局部备择 \(P_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}\) 下的极限。
