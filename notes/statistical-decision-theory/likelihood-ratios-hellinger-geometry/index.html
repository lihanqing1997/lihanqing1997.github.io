9. Likelihood ratios and Hellinger geometry

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/deficiency-and-distance/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/contiguity/" %}

Likelihood ratios encode the relative position of measures. Hellinger distance gives a stable geometry for comparing product experiments and proving testing inequalities.

Likelihood ratio process

Definition. Fix \(P_{\theta_0}\). The likelihood ratio process is \(L_\theta=\mathrm{d}P_\theta/\mathrm{d}P_{\theta_0}\) on the part dominated by \(P_{\theta_0}\). Its finite-dimensional laws determine local experiment limits.

Hellinger distance

Definition. For dominated measures \(P,Q\),

\[ h^2(P,Q)=\frac12\int(\sqrt p-\sqrt q)^2\,\mathrm{d}\mu =1-\int\sqrt{pq}\,\mathrm{d}\mu. \]

Testing bound from Hellinger affinity

Lemma. The affinity \(\rho(P,Q)=\int\sqrt{\mathrm{d}P\,\mathrm{d}Q}\) controls testing error: if \(P\) and \(Q\) are close in Hellinger distance, no test can separate them with small errors under both measures.

Product geometry

For independent components, affinities multiply and Hellinger squared distances approximately add. This additivity is why Le Cam's product-experiment arguments use Hellinger sums.

9. Rapports de vraisemblance et géométrie de Hellinger

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/deficiency-and-distance/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/contiguity/" %}

Les rapports de vraisemblance encodent la position relative des mesures. La distance de Hellinger donne une géométrie stable.

Processus de rapports de vraisemblance

Définition. Fixons \(P_{\theta_0}\). Le processus \(L_\theta=\mathrm{d}P_\theta/\mathrm{d}P_{\theta_0}\) encode les changements de loi autour de \(\theta_0\).

Distance de Hellinger

Définition. Pour \(P,Q\) dominées,

\[ h^2(P,Q)=\frac12\int(\sqrt p-\sqrt q)^2\,\mathrm{d}\mu =1-\int\sqrt{pq}\,\mathrm{d}\mu. \]

Borne de test par affinité de Hellinger

Lemme. L'affinité \(\rho(P,Q)\) borne les erreurs de test : deux mesures proches en Hellinger ne peuvent pas être séparées uniformément bien.

Géométrie des produits

Pour des composantes indépendantes, les affinités se multiplient et les distances de Hellinger s'additionnent approximativement.

9. 似然比与 Hellinger 几何

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/deficiency-and-distance/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/contiguity/" %}

似然比编码测度之间的相对位置。Hellinger 距离给乘积实验与检验不等式提供稳定几何。

似然比过程

定义. 固定 \(P_{\theta_0}\)。似然比过程 \(L_\theta=\mathrm{d}P_\theta/\mathrm{d}P_{\theta_0}\) 描述 \(\theta_0\) 附近分布如何变化。

Hellinger 距离

定义. 对被控制测度 \(P,Q\)，

\[ h^2(P,Q)=\frac12\int(\sqrt p-\sqrt q)^2\,\mathrm{d}\mu =1-\int\sqrt{pq}\,\mathrm{d}\mu. \]

由 Hellinger 亲和度得到检验界

引理. 亲和度 \(\rho(P,Q)\) 控制检验错误：若两测度在 Hellinger 距离下很近，就不可能同时让两类错误都很小。

乘积几何

独立分量下，亲和度相乘，Hellinger 平方距离近似相加。这解释了乘积实验中 Hellinger 和的作用。
