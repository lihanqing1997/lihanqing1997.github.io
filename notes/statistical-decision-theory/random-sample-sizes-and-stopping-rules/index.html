32. Random sample sizes and stopping rules

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/maximum-likelihood-and-pathologies/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/synthesis-experiments-to-procedures/" %}

Random sample sizes change the experiment because the stopping rule is part of the observation. Under stable information accumulation, many LAN results survive.

Randomized observation horizon

Definition. Let \(N_n\) be a data-dependent or external sample size. The observed experiment contains both \(N_n\) and \(X_1,\ldots,X_{N_n}\).

Information-time normalization

Proposition. If \(N_n/n\to \tau>0\) in probability and the fixed-\(n\) experiment is LAN with information \(I\), then the random-size experiment is LAN with information \(\tau I\), under suitable independence or stopping assumptions.

Random time-change proof

Proof. The central sequence at time \(N_n\) is a stopped or randomly indexed partial sum. Martingale CLTs and random time-change arguments replace \(n\) by \(N_n\).

Sequential design consequences

Stopping rules can improve cost or ethical performance without changing first-order local risk, provided they do not destroy contiguity or information stability.

32. Tailles d'échantillon aléatoires et règles d'arrêt

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/maximum-likelihood-and-pathologies/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/synthesis-experiments-to-procedures/" %}

Une taille d'échantillon aléatoire change l'expérience, car la règle d'arrêt fait partie de l'observation.

Horizon d'observation aléatoire

Définition. L'expérience observe \(N_n\) et \(X_1,\ldots,X_{N_n}\); la taille d'échantillon fait partie des données.

Normalisation par temps d'information

Proposition. Si \(N_n/n\to\tau>0\), l'information locale devient \(\tau I\) sous conditions de stabilité.

Preuve par changement de temps

Preuve. La suite centrale est une somme partielle arrêtée; les TCL martingales remplacent \(n\) par \(N_n\).

Conséquences pour le séquentiel

Les règles d'arrêt peuvent modifier le coût sans changer le risque local de premier ordre si la contiguïté est préservée.

32. 随机样本量与停止规则

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/maximum-likelihood-and-pathologies/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/synthesis-experiments-to-procedures/" %}

随机样本量会改变实验，因为停止规则本身属于观测机制。在信息累积稳定时，许多 LAN 结果仍成立。

随机观测时域

定义. 设 \(N_n\) 是依赖数据或外部随机性的样本量。实验观测 \(N_n\) 以及 \(X_1,\ldots,X_{N_n}\)。

信息时间归一化

命题. 若 \(N_n/n\to\tau>0\)，且固定 \(n\) 实验 LAN 信息为 \(I\)，则在适当条件下随机样本量实验 LAN 信息为 \(\tau I\)。

随机时间变换证明

证明. 中心序列成为停止的或随机指标的部分和。鞅 CLT 与随机时间变换把 \(n\) 替换为 \(N_n\)。

序贯设计后果

若 stopping rule 不破坏 contiguity 与信息稳定性，它可改善成本或伦理表现而不改变一阶局部风险。
