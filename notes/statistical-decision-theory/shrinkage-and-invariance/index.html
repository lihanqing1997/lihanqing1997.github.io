17. Invariance, equivariance, and shrinkage

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/convolution-and-asymptotic-admissibility/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/poisson-and-infinitely-divisible-experiments/" %}

Invariance reduces decision problems by symmetry. Shrinkage shows that symmetry alone does not always imply admissibility, especially in multivariate normal means.

Invariant experiment and equivariant rule

Definition. A group \(G\) acts on parameters, observations, and actions. A rule \(\delta\) is equivariant if \(\delta(gx)=g\delta(x)\). Its risk satisfies \(R(g\theta,\delta)=R(\theta,\delta)\).

James-Stein phenomenon

Theorem. If \(X\sim N_p(\theta,\sigma^2I)\) and loss is \(\|\delta-\theta\|^2\), the usual estimator \(\delta_0(X)=X\) is inadmissible for \(p\ge3\).

Stein identity calculation

Proof. For estimators \(X+g(X)\), Stein's identity expresses the risk difference by a divergence term. Taking \(g(x)=-(p-2)\sigma^2x/\|x\|^2\) yields uniformly smaller risk when \(p\ge3\).

Hunt-Stein reduction

In testing problems with group symmetry, invariant or nearly invariant rules can often be taken as minimax candidates. The decision problem is then solved on orbits rather than the full space.

17. Invariance, équivariance et shrinkage

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/convolution-and-asymptotic-admissibility/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/poisson-and-infinitely-divisible-experiments/" %}

L'invariance réduit les problèmes de décision par symétrie. Le shrinkage montre que la symétrie ne garantit pas toujours l'admissibilité.

Expérience invariante et règle équivariante

Définition. Un groupe \(G\) agit sur paramètres, observations et actions. Une règle est équivariante si \(\delta(gx)=g\delta(x)\).

Phénomène de James-Stein

Théorème. Si \(X\sim N_p(\theta,\sigma^2I)\) et la perte est \(\|\delta-\theta\|^2\), l'estimateur \(X\) est inadmissible pour \(p\ge3\).

Calcul par l'identité de Stein

Preuve. L'identité de Stein calcule la différence de risque pour \(X+g(X)\). Le choix \(g(x)=-(p-2)\sigma^2x/\|x\|^2\) l'améliore uniformément.

Réduction de Hunt-Stein

Dans les tests avec symétrie de groupe, on peut souvent chercher des règles minimax parmi les règles invariantes.

17. 不变性、等变性与 Shrinkage

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/convolution-and-asymptotic-admissibility/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/poisson-and-infinitely-divisible-experiments/" %}

不变性利用对称性归约决策问题。Shrinkage 表明，仅有对称性并不总保证可容许性，特别是在多元正态均值问题中。

不变实验与等变规则

定义. 群 \(G\) 作用在参数、观测和行动上。若 \(\delta(gx)=g\delta(x)\)，则规则等变，并满足 \(R(g\theta,\delta)=R(\theta,\delta)\)。

James-Stein 现象

定理. 若 \(X\sim N_p(\theta,\sigma^2I)\)，损失为 \(\|\delta-\theta\|^2\)，则通常估计量 \(\delta_0(X)=X\) 在 \(p\ge3\) 时不可容许。

Stein 恒等式计算

证明. 对 \(X+g(X)\) 型估计量，Stein 恒等式把风险差写成散度项。取 \(g(x)=-(p-2)\sigma^2x/\|x\|^2\) 得到一致改进。

Hunt-Stein 归约

有群对称性的检验问题中，常可把 minimax 候选限制在不变或近似不变规则上，从而在轨道空间上求解。
