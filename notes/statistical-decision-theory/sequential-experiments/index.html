24. Sequential experiments

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/posterior-distributions-and-bayes-procedures/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/approximation-by-exponential-families/" %}

Sequential experiments allow the amount of data to depend on observations. The decision object is not a fixed sample but a stopped experiment.

Stopped experiment

Definition. A sequential experiment consists of observations adapted to a filtration and a stopping time \(\tau\). The observed data are \(\mathcal F_\tau\), and decision rules must be \(\mathcal F_\tau\)-measurable.

Likelihood process

Proposition. For two parameters, the likelihood ratio stopped at \(\tau\) is the likelihood-ratio martingale evaluated at \(\tau\), provided the stopping rule is nonanticipating.

Optional-stopping mechanism

Proof. Likelihood ratios form nonnegative martingales under the reference law. Stopping at \(\tau\) preserves the change-of-measure identity on \(\mathcal F_\tau\).

Brownian approximation

Under local Gaussian approximations, sequential experiments are often approximated by observing Brownian motion with drift up to a stopping time.

24. Expériences séquentielles

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/posterior-distributions-and-bayes-procedures/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/approximation-by-exponential-families/" %}

Les expériences séquentielles permettent à la quantité de données de dépendre des observations.

Expérience arrêtée

Définition. Une expérience séquentielle est adaptée à une filtration et arrêtée à un temps d'arrêt \(\tau\). Les règles sont \(\mathcal F_\tau\)-mesurables.

Processus de vraisemblance

Proposition. Le rapport de vraisemblance arrêté est le martingale de vraisemblance évalué en \(\tau\).

Mécanisme d'arrêt optionnel

Preuve. Les rapports de vraisemblance sont des martingales positives; l'arrêt préserve l'identité de changement de mesure.

Approximation brownienne

Les expériences séquentielles régulières se rapprochent souvent d'un mouvement brownien avec dérive observé jusqu'à un temps d'arrêt.

24. 序贯实验

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/posterior-distributions-and-bayes-procedures/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/approximation-by-exponential-families/" %}

序贯实验允许数据量依赖已观测数据。决策对象不再是固定样本，而是停止后的实验。

停止实验

定义. 序贯实验由适应某滤过的观测与停时 \(\tau\) 构成。观测信息为 \(\mathcal F_\tau\)，规则必须 \(\mathcal F_\tau\)-可测。

似然过程

命题. 对两个参数，停在 \(\tau\) 的似然比就是似然比鞅在 \(\tau\) 处的值，前提是停止规则不预见未来。

可选停止机制

证明. 在参考分布下，似然比是非负鞅。停在 \(\tau\) 后，\(\mathcal F_\tau\) 上的变测度恒等式仍成立。

布朗近似

局部高斯近似下，序贯实验常可近似为观察带漂移布朗运动直到某停时。
