24. Approximation by exponential families

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/sequential-experiments/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/independent-nonidentically-distributed-observations/" %}

Gaussian approximation is a special case of exponential-family approximation. Le Cam's broader program asks when an experiment behaves like a finite-rank exponential family.

Finite-rank exponential experiment

Definition. An exponential experiment has likelihoods of the form

\[ \log\frac{\mathrm{d}P_\theta}{\mathrm{d}P_0} =\eta(\theta)^\top T-c(\theta). \]

Approximate linearity of log likelihoods

Theorem. An experiment is approximable by a finite-rank exponential family when its log likelihood ratios are uniformly close to a finite-dimensional linear span plus normalizing constants.

Sufficient statistic construction

Proof. The coordinates spanning the likelihood ratios become approximate sufficient statistics. Once the residual likelihood is uniformly small, the induced finite-rank experiment has small deficiency.

Beyond Gaussian limits

LAMN, Poisson, and other non-Gaussian local limits can often be understood as exponential-family approximations with random or nonquadratic cumulants.

24. Approximation par familles exponentielles

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/sequential-experiments/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/independent-nonidentically-distributed-observations/" %}

L'approximation gaussienne est un cas particulier d'approximation par famille exponentielle.

Expérience exponentielle de rang fini

Définition. Une expérience exponentielle a des vraisemblances

\[ \log\frac{\mathrm{d}P_\theta}{\mathrm{d}P_0} =\eta(\theta)^\top T-c(\theta). \]

Linéarité approchée des log-vraisemblances

Théorème. L'approximation exponentielle demande que les log-rapports soient proches d'un espace linéaire fini-dimensionnel plus des constantes.

Construction de statistique suffisante

Preuve. Les coordonnées de cet espace deviennent des statistiques approximativement suffisantes; le résidu contrôle la déficience.

Au-delà des limites gaussiennes

LAMN, Poisson et d'autres limites non gaussiennes entrent naturellement dans ce cadre exponentiel.

24. 由指数族近似

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/sequential-experiments/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/independent-nonidentically-distributed-observations/" %}

Gaussian 近似是指数族近似的特殊情形。Le Cam 更一般的问题是实验何时表现得像有限秩指数族。

有限秩指数实验

定义. 指数实验的似然具有形式

\[ \log\frac{\mathrm{d}P_\theta}{\mathrm{d}P_0} =\eta(\theta)^\top T-c(\theta). \]

log-likelihood 的近似线性

定理. 当 log-likelihood ratio 能被有限维线性空间加归一化常数一致近似时，实验可由有限秩指数族近似。

充分统计量构造

证明. 张成似然比的坐标成为近似充分统计量。若剩余似然项一致小，则诱导的有限秩实验 deficiency 小。

超越 Gaussian 极限

LAMN、Poisson 与其他非 Gaussian 局部极限都可看作具有随机或非二次 cumulant 的指数族近似。
