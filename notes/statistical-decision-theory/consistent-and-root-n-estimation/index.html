30. Consistent and root-n estimation

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/iid-experiments-and-dqm/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/maximum-likelihood-and-pathologies/" %}

Consistency localizes the parameter; root-(n) estimation enters the LAN scale. One-step estimators show how rough consistency can be upgraded by likelihood curvature.

Consistency by separation

Definition. An estimator \(\hat\theta_n\) is consistent if \(P_\theta\{d(\hat\theta_n,\theta)>\varepsilon\}\to0\) for every \(\varepsilon>0\). Separation of risks or likelihoods supplies a common proof.

One-step estimator

Proposition. Given a consistent \(\tilde\theta_n\), a score \(S_n(\tilde\theta_n)\), and information \(J_n(\tilde\theta_n)\), define

\[ \hat\theta_n=\tilde\theta_n+J_n(\tilde\theta_n)^{-1}S_n(\tilde\theta_n). \]

Newton expansion

Proof. Taylor expansion of the score around \(\theta_0\) gives \(S_n(\tilde\theta_n)\approx S_n(\theta_0)-J_n(\theta_0)(\tilde\theta_n-\theta_0)\). The correction cancels the preliminary error to first order.

Root-n scale

If the preliminary estimator is close enough for the Taylor remainder to be negligible, the one-step estimator has the same first-order limit as the MLE.

30. Estimation consistante et à vitesse racine de n

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/iid-experiments-and-dqm/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/maximum-likelihood-and-pathologies/" %}

La consistance localise le paramètre; l'estimation en racine de (n) entre dans l'échelle LAN.

Consistance par séparation

Définition. \(\hat\theta_n\) est consistant si \(P_\theta\{d(\hat\theta_n,\theta)>\varepsilon\}\to0\).

Estimateur one-step

Proposition. À partir de \(\tilde\theta_n\), du score \(S_n\), et de l'information \(J_n\), on applique une correction de Newton.

Expansion de Newton

Preuve. Le développement du score annule l'erreur préliminaire au premier ordre.

Échelle racine de n

Si le reste est négligeable, l'estimateur one-step a la même limite de premier ordre que le maximum de vraisemblance.

30. 一致估计与 Root-n 估计

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/iid-experiments-and-dqm/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/maximum-likelihood-and-pathologies/" %}

一致性先定位参数；root-(n) 估计进入 LAN 尺度。one-step 估计说明如何用似然曲率升级粗一致估计。

由分离得到一致性

定义. 若对每个 \(\varepsilon>0\)，\(P_\theta\{d(\hat\theta_n,\theta)>\varepsilon\}\to0\)，则 \(\hat\theta_n\) 一致。

One-step 估计量

命题. 给定一致的 \(\tilde\theta_n\)、score \(S_n(\tilde\theta_n)\) 与信息 \(J_n(\tilde\theta_n)\)，用 Newton 修正得到 one-step 估计量。

Newton 展开

证明. 在 \(\theta_0\) 附近展开 score，修正项在一阶上抵消预估计误差。

Root-n 尺度

若预估计足够接近使 Taylor 余项可忽略，则 one-step 估计量与 MLE 有相同的一阶极限。
