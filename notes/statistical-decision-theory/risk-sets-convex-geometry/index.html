3. Risk sets and convex geometry

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/decision-problems-rules-risks/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/risk-bayes-and-minimax/" %}

Randomization turns decision theory into convex geometry. Supporting hyperplanes become priors, boundary points become Bayes risks, and domination becomes movement inside the positive cone.

Risk set

Definition. For a finite parameter set \(\Theta=\{\theta_1,\ldots,\theta_m\}\), the risk set is \(\mathcal R=\{(R(\theta_1,\delta),\ldots,R(\theta_m,\delta)):\delta\in\mathcal D\}\subset\mathbb R^m\). Randomization makes \(\mathcal R\) convex.

Supporting priors

Proposition. A prior \(\Pi=(\pi_1,\ldots,\pi_m)\) defines the linear functional \(r_\Pi(R)=\sum_j\pi_jR(\theta_j)\). A Bayes rule is a point of \(\mathcal R\) minimizing this supporting functional.

Separation proof of Bayes optimality

Proof. If a risk vector is optimal for a prior, the hyperplane orthogonal to \(\Pi\) supports the risk set at that vector. Conversely, exposed boundary points are Bayes risk vectors for some prior.

Admissible boundary

A risk vector is inadmissible when another vector lies below it in every coordinate and strictly below in one. Thus admissible rules live on the lower boundary of the convex risk set.

3. Ensembles de risque et géométrie convexe

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/decision-problems-rules-risks/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/risk-bayes-and-minimax/" %}

La randomisation transforme la théorie de la décision en géométrie convexe. Les hyperplans d'appui deviennent des a priori.

Ensemble de risque

Définition. Pour \(\Theta=\{\theta_1,\ldots,\theta_m\}\), l'ensemble de risque est \(\mathcal R=\{(R(\theta_1,\delta),\ldots,R(\theta_m,\delta)):\delta\in\mathcal D\}\subset\mathbb R^m\). La randomisation le rend convexe.

A priori d'appui

Proposition. Un a priori \(\Pi=(\pi_1,\ldots,\pi_m)\) définit \(r_\Pi(R)=\sum_j\pi_jR(\theta_j)\). Une règle bayésienne minimise cette fonctionnelle linéaire sur \(\mathcal R\).

Preuve par séparation

Preuve. Un hyperplan d'appui orthogonal à \(\Pi\) certifie l'optimalité bayésienne. Les points exposés de la frontière inférieure sont donc des risques bayésiens.

Frontière admissible

L'inadmissibilité correspond à un autre vecteur de risque plus petit dans toutes les coordonnées et strictement plus petit dans au moins une.

3. 风险集合与凸几何

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/decision-problems-rules-risks/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/risk-bayes-and-minimax/" %}

随机化把决策理论变成凸几何。支撑超平面对应先验，边界点对应 Bayes 风险，支配对应向正锥内移动。

风险集合

定义. 当 \(\Theta=\{\theta_1,\ldots,\theta_m\}\) 时，风险集合为 \(\mathcal R=\{(R(\theta_1,\delta),\ldots,R(\theta_m,\delta)):\delta\in\mathcal D\}\subset\mathbb R^m\)。随机化使 \(\mathcal R\) 凸。

支撑先验

命题. 先验 \(\Pi=(\pi_1,\ldots,\pi_m)\) 定义线性泛函 \(r_\Pi(R)=\sum_j\pi_jR(\theta_j)\)。Bayes 规则就是在 \(\mathcal R\) 上最小化该泛函的点。

分离证明

证明. 若某风险向量对先验最优，则垂直于 \(\Pi\) 的超平面在该点支撑风险集合。反过来，被暴露的下边界点对应某个 Bayes 风险。

可容许边界

若另一个风险向量在所有坐标上不大于它且某处严格小于它，则原规则不可容许。因此可容许规则位于凸风险集合的下边界。
