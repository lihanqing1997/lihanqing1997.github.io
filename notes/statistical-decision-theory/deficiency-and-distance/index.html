9. Deficiency and distance between experiments

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/blackwell-comparison-and-transitions/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/likelihood-ratios-hellinger-geometry/" %}

Deficiency turns exact Blackwell comparison into approximate comparison. It is the basic metric language for asymptotic equivalence of experiments.

Deficiency

Definition. For experiments \(\mathcal E=\{P_\theta\}\) and \(\mathcal F=\{Q_\theta\}\), the deficiency of \(\mathcal E\) relative to \(\mathcal F\) is

\[ \delta(\mathcal E,\mathcal F)=\inf_K\sup_{\theta\in\Theta}\|P_\theta K-Q_\theta\|_{\mathrm{TV}}. \]

Le Cam distance

Definition. The symmetric Le Cam distance is \(\Delta(\mathcal E,\mathcal F)=\max\{\delta(\mathcal E,\mathcal F),\delta(\mathcal F,\mathcal E)\}\). Small distance means each experiment can simulate the other with small total-variation error.

Risk transfer by total variation

Proof. If losses are bounded by one and \(P_\theta K\) is within \(\varepsilon\) of \(Q_\theta\) in total variation, then the risk of any rule changes by at most \(\varepsilon\), uniformly in \(\theta\).

Asymptotic equivalence

A sequence \(\mathcal E_n\) is asymptotically equivalent to \(\mathcal F_n\) when \(\Delta(\mathcal E_n,\mathcal F_n)\to0\). Then bounded-loss decision problems have the same asymptotic value.

9. Déficience et distance entre expériences

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/blackwell-comparison-and-transitions/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/likelihood-ratios-hellinger-geometry/" %}

La déficience transforme la comparaison exacte de Blackwell en comparaison approchée.

Déficience

Définition. La déficience de \(\mathcal E=\{P_\theta\}\) par rapport à \(\mathcal F=\{Q_\theta\}\) est

\[ \delta(\mathcal E,\mathcal F)=\inf_K\sup_{\theta\in\Theta}\|P_\theta K-Q_\theta\|_{\mathrm{TV}}. \]

Distance de Le Cam

Définition. La distance symétrique est \(\Delta(\mathcal E,\mathcal F)=\max\{\delta(\mathcal E,\mathcal F),\delta(\mathcal F,\mathcal E)\}\).

Transfert du risque par variation totale

Preuve. Pour une perte bornée par un, une erreur \(\varepsilon\) en variation totale change le risque d'au plus \(\varepsilon\), uniformément en \(\theta\).

Équivalence asymptotique

\(\Delta(\mathcal E_n,\mathcal F_n)\to0\) signifie que les problèmes de décision à perte bornée ont la même valeur asymptotique.

9. 实验之间的 Deficiency 与距离

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/blackwell-comparison-and-transitions/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/likelihood-ratios-hellinger-geometry/" %}

deficiency 把精确的 Blackwell 比较变成近似比较，是实验渐近等价的基本度量语言。

Deficiency

定义. 实验 \(\mathcal E=\{P_\theta\}\) 相对于 \(\mathcal F=\{Q_\theta\}\) 的 deficiency 为

\[ \delta(\mathcal E,\mathcal F)=\inf_K\sup_{\theta\in\Theta}\|P_\theta K-Q_\theta\|_{\mathrm{TV}}. \]

Le Cam 距离

定义. 对称距离为 \(\Delta(\mathcal E,\mathcal F)=\max\{\delta(\mathcal E,\mathcal F),\delta(\mathcal F,\mathcal E)\}\)。距离小表示两个实验能互相近似模拟。

由总变差转移风险

证明. 若损失被一控制，且 \(P_\theta K\) 与 \(Q_\theta\) 的总变差距离不超过 \(\varepsilon\)，则任意规则的风险改变至多 \(\varepsilon\)。

渐近等价

若 \(\Delta(\mathcal E_n,\mathcal F_n)\to0\)，则有界损失决策问题在两列实验下具有相同的渐近值。
