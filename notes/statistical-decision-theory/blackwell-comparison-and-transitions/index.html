7. Blackwell comparison and Markov transitions

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/sufficiency-and-reduction/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/deficiency-and-distance/" %}

Blackwell comparison orders experiments by information. One experiment is more informative if the other can be obtained from it by randomized post-processing.

Markov transition

Definition. A transition \(K\) from \(\mathcal X\) to \(\mathcal Y\) is a Markov kernel. It sends a measure \(P\) to \(PK\), defined by \((PK)(B)=\int K(x,B)\,P(\mathrm{d}x)\).

Blackwell order

Theorem. \(\mathcal E=\{P_\theta\}\) is more informative than \(\mathcal F=\{Q_\theta\}\) if there exists \(K\) such that \(Q_\theta=P_\theta K\) for all \(\theta\). Then every decision problem has minimal risk under \(\mathcal E\) no larger than under \(\mathcal F\).

Simulation of rules

Proof. A rule for \(\mathcal F\) can be run after observing \(X\sim P_\theta\) by first drawing \(Y\sim K(X,\cdot)\). The induced distribution of \(Y\) is \(Q_\theta\), so the risk matches the rule in \(\mathcal F\).

Information as experiment dominance

Blackwell comparison turns “more data” into an exact risk statement: a more informative experiment cannot make any decision problem harder.

7. Comparaison de Blackwell et transitions de Markov

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/sufficiency-and-reduction/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/deficiency-and-distance/" %}

La comparaison de Blackwell ordonne les expériences par information. Une expérience est plus informative si l'autre s'obtient par post-traitement randomisé.

Transition de Markov

Définition. Une transition \(K\) de \(\mathcal X\) vers \(\mathcal Y\) est un noyau. Elle envoie \(P\) vers \(PK\), où \((PK)(B)=\int K(x,B)\,P(\mathrm{d}x)\).

Ordre de Blackwell

Théorème. \(\mathcal E=\{P_\theta\}\) domine \(\mathcal F=\{Q_\theta\}\) s'il existe \(K\) tel que \(Q_\theta=P_\theta K\). Alors tout problème de décision est au moins aussi facile sous \(\mathcal E\).

Simulation des règles

Preuve. Une règle pour \(\mathcal F\) est simulée depuis \(\mathcal E\) en tirant \(Y\sim K(X,\cdot)\), puis en appliquant la règle sur \(Y\).

Information comme domination d'expériences

La comparaison de Blackwell donne une traduction exacte de l'information : plus d'information ne peut augmenter le risque optimal.

7. Blackwell 比较与 Markov 转移

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/sufficiency-and-reduction/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/deficiency-and-distance/" %}

Blackwell 比较用信息量给实验排序。若一个实验经过随机后处理可得到另一个实验，则前者更有信息。

Markov 转移

定义. 从 \(\mathcal X\) 到 \(\mathcal Y\) 的转移 \(K\) 是 Markov 核。它把测度 \(P\) 送到 \(PK\)，其中 \((PK)(B)=\int K(x,B)\,P(\mathrm{d}x)\)。

Blackwell 序

定理. 若存在 \(K\) 使 \(Q_\theta=P_\theta K\) 对所有 \(\theta\) 成立，则 \(\mathcal E=\{P_\theta\}\) 比 \(\mathcal F=\{Q_\theta\}\) 更有信息。此时任何决策问题在 \(\mathcal E\) 下的最优风险不大于在 \(\mathcal F\) 下的最优风险。

规则模拟证明

证明. 观察 \(X\sim P_\theta\) 后先抽取 \(Y\sim K(X,\cdot)\)，再使用 \(\mathcal F\) 中的规则。由于 \(Y\sim Q_\theta\)，风险完全一致。

信息作为实验支配

Blackwell 比较把“信息更多”变成精确风险陈述：更有信息的实验不会让任何决策问题变难。
