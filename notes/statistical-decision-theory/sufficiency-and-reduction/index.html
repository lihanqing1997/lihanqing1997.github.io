7. Sufficiency and reduction of experiments

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/admissibility-and-complete-classes/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/blackwell-comparison-and-transitions/" %}

Sufficiency says that an experiment can be compressed without changing any decision problem. In Le Cam language, sufficiency is exact equivalence by transitions.

Statistic as a transition

Definition. A statistic \(T:\mathcal X\to\mathcal Y\) sends \(\mathcal E=\{P_\theta\}\) to \(T\mathcal E=\{P_\theta T^{-1}\}\). This map is a Markov transition from observations to statistic values.

Sufficiency as lossless reduction

Theorem. A statistic \(T\) is sufficient when there exists a transition \(K\) from \(\mathcal Y\) back to \(\mathcal X\) such that \(K(P_\theta T^{-1})=P_\theta\) for every \(\theta\). Then \(T\mathcal E\) and \(\mathcal E\) have the same attainable risks.

Reconstruction kernel argument

Proof. Any rule using \(X\) can be simulated from \(T(X)\) by first drawing \(X'\) from the conditional kernel \(K(\cdot\mid T)\), then applying the original rule. The risk is unchanged for every \(\theta\).

Conditional distributions

In dominated models this is the familiar factorization or conditional-distribution criterion: the conditional law of \(X\) given \(T\) does not depend on \(\theta\).

7. Suffisance et réduction des expériences

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/admissibility-and-complete-classes/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/blackwell-comparison-and-transitions/" %}

La suffisance dit qu'une expérience peut être comprimée sans changer les problèmes de décision.

Statistique comme transition

Définition. Une statistique \(T:\mathcal X\to\mathcal Y\) envoie \(\mathcal E=\{P_\theta\}\) vers \(T\mathcal E=\{P_\theta T^{-1}\}\).

Suffisance comme réduction sans perte

Théorème. \(T\) est suffisante s'il existe un noyau \(K\) tel que \(K(P_\theta T^{-1})=P_\theta\) pour tout \(\theta\). Les risques atteignables sont alors les mêmes.

Argument par noyau de reconstruction

Preuve. Une règle utilisant \(X\) est simulée à partir de \(T(X)\) en reconstruisant \(X'\) par \(K\), puis en appliquant la règle originale.

Lois conditionnelles

Dans les modèles dominés, cela devient le critère de factorisation : la loi conditionnelle de \(X\) sachant \(T\) ne dépend pas de \(\theta\).

7. 充分性与实验归约

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/admissibility-and-complete-classes/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/blackwell-comparison-and-transitions/" %}

充分性表示一个实验可以被压缩而不改变任何决策问题。在 Le Cam 语言中，充分性是由转移核给出的精确等价。

统计量作为转移

定义. 统计量 \(T:\mathcal X\to\mathcal Y\) 把实验 \(\mathcal E=\{P_\theta\}\) 送到 \(T\mathcal E=\{P_\theta T^{-1}\}\)。

无损归约意义下的充分性

定理. 若存在从 \(\mathcal Y\) 回到 \(\mathcal X\) 的核 \(K\)，使 \(K(P_\theta T^{-1})=P_\theta\) 对所有 \(\theta\) 成立，则 \(T\) 充分，且两个实验有相同的可达风险。

重构核证明

证明. 任何使用 \(X\) 的规则，都可以先由 \(T(X)\) 通过核 \(K\) 重构 \(X'\)，再使用原规则。于是每个参数下的风险不变。

条件分布判别

在被控制模型中，这就是分解定理或条件分布准则：给定 \(T\) 后，\(X\) 的条件分布不依赖 \(\theta\)。
