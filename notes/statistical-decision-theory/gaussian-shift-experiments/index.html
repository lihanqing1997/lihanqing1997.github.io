13. Gaussian shift experiments

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/le-cam-lemmas/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/local-asymptotic-normality/" %}

Gaussian shift experiments are the canonical local limits of smooth statistical models. Their risks become the benchmark for asymptotic efficiency.

Finite-dimensional Gaussian shift

Definition. In the experiment \(\mathcal G=\{N(h,I^{-1}):h\in H\}\), one observes \(Y=h+Z\), where \(Z\sim N(0,I^{-1})\). The parameter \(h\) is local displacement.

Likelihood ratio in the shift experiment

Proposition. Relative to \(h=0\), the log likelihood ratio is

\[ \ell_h(Y)=h^\top IY-\frac12h^\top Ih. \]

Gaussian completion of the square

Proof. Compare the two normal densities. The quadratic terms in \(Y\) cancel, leaving the linear term \(h^\top IY\) and the curvature term \(-h^\top Ih/2\).

Risk benchmark

When \(\mathcal E_n\) converges locally to \(\mathcal G\), no regular procedure can beat the corresponding Gaussian-shift risk in shrinking neighborhoods.

13. Expériences de décalage gaussiennes

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/le-cam-lemmas/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/local-asymptotic-normality/" %}

Les expériences de décalage gaussiennes sont les limites locales canoniques des modèles réguliers.

Décalage gaussien fini-dimensionnel

Définition. Dans \(\mathcal G=\{N(h,I^{-1}):h\in H\}\), on observe \(Y=h+Z\) avec \(Z\sim N(0,I^{-1})\).

Rapport de vraisemblance gaussien

Proposition. Par rapport à \(h=0\),

\[ \ell_h(Y)=h^\top IY-\frac12h^\top Ih. \]

Complétion du carré gaussien

Preuve. La comparaison des densités normales annule les termes quadratiques en \(Y\) et laisse le terme linéaire et la courbure.

Référence de risque

Les risques du décalage gaussien bornent les risques asymptotiques des modèles qui y convergent localement.

13. Gaussian Shift 实验

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/le-cam-lemmas/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/local-asymptotic-normality/" %}

Gaussian shift 实验是光滑统计模型的典型局部极限，其风险成为渐近效率的基准。

有限维 Gaussian shift

定义. 在实验 \(\mathcal G=\{N(h,I^{-1}):h\in H\}\) 中，观察 \(Y=h+Z\)，其中 \(Z\sim N(0,I^{-1})\)。

Shift 实验中的似然比

命题. 相对于 \(h=0\)，对数似然比为

\[ \ell_h(Y)=h^\top IY-\frac12h^\top Ih. \]

Gaussian 配方证明

证明. 比较两个正态密度时，关于 \(Y\) 的二次项相消，只留下线性项 \(h^\top IY\) 与曲率项 \(-h^\top Ih/2\)。

风险基准

当 \(\mathcal E_n\) 局部收敛到 \(\mathcal G\) 时，任何正则程序都不能在缩小邻域中优于 Gaussian shift 风险。
