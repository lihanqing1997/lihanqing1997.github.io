21. Quadratic likelihood approximation

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/global-asymptotic-normality/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/tests-and-confidence-sets-gaussian/" %}

Quadratic likelihood approximation is the computational face of LAN. It explains one-step estimators, Wald statistics, score tests, and likelihood-ratio tests.

Local quadratic form

Definition. Around a center \(\tilde\theta_n\), write \(u=r_n(\theta-\tilde\theta_n)\). A quadratic likelihood approximation has form

\[ \ell_n(\tilde\theta_n+u/r_n)-\ell_n(\tilde\theta_n) \approx u^\top S_n-\frac12u^\top J_nu. \]

Argmax of the quadratic likelihood

Proposition. If \(J_n\) is nonsingular, the maximizer of the quadratic approximation is \(u_n^\star=J_n^{-1}S_n\). This gives the one-step update \(\tilde\theta_n+J_n^{-1}S_n/r_n\).

Completing the square

Proof. The quadratic equals \(-\frac12(u-J_n^{-1}S_n)^\top J_n(u-J_n^{-1}S_n)+\frac12S_n^\top J_n^{-1}S_n\). The maximum is therefore at \(J_n^{-1}S_n\).

Observed information

When \(J_n\) is the observed information, the approximation adapts to local curvature. In regular models \(J_n\) converges to Fisher information.

21. Approximation quadratique de la vraisemblance

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/global-asymptotic-normality/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/tests-and-confidence-sets-gaussian/" %}

L'approximation quadratique est la face calculatoire de LAN.

Forme quadratique locale

Définition. Autour de \(\tilde\theta_n\), avec \(u=r_n(\theta-\tilde\theta_n)\),

\[ \ell_n(\tilde\theta_n+u/r_n)-\ell_n(\tilde\theta_n) \approx u^\top S_n-\frac12u^\top J_nu. \]

Argmax de la vraisemblance quadratique

Proposition. Si \(J_n\) est inversible, le maximiseur est \(u_n^\star=J_n^{-1}S_n\), ce qui donne la correction one-step.

Complétion du carré

Preuve. On réécrit la quadratique comme un carré négatif plus la constante \(\frac12S_n^\top J_n^{-1}S_n\).

Information observée

Avec \(J_n\) information observée, l'approximation s'adapte à la courbure locale.

21. 似然的二次近似

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/global-asymptotic-normality/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/tests-and-confidence-sets-gaussian/" %}

似然二次近似是 LAN 的计算形式，解释 one-step 估计、Wald 统计量、score 检验与似然比检验。

局部二次型

定义. 围绕中心 \(\tilde\theta_n\)，令 \(u=r_n(\theta-\tilde\theta_n)\)。二次似然近似为

\[ \ell_n(\tilde\theta_n+u/r_n)-\ell_n(\tilde\theta_n) \approx u^\top S_n-\frac12u^\top J_nu. \]

二次似然的 argmax

命题. 若 \(J_n\) 非奇异，二次近似的最大点是 \(u_n^\star=J_n^{-1}S_n\)，给出 one-step 更新 \(\tilde\theta_n+J_n^{-1}S_n/r_n\)。

配方证明

证明. 把二次型写成 \(-\frac12(u-J_n^{-1}S_n)^\top J_n(u-J_n^{-1}S_n)+\frac12S_n^\top J_n^{-1}S_n\)，最大点显然为 \(J_n^{-1}S_n\)。

观测信息

当 \(J_n\) 是观测信息时，近似会适应局部曲率；正则模型中它收敛到 Fisher 信息。
