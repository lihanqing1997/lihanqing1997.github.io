31. Maximum likelihood and its pathologies

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/consistent-and-root-n-estimation/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/random-sample-sizes-and-stopping-rules/" %}

Maximum likelihood is powerful in regular experiments, but Le Cam's perspective warns that likelihood maximization alone does not guarantee good decision behavior.

Likelihood maximizer

Definition. An MLE is any measurable \(\hat\theta_n\) satisfying \(\ell_n(\hat\theta_n)\ge \sup_\theta\ell_n(\theta)-o_p(1)\). Approximate maximizers are enough for first-order asymptotics.

Regular MLE limit

Theorem. In regular LAN models with suitable consistency and curvature,

\[ \sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0)\Rightarrow N(0,I_{\theta_0}^{-1}). \]

Argmax of the quadratic approximation

Proof. Consistency puts the MLE in a LAN neighborhood. The log likelihood is then approximated by a concave quadratic whose argmax is \(I_{\theta_0}^{-1}\Delta_n\).

Incidental parameters and nonregularity

Neyman-Scott type problems show that maximizing likelihood with growing nuisance dimension can be inconsistent. The experiment, not the slogan “MLE,” determines the asymptotics.

31. Maximum de vraisemblance et pathologies

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/consistent-and-root-n-estimation/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/random-sample-sizes-and-stopping-rules/" %}

Le maximum de vraisemblance est puissant dans les expériences régulières, mais il peut échouer hors régularité.

Maximiseur de vraisemblance

Définition. Un MLE est un maximiseur mesurable, exact ou approché, de la log-vraisemblance.

Limite régulière du MLE

Théorème. Dans les modèles LAN réguliers avec consistance et courbure, \(\sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0)\) converge vers \(N(0,I_{\theta_0}^{-1})\).

Argmax de l'approximation quadratique

Preuve. La consistance place le MLE dans un voisinage LAN; la vraisemblance devient quadratique et son argmax donne la limite.

Paramètres incidents et non-régularité

Les exemples de Neyman-Scott montrent que le MLE peut être inconsistant lorsque la dimension nuisance croît.

31. 最大似然及其病态现象

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/consistent-and-root-n-estimation/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/random-sample-sizes-and-stopping-rules/" %}

最大似然在正则实验中很强，但勒卡姆视角提醒我们：单纯最大化似然并不保证好的决策表现。

似然最大化点

定义. MLE 是满足 \(\ell_n(\hat\theta_n)\ge \sup_\theta\ell_n(\theta)-o_p(1)\) 的可测近似最大点。一阶渐近中近似最大点通常足够。

正则 MLE 极限

定理. 在正则 LAN 模型中，若一致性和曲率条件成立，则 \(\sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0)\Rightarrow N(0,I_{\theta_0}^{-1})\)。

二次近似的 argmax

证明. 一致性把 MLE 放入 LAN 邻域；对数似然由凹二次型近似，其最大点为 \(I_{\theta_0}^{-1}\Delta_n\)。

Incidental parameters 与非正则性

内曼-Scott 型问题表明， nuisance 维数增长时 MLE 可能不一致。决定渐近性质的是实验本身，而不是“MLE”这个名称。
