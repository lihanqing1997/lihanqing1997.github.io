2. Decision problems, rules, and risks

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/overview-and-roadmap/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/risk-sets-convex-geometry/" %}

The risk function is the central mathematical object. It records the loss of a rule at every parameter value and makes comparisons between procedures precise.

Statistical decision problem

Definition. A statistical decision problem is \((\Theta,\mathcal X,\{P_\theta\},\mathcal A,L)\). A nonrandomized rule \(\delta:\mathcal X\to\mathcal A\) has risk

\[ R(\theta,\delta)=E_\theta L\{\theta,\delta(X)\}. \]

Randomized rules and convex risk sets

Proposition. Randomized rules choose a probability measure on \(\mathcal A\) after observing \(X\). If rules are allowed to randomize, the attainable set of risk functions is convex.

Convexification by mixing rules

Proof. If \(\delta_1,\delta_2\) have risks \(R_1,R_2\), then the rule that uses \(\delta_1\) with probability \(\lambda\) has risk \(\lambda R_1+(1-\lambda)R_2\). Hence mixtures of rules give mixtures of risks.

Risk functions as procedure comparisons

A scalar summary is chosen only after a principle is chosen: Bayes risk averages \(R\), minimax risk takes its supremum, and admissibility uses the partial order induced by pointwise domination.

2. Problèmes de décision, règles et risques

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/overview-and-roadmap/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/risk-sets-convex-geometry/" %}

La fonction de risque est l'objet mathématique central. Elle décrit la perte d'une règle pour chaque valeur du paramètre.

Problème statistique de décision

Définition. Un problème de décision est \((\Theta,\mathcal X,\{P_\theta\},\mathcal A,L)\). Une règle \(\delta:\mathcal X\to\mathcal A\) a pour risque

\[ R(\theta,\delta)=E_\theta L\{\theta,\delta(X)\}. \]

Règles randomisées et ensembles de risques convexes

Proposition. Une règle randomisée choisit une loi sur \(\mathcal A\) après l'observation. En autorisant la randomisation, l'ensemble des risques atteignables devient convexe.

Convexification par mélange de règles

Preuve. Le mélange de deux règles de risques \(R_1\) et \(R_2\) avec poids \(\lambda\) a pour risque \(\lambda R_1+(1-\lambda)R_2\).

Fonctions de risque comme comparaisons

Bayes moyenne le risque, minimax en prend le supremum, et l'admissibilité utilise l'ordre partiel par domination point par point.

2. 决策问题、规则与风险

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/overview-and-roadmap/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/risk-sets-convex-geometry/" %}

风险函数是核心数学对象。它记录一个规则在每个参数值下的损失，使程序之间的比较精确化。

统计决策问题

定义. 统计决策问题是 \((\Theta,\mathcal X,\{P_\theta\},\mathcal A,L)\)。非随机规则 \(\delta:\mathcal X\to\mathcal A\) 的风险为

\[ R(\theta,\delta)=E_\theta L\{\theta,\delta(X)\}. \]

随机规则与凸风险集合

命题. 随机规则在观察 \(X\) 后选择行动空间 \(\mathcal A\) 上的一个分布。允许随机化后，可达到的风险函数集合是凸的。

由混合规则得到凸化

证明. 若两个规则风险为 \(R_1,R_2\)，以概率 \(\lambda\) 使用第一个规则的混合规则，其风险为 \(\lambda R_1+(1-\lambda)R_2\)。

风险函数作为程序比较

Bayes 风险对 \(R\) 取平均，minimax 风险取上确界，可容许性则使用逐点支配给出的偏序。
