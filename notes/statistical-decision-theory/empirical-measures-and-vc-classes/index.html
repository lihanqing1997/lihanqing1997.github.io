28. Empirical measures and VC classes

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/lower-bounds/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/iid-experiments-and-dqm/" %}

Le Cam's product-experiment theory connects naturally to empirical measures. VC bounds provide uniform control needed for minimum-distance estimators.

Empirical measure indexed by sets

Definition. For observations \(X_1,\ldots,X_n\), the empirical measure is \(P_n(A)=n^{-1}\sum_i1_A(X_i)\). Uniform control over \(A\in\mathcal C\) concerns \(\sup_{A\in\mathcal C}|P_nA-PA|\).

VC uniform law

Theorem. If \(\mathcal C\) is a VC class, then \(\sup_{A\in\mathcal C}|P_nA-PA|\to0\) under broad measurability conditions, and exponential inequalities give finite-sample control.

Symmetrization and growth

Proof. Symmetrization reduces deviations to Rademacher averages, while Sauer-Shelah bounds the number of traces of \(\mathcal C\) on the sample by a polynomial in \(n\).

Minimum-distance estimation

Estimators based on minimizing empirical distances over VC classes inherit consistency and rates from these uniform empirical bounds.

28. Mesures empiriques et classes VC

La théorie des expériences produits se relie naturellement aux mesures empiriques et aux classes VC.

Mesure empirique indexée par des ensembles

Définition. La mesure empirique est \(P_n(A)=n^{-1}\sum_i1_A(X_i)\). On contrôle uniformément \(\sup_{A\in\mathcal C}|P_nA-PA|\).

Loi uniforme VC

Théorème. Pour une classe VC, cette sup-norme converge vers zéro et des inégalités exponentielles donnent un contrôle non asymptotique.

Symétrisation et croissance

Preuve. La symétrisation ramène aux moyennes de Rademacher, puis Sauer-Shelah borne les traces possibles.

Estimation par distance minimale

Les estimateurs de distance minimale utilisent ces bornes uniformes pour obtenir consistance et vitesses.

28. 经验测度与 VC 类

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/lower-bounds/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/iid-experiments-and-dqm/" %}

Le Cam 的乘积实验理论自然连接到经验测度。VC 界提供 minimum-distance 估计所需的统一控制。

由集合索引的经验测度

定义. 经验测度为 \(P_n(A)=n^{-1}\sum_i1_A(X_i)\)。统一控制研究 \(\sup_{A\in\mathcal C}|P_nA-PA|\)。

VC 统一律

定理. 若 \(\mathcal C\) 是 VC 类，则在温和可测条件下 \(\sup_{A\in\mathcal C}|P_nA-PA|\to0\)，并且指数不等式给出有限样本控制。

对称化与增长界

证明. 对称化把偏差化为 Rademacher 平均，而 Sauer-Shelah 引理把样本上的 trace 数控制为 \(n\) 的多项式。

Minimum-distance 估计

基于 VC 类经验距离最小化的估计量，其一致性和速度来自这些统一经验界。
