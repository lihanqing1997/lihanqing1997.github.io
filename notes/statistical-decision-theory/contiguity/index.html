11. Contiguity

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/likelihood-ratios-hellinger-geometry/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/weak-convergence-of-experiments/" %}

Contiguity is the asymptotic version of absolute continuity that is strong enough to transfer (o_p(1)) statements between changing measures.

Contiguous sequences

Definition. A sequence \(Q_n\) is contiguous with respect to \(P_n\), written \(Q_n\triangleleft P_n\), if \(P_n(A_n)\to0\) implies \(Q_n(A_n)\to0\) for every event sequence \(A_n\).

Likelihood-ratio tightness criterion

Lemma. If \(Q_n\ll P_n\) with \(L_n=\mathrm{d}Q_n/\mathrm{d}P_n\), then \(Q_n\triangleleft P_n\) when the laws of \(L_n\) under \(P_n\) are tight and have no mass escaping to infinity in the limit.

Transfer of negligible events

Proof. Write \(Q_n(A_n)=E_{P_n}L_n1_{A_n}\). On \(\{L_n\le M\}\) this is at most \(MP_n(A_n)\); the complement is controlled by tightness of \(L_n\).

Local alternatives

LAN alternatives \(P_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}^{(n)}\) are typically mutually contiguous with \(P_{\theta_0}^{(n)}\). This is why local power can be computed under one base measure and transferred to another.

11. Contiguïté

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/likelihood-ratios-hellinger-geometry/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/weak-convergence-of-experiments/" %}

La contiguïté est la version asymptotique de la continuité absolue qui permet de transférer les énoncés (o_p(1)).

Suites contiguës

Définition. On écrit \(Q_n\triangleleft P_n\) si \(P_n(A_n)\to0\) implique \(Q_n(A_n)\to0\) pour toute suite d'événements \(A_n\).

Critère par rapports de vraisemblance tendus

Lemme. Si \(L_n=\mathrm{d}Q_n/\mathrm{d}P_n\), la tension de \(L_n\) sous \(P_n\), avec absence de masse à l'infini, donne \(Q_n\triangleleft P_n\).

Transfert des événements négligeables

Preuve. On écrit \(Q_n(A_n)=E_{P_n}L_n1_{A_n}\). Sur \(\{L_n\le M\}\), le terme est borné par \(MP_n(A_n)\); le reste est contrôlé par la tension.

Alternatives locales

Dans les modèles LAN, \(P_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}^{(n)}\) et \(P_{\theta_0}^{(n)}\) sont généralement mutuellement contiguës.

11. Contiguity

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/likelihood-ratios-hellinger-geometry/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/weak-convergence-of-experiments/" %}

contiguity 是绝对连续性的渐近版本，它足以在变化测度之间转移 (o_p(1)) 型陈述。

Contiguous 测度序列

定义. 若对任意事件列 \(A_n\)，\(P_n(A_n)\to0\) 都推出 \(Q_n(A_n)\to0\)，则记 \(Q_n\triangleleft P_n\)。

似然比紧性准则

引理. 若 \(Q_n\ll P_n\)，\(L_n=\mathrm{d}Q_n/\mathrm{d}P_n\)，则 \(L_n\) 在 \(P_n\) 下紧且极限无质量逃到无穷时，得到 \(Q_n\triangleleft P_n\)。

可忽略事件的转移

证明. 写 \(Q_n(A_n)=E_{P_n}L_n1_{A_n}\)。在 \(\{L_n\le M\}\) 上它不超过 \(MP_n(A_n)\)，余项由 \(L_n\) 的紧性控制。

局部备择

LAN 模型中，\(P_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}^{(n)}\) 通常与 \(P_{\theta_0}^{(n)}\) 相互 contiguous，因此局部功效可以在基准测度下计算后转移。
