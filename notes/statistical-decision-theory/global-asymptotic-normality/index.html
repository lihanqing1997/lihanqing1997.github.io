20. Global asymptotic normality

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/poisson-and-infinitely-divisible-experiments/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/quadratic-likelihood-approximation/" %}

Local Gaussian approximations are not enough for estimation over a whole parameter space. Global theory combines preliminary localization with local quadratic approximation.

Preliminary localization

Definition. A preliminary estimator \(\tilde\theta_n\) localizes the parameter if \(d(\tilde\theta_n,\theta)=O_{P_\theta}(r_n^{-1})\) uniformly on relevant sets. It lets the procedure choose the right local chart.

Global-to-local reduction

Theorem. If preliminary estimators localize uniformly and local LAN approximations hold in neighborhoods of \(\tilde\theta_n\), then global estimation can be reduced to local Gaussian decision problems.

Random centering argument

Proof. Condition on the preliminary estimator falling in a small neighborhood. On that event the likelihood is approximated by a quadratic form around the random center; the complement has probability tending to zero.

Random information

Le Cam's global theory allows observed or random information matrices. This is essential for mixed normal limits and heteroscedastic local approximations.

20. Normalité asymptotique globale

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/poisson-and-infinitely-divisible-experiments/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/quadratic-likelihood-approximation/" %}

Les approximations locales gaussiennes ne suffisent pas pour estimer sur tout l'espace des paramètres.

Localisation préliminaire

Définition. Un estimateur préliminaire \(\tilde\theta_n\) localise le paramètre si \(d(\tilde\theta_n,\theta)=O_{P_\theta}(r_n^{-1})\) uniformément.

Réduction globale-locale

Théorème. Une bonne localisation préliminaire et des approximations LAN locales réduisent l'estimation globale à des problèmes gaussiens locaux.

Argument par centrage aléatoire

Preuve. Sur l'événement où \(\tilde\theta_n\) est proche, la vraisemblance est approchée par une forme quadratique autour d'un centre aléatoire.

Information aléatoire

La théorie globale permet des matrices d'information observées ou aléatoires, utiles pour les limites mixtes normales.

20. 全局渐近正态性

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/poisson-and-infinitely-divisible-experiments/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-decision-theory/quadratic-likelihood-approximation/" %}

局部高斯近似不足以处理整个参数空间上的估计。全局理论把预定位估计与局部二次近似结合起来。

预定位估计

定义. 若 \(\tilde\theta_n\) 满足 \(d(\tilde\theta_n,\theta)=O_{P_\theta}(r_n^{-1})\) 并在相关集合上一致成立，则它把参数定位到局部图中。

全局到局部归约

定理. 若预定位估计一致有效，且在 \(\tilde\theta_n\) 附近有局部 LAN 近似，则全局估计问题可归约为局部高斯决策问题。

随机中心证明

证明. 在 \(\tilde\theta_n\) 落入小邻域的事件上，似然可由围绕随机中心的二次型近似；该事件的补集概率趋零。

随机信息

勒卡姆的全局理论允许观测信息或随机信息矩阵，这对混合正态极限和异方差局部近似很重要。
