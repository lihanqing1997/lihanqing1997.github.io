Holonomy and parallel transport records the objects, main results, proof mechanisms, and boundary cases needed for the differential geometry note.

Definition

Definition. Parallel transport moves vectors along curves using a connection. Holonomy records the transformations obtained by transporting around loops.

Proposition

Proposition. The curvature of a connection is the infinitesimal source of holonomy; flat connections have locally path-independent parallel transport.

Proof

Proof. Compute parallel transport around small coordinate rectangles and compare the result with the identity. The leading second-order term is the curvature operator.

Remark

Remark. Holonomy converts local differential equations along paths into global geometric structure. It is a compact way to summarize connection geometry.
