12. Levi-Civita connection

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/differential-geometry/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/differential-geometry/riemannian-metrics/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/differential-geometry/geodesics-and-exponential-map/" %}

Levi-Civita connection records the objects, main results, proof mechanisms, and boundary cases needed for the differential geometry note.

Definition

Definition. A connection differentiates vector fields along vector fields. The Levi-Civita connection is the unique connection that is torsion-free and compatible with a Riemannian metric.

Fundamental theorem of Riemannian geometry

Theorem. For every Riemannian metric \(g\), there exists a unique connection \(\nabla\) satisfying \(\nabla g=0\) and \(\nabla_XY-\nabla_YX=[X,Y]\).

Proof

Proof. The Koszul formula expresses \(2g(\nabla_XY,Z)\) in terms of derivatives of \(g\) and Lie brackets. It proves uniqueness and defines the connection.

Remark

Remark. The Levi-Civita connection is the device that makes acceleration, geodesics, and curvature intrinsic.
