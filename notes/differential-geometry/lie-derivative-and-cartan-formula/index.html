Lie derivative and Cartan formula records the objects, main results, proof mechanisms, and boundary cases needed for the differential geometry note.

Definition

Definition. The Lie derivative measures infinitesimal change of tensor fields along a vector field flow. For forms it interacts with exterior derivative and contraction.

Cartan magic formula

Theorem. For a vector field \(X\), the Lie derivative on forms satisfies \(\mathcal L_X=i_X\mathrm{d}+\mathrm{d}i_X\).

Proof

Proof. Differentiate the pullback of a form by the flow of \(X\). Check the formula on functions and one-forms, then extend using the graded derivation rules.

Remark

Remark. Cartan formula is a compact expression of how dynamics, differentiation, and algebra of forms fit together.
