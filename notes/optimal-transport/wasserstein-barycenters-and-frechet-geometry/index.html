15. Wasserstein barycenters and Fréchet geometry

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/optimal-transport/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/optimal-transport/benamou-brenier-formula/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/optimal-transport/displacement-convexity/" %}

A Wasserstein barycenter is an average of distributions formed in transport geometry rather than by mixing their densities. It is the Fréchet mean of probability measures and links geodesics, multi-marginal transport, convexity, and statistical estimation.

The Fréchet functional

Definition. Given \(\mu_1,\ldots,\mu_N\in\mathcal P_2(\mathbb R^d)\) and weights \(\lambda_i>0\), \(\sum_i\lambda_i=1\), define \[ \mathcal V(\nu)=\frac12\sum_{i=1}^N\lambda_iW_2^2(\nu,\mu_i). \] Any minimizer \(\bar\mu\) of \(\mathcal V\) is a Wasserstein barycenter.

Existence and uniqueness. A barycenter always exists. If at least one \(\mu_i\) is absolutely continuous, then the barycenter is unique; under standard hypotheses it is also absolutely continuous.

Existence. Let \((\nu_n)\) be minimizing. For any fixed \(i\), \[ W_2(\nu_n,\delta_0)\le W_2(\nu_n,\mu_i)+W_2(\mu_i,\delta_0), \] so bounded objective gives a uniform second-moment bound. Tightness and moment control yield a \(W_2\)-convergent subsequence. Lower semicontinuity of each squared distance makes its limit a minimizer.

Uniqueness mechanism. Suppose \(\mu_j\) is absolutely continuous. Interpolate linearly between two candidate barycenters through a generalized geodesic with base \(\mu_j\), obtained by gluing their unique optimal maps from \(\mu_j\). The term \(W_2^2(\cdot,\mu_j)\) is strictly convex along this interpolation unless the endpoints coincide, while the remaining terms are convex. Two distinct minimizers would therefore produce a midpoint with strictly smaller objective.

First-order optimality

Barycenter equation. Suppose the barycenter \(\bar\mu\) is absolutely continuous and let \(T_i\) be the Brenier map from \(\bar\mu\) to \(\mu_i\). Then \[ \sum_{i=1}^N\lambda_iT_i(x)=x \qquad\text{for \(\bar\mu\)-almost every \(x\)}. \]

Proof. Perturb \(\bar\mu\) by \(S_\varepsilon=\operatorname{id}+\varepsilon\xi\), with smooth compactly supported \(\xi\). Coupling \(S_\varepsilon(x)\) with \(T_i(x)\) gives \[ W_2^2(S_{\varepsilon\#}\bar\mu,\mu_i) \le\int|x+\varepsilon\xi(x)-T_i(x)|^2\,\mathrm d\bar\mu(x). \] Differentiate the upper bound at \(\varepsilon=0\). Minimality for positive and negative \(\varepsilon\) gives \[ \int \xi(x)\cdot\sum_i\lambda_i(x-T_i(x))\,\mathrm d\bar\mu(x)=0 \] for every \(\xi\). Hence the vector field in parentheses vanishes almost surely.

Multi-marginal formulation

Multi-marginal representation. Let \[ \bar x_\lambda=\sum_i\lambda_ix_i. \] Then \[ \inf_{\nu}\sum_i\lambda_iW_2^2(\nu,\mu_i) = \inf_{\gamma\in\Pi(\mu_1,\ldots,\mu_N)} \int\sum_i\lambda_i|x_i-\bar x_\lambda|^2\,\mathrm d\gamma. \] If \(\gamma^\star\) solves the right side, then \((\bar x_\lambda)_\#\gamma^\star\) is a barycenter.

Proof. Given a candidate \(\nu\), glue optimal couplings of \(\nu\) with all \(\mu_i\) conditionally on the barycenter variable \(z\). For every \(x_1,\ldots,x_N,z\), \[ \sum_i\lambda_i|x_i-z|^2 = \sum_i\lambda_i|x_i-\bar x_\lambda|^2+|z-\bar x_\lambda|^2. \] The minimum over \(z\) is attained at \(\bar x_\lambda\), giving one inequality. Conversely, any multi-marginal coupling and \(z=\bar x_\lambda\) induce couplings of its law with each \(\mu_i\), giving the other inequality.

One-dimensional barycenters

Quantile formula. On \(\mathbb R\), the unique \(W_2\)-barycenter satisfies \[ F_{\bar\mu}^{-1}(u)=\sum_{i=1}^N\lambda_iF_{\mu_i}^{-1}(u) \quad\text{for almost every }u\in(0,1). \]

Proof. By the one-dimensional formula, \[ \mathcal V(\nu)=\frac12\int_0^1 \sum_i\lambda_i|F_\nu^{-1}(u)-F_{\mu_i}^{-1}(u)|^2\,\mathrm du. \] Pointwise minimization in \(F_\nu^{-1}(u)\) gives the weighted average. It remains nondecreasing because it is a positive combination of nondecreasing functions, so it is a valid quantile.

Gaussian barycenters

Gaussian barycenter. The barycenter of nondegenerate Gaussian laws \(N(m_i,\Sigma_i)\) is \(N(\bar m,\bar\Sigma)\), where \[ \bar m=\sum_i\lambda_im_i \] and \(\bar\Sigma\) is the unique positive-definite solution of \[ \bar\Sigma = \sum_i\lambda_i (\bar\Sigma^{1/2}\Sigma_i\bar\Sigma^{1/2})^{1/2}. \]

The equation is the matrix form of \(\sum_i\lambda_iT_i(x)=x\). If the covariance matrices commute, it simplifies to \[ \bar\Sigma^{1/2}=\sum_i\lambda_i\Sigma_i^{1/2}. \]

Stability of barycenters

Stability. Suppose \(\mu_{i,n}\to\mu_i\) in \(W_2\) and let \(\bar\mu_n\) be corresponding barycenters with fixed weights. Then \((\bar\mu_n)\) is relatively compact in \(W_2\), and every limit point is a barycenter of \((\mu_i)\). If the limiting barycenter is unique, then \(\bar\mu_n\to\bar\mu\) in \(W_2\).

Proof. Compare \(\mathcal V_n(\bar\mu_n)\) with \(\mathcal V_n(\mu_{1,n})\) to obtain a uniform second-moment bound. Compactness follows. The triangle inequality implies locally uniform convergence of \(W_2^2(\nu,\mu_{i,n})\) on moment-bounded sets, so the liminf inequality and recovery at any fixed \(\nu\) identify each limit as a minimizer. Uniqueness removes subsequences.

Population Fréchet means

If \(\mathsf Q\) is a probability distribution on \(\mathcal P_2(\mathbb R^d)\), a population barycenter minimizes \[ \nu\longmapsto\frac12\int W_2^2(\nu,\mu)\,\mathrm d\mathsf Q(\mu). \] The empirical barycenter replaces \(\mathsf Q\) by sampled distributions. Existence, uniqueness, consistency, and limit distributions are therefore Fréchet-mean questions in a nonlinear metric space; page 21 returns to their statistical theory.

15. Barycentres de Wasserstein et géométrie de Fréchet

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/optimal-transport/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/optimal-transport/benamou-brenier-formula/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/optimal-transport/displacement-convexity/" %}

Un barycentre de Wasserstein est une moyenne de lois construite dans la géométrie du transport plutôt que par mélange des densités. C'est la moyenne de Fréchet des mesures de probabilité.

Fonctionnelle de Fréchet

Définition. Pour \(\mu_1,\ldots,\mu_N\in\mathcal P_2(\mathbb R^d)\) et \(\lambda_i>0\), \(\sum_i\lambda_i=1\), \[ \mathcal V(\nu)=\frac12\sum_i\lambda_iW_2^2(\nu,\mu_i). \] Tout minimiseur \(\bar\mu\) est un barycentre de Wasserstein.

Existence et unicité. Un barycentre existe toujours. Si au moins une \(\mu_i\) est absolument continue, il est unique et, sous les hypothèses usuelles, absolument continu.

Existence. Une suite minimisante a un second moment uniformément borné, car \[ W_2(\nu_n,\delta_0)\le W_2(\nu_n,\mu_i)+W_2(\mu_i,\delta_0). \] Compacité et semi-continuité fournissent un minimiseur.

Mécanisme d'unicité. Si \(\mu_j\) est absolument continue, interpolons deux minimiseurs le long d'une géodésique généralisée de base \(\mu_j\), en recollant leurs applications optimales uniques depuis \(\mu_j\). Le terme correspondant à \(\mu_j\) est strictement convexe sauf si les deux extrémités coïncident, les autres sont convexes. Deux minimiseurs distincts donneraient donc un milieu de valeur plus faible.

Condition du premier ordre

Équation du barycentre. Si \(\bar\mu\) est absolument continue et \(T_i\) est l'application de Brenier de \(\bar\mu\) vers \(\mu_i\), alors \[ \sum_i\lambda_iT_i(x)=x \quad\text{pour \(\bar\mu\)-presque tout \(x\)}. \]

Preuve. Perturbons \(\bar\mu\) par \(S_\varepsilon=\operatorname{id}+\varepsilon\xi\). Le couplage de \(S_\varepsilon(x)\) avec \(T_i(x)\) donne une borne supérieure sur chaque distance. La dérivée en zéro, dans les deux signes de \(\varepsilon\), fournit \[ \int\xi(x)\cdot\sum_i\lambda_i(x-T_i(x))\,\mathrm d\bar\mu(x)=0. \] Comme \(\xi\) est arbitraire, le champ s'annule presque partout.

Formulation multi-marginale

Représentation multi-marginale. Avec \(\bar x_\lambda=\sum_i\lambda_ix_i\), \[ \inf_\nu\sum_i\lambda_iW_2^2(\nu,\mu_i) = \inf_{\gamma\in\Pi(\mu_1,\ldots,\mu_N)} \int\sum_i\lambda_i|x_i-\bar x_\lambda|^2\,\mathrm d\gamma. \] Si \(\gamma^\star\) est optimal, \((\bar x_\lambda)_\#\gamma^\star\) est un barycentre.

Preuve. Recolons les couplages optimaux d'un candidat \(\nu\) avec les \(\mu_i\). L'identité \[ \sum_i\lambda_i|x_i-z|^2 =\sum_i\lambda_i|x_i-\bar x_\lambda|^2+|z-\bar x_\lambda|^2 \] montre que le meilleur \(z\) est \(\bar x_\lambda\), et donne les deux inégalités en passant d'une formulation à l'autre.

Barycentres unidimensionnels

Formule quantile. \[ F_{\bar\mu}^{-1}(u)=\sum_i\lambda_iF_{\mu_i}^{-1}(u). \]

Preuve. La formule unidimensionnelle de \(W_2\) transforme l'objectif en une intégrale de problèmes quadratiques ponctuels. Leur minimiseur est la moyenne pondérée des quantiles, encore croissante.

Barycentres gaussiens

Barycentre gaussien. Le barycentre des \(N(m_i,\Sigma_i)\) est \(N(\bar m,\bar\Sigma)\), où \[ \bar m=\sum_i\lambda_im_i,\qquad \bar\Sigma=\sum_i\lambda_i(\bar\Sigma^{1/2}\Sigma_i\bar\Sigma^{1/2})^{1/2}. \] Si les covariances commutent, \(\bar\Sigma^{1/2}=\sum_i\lambda_i\Sigma_i^{1/2}\).

Stabilité

Stabilité. Si \(\mu_{i,n}\to\mu_i\) dans \(W_2\), les barycentres correspondants sont relativement compacts, et toute limite est un barycentre des \(\mu_i\). Si celui-ci est unique, la suite entière converge.

Preuve. La comparaison avec \(\mu_{1,n}\) donne une borne uniforme de second moment. Sur les ensembles à moments bornés, les distances carrées vers \(\mu_{i,n}\) convergent localement uniformément vers celles vers \(\mu_i\). Les inégalités liminf et de récupération identifient les limites comme minimiseurs; l'unicité supprime les sous-suites.

Moyennes de Fréchet de population

Pour une loi \(\mathsf Q\) sur \(\mathcal P_2\), un barycentre de population minimise \[ \nu\longmapsto\frac12\int W_2^2(\nu,\mu)\,\mathrm d\mathsf Q(\mu). \] Le barycentre empirique remplace \(\mathsf Q\) par les distributions observées. La page 21 étudiera cohérence et lois limites.

15. 瓦瑟斯坦重心与弗雷歇几何

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/optimal-transport/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/optimal-transport/benamou-brenier-formula/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/optimal-transport/displacement-convexity/" %}

瓦瑟斯坦重心是在传输几何中形成的分布平均，而不是简单混合密度。它是概率测度的弗雷歇均值，并把测地线、多边缘传输、凸性和统计估计联系起来。

弗雷歇泛函

定义. 给定 \(\mu_1,\ldots,\mu_N\in\mathcal P_2(\mathbb R^d)\) 以及 \(\lambda_i>0\)、\(\sum_i\lambda_i=1\)，定义 \[ \mathcal V(\nu)=\frac12\sum_i\lambda_iW_2^2(\nu,\mu_i). \] \(\mathcal V\) 的任一极小化解 \(\bar\mu\) 称为瓦瑟斯坦重心。

存在性与唯一性. 瓦瑟斯坦重心总是存在。若至少一个 \(\mu_i\) 绝对连续，则重心唯一；在标准条件下它也绝对连续。

存在性. 对极小化序列 \((\nu_n)\)， \[ W_2(\nu_n,\delta_0)\le W_2(\nu_n,\mu_i)+W_2(\mu_i,\delta_0). \] 有界目标值给出一致二阶矩界。紧性和矩控制提供 \(W_2\)-收敛子列，各平方距离的下半连续性说明极限为极小化解。

唯一性机制. 若 \(\mu_j\) 绝对连续，通过从 \(\mu_j\) 出发的唯一最优映射，把两个候选重心沿以 \(\mu_j\) 为基的广义测地线插值。与 \(\mu_j\) 对应的平方距离项严格凸，除非两个端点相同；其余项凸。因此两个不同极小化解的中点会有更小目标值，矛盾。

一阶最优性条件

重心方程. 若重心 \(\bar\mu\) 绝对连续，且 \(T_i\) 为从 \(\bar\mu\) 到 \(\mu_i\) 的布雷尼耶映射，则 \[ \sum_i\lambda_iT_i(x)=x \qquad\text{对 \(\bar\mu\)-几乎处处的 \(x\)}. \]

证明. 用 \(S_\varepsilon=\operatorname{id}+\varepsilon\xi\) 扰动 \(\bar\mu\)。把 \(S_\varepsilon(x)\) 与 \(T_i(x)\) 耦合，可得每个平方距离的上界。分别从正负方向在 \(\varepsilon=0\) 求导，极小性给出 \[ \int \xi(x)\cdot\sum_i\lambda_i(x-T_i(x))\,\mathrm d\bar\mu(x)=0. \] 由于 \(\xi\) 任意，括号中的向量场几乎处处为零。

多边缘表述

多边缘表示. 令 \(\bar x_\lambda=\sum_i\lambda_ix_i\)，则 \[ \inf_\nu\sum_i\lambda_iW_2^2(\nu,\mu_i) = \inf_{\gamma\in\Pi(\mu_1,\ldots,\mu_N)} \int\sum_i\lambda_i|x_i-\bar x_\lambda|^2\,\mathrm d\gamma. \] 若 \(\gamma^\star\) 解右侧问题，则 \((\bar x_\lambda)_\#\gamma^\star\) 是重心。

证明. 给定候选 \(\nu\)，以重心变量 \(z\) 为条件，拼接它与所有 \(\mu_i\) 的最优耦合。恒等式 \[ \sum_i\lambda_i|x_i-z|^2 =\sum_i\lambda_i|x_i-\bar x_\lambda|^2+|z-\bar x_\lambda|^2 \] 表明对固定 \(x_i\)，最优 \(z\) 是 \(\bar x_\lambda\)。从两种表述分别构造另一种表述的可行解，即得两个方向的不等式。

一维重心

分位数公式. \[ F_{\bar\mu}^{-1}(u)=\sum_i\lambda_iF_{\mu_i}^{-1}(u) \quad\text{对几乎每个 }u\in(0,1). \]

证明. 一维 \(W_2\) 公式把目标函数写成关于 \(F_\nu^{-1}(u)\) 的逐点二次极小化问题。其解是分位数的加权平均；正权重的单调函数组合仍单调，因此确为合法分位数函数。

高斯重心

高斯重心. 非退化高斯分布 \(N(m_i,\Sigma_i)\) 的重心为 \(N(\bar m,\bar\Sigma)\)，其中 \[ \bar m=\sum_i\lambda_im_i,\qquad \bar\Sigma=\sum_i\lambda_i(\bar\Sigma^{1/2}\Sigma_i\bar\Sigma^{1/2})^{1/2}. \] 若协方差矩阵两两可交换，则 \(\bar\Sigma^{1/2}=\sum_i\lambda_i\Sigma_i^{1/2}\)。

重心稳定性

稳定性. 若 \(\mu_{i,n}\to\mu_i\) 于 \(W_2\)，对应重心 \(\bar\mu_n\) 在 \(W_2\) 中相对紧，且每个极限点都是 \((\mu_i)\) 的重心。若极限重心唯一，则 \(\bar\mu_n\to\bar\mu\)。

证明. 将 \(\bar\mu_n\) 的目标值与 \(\mu_{1,n}\) 比较，得到一致二阶矩界。矩有界集上，\(W_2^2(\nu,\mu_{i,n})\) 局部一致收敛到 \(W_2^2(\nu,\mu_i)\)。下极限不等式与任意固定 \(\nu\) 的恢复不等式识别出每个极限都是极小化解；唯一性去除对子列的依赖。

总体弗雷歇均值

若 \(\mathsf Q\) 是 \(\mathcal P_2(\mathbb R^d)\) 上的概率分布，总体重心最小化 \[ \nu\longmapsto\frac12\int W_2^2(\nu,\mu)\,\mathrm d\mathsf Q(\mu). \] 经验重心以抽样分布替代 \(\mathsf Q\)。其存在性、唯一性、相合性与极限分布都是非线性度量空间中的弗雷歇均值问题，第 21 页将回到其统计理论。
