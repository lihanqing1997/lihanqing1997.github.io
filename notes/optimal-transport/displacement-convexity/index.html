Displacement convexity records the objects, main results, proof mechanisms, and boundary cases needed for the optimal transport note.

Definition

Definition. A functional on probability measures is displacement convex if it is convex along Wasserstein geodesics.

Proposition

Proposition. Entropy-type functionals and interaction energies satisfy displacement convexity under structural convexity assumptions on the integrand or kernel.

Proof

Proof. Write the functional along a geodesic induced by a transport map and use change-of-variables plus determinant or pointwise convexity inequalities.

Remark

Remark. Displacement convexity is the right convexity notion for variational problems in Wasserstein space.
