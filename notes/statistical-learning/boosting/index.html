Boosting records the objects, main results, proof mechanisms, and boundary cases needed for the statistical learning note.

Definition

Definition. Boosting combines weak learners into a strong predictor by reweighting data or fitting additive models.

Proposition

Proposition. Exponential-loss boosting can be interpreted as stagewise additive optimization, and margins explain its classification behavior.

Proof

Proof. At each step choose a weak learner correlated with the negative gradient of the current loss. The additive update decreases empirical surrogate loss under weak learning conditions.

Remark

Remark. Boosting shows that optimization dynamics can change statistical complexity in subtle ways.
