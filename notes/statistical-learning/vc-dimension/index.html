6. VC dimension

VC dimension records the objects, main results, proof mechanisms, and boundary cases needed for the statistical learning note.

Definition

Definition. The VC dimension of a binary class is the largest size of a set shattered by the class. Shattering means all binary labelings are realized.

Proposition

Proposition. A class with finite VC dimension has uniform convergence of empirical classification risk to population risk at rates controlled by the VC dimension.

Proof

Proof. Sauer-Shelah bounds the growth function by a polynomial in sample size when VC dimension is finite. Combine this counting bound with concentration over finite label patterns.

Remark

Remark. VC dimension measures combinatorial richness, not parameter count alone. It is central for distribution-free classification theory.
