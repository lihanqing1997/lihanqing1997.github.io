4. Data processing and sufficiency

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/information-theory/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/information-theory/mutual-information-conditional-mutual-information/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/information-theory/fano-inequality-inverse-bounds/" %}

The data-processing principle says that stochastic postprocessing cannot create information. Equality is the mathematical form of lossless reduction.

Markov kernels

Definition. A channel or Markov kernel \(K\) sends an input law \(P\) to the output law \(PK\). A Markov chain \(U\to X\to Y\) means the conditional law of \(Y\) given \((U,X)\) depends only on \(X\).

Data-processing inequality

Theorem. If \(U\to X\to Y\), then \[ I(U;Y)\le I(U;X). \] Equivalently, for every Markov kernel \(K\), \[ D(PK\|QK)\le D(P\|Q). \]

Proof by conditional mutual information

Proof. The chain rule gives \[ I(U;X,Y)=I(U;X)+I(U;Y\mid X)=I(U;Y)+I(U;X\mid Y). \] The Markov condition makes \(I(U;Y\mid X)=0\). Hence \(I(U;X)-I(U;Y)=I(U;X\mid Y)\ge0\).

Sufficiency as equality

Proposition. For a family \(\{P_\theta\}\), a statistic \(T(X)\) is information-lossless for pairwise discrimination if \[ D(P_\theta\|P_{\theta'})=D(P_\theta T^{-1}\|P_{\theta'}T^{-1}) \] for all \(\theta,\theta'\). In dominated finite models, this is equivalent to the usual factorization form of sufficiency.

4. 数据处理与充分性

本页整理数据处理与充分性的主要定义、定理与证明机制。

定义

定义. 表述. A 信道 or 马尔可夫核 \(K\) sends an input law \(P\) to the output law \(PK\). A 马尔可夫链 \(U\to X\to Y\) means the conditional law of \(Y\) given \((U,X)\) depends only on \(X\).

定理

定理. 表述. If \(U\to X\to Y\), then \[ I(U;Y)\le I(U;X). \] Equivalently, for every 马尔可夫核 \(K\), \[ D(PK\|QK)\le D(P\|Q). \]

证明

证明. 证明思路. The chain rule gives \[ I(U;X,Y)=I(U;X)+I(U;Y\mid X)=I(U;Y)+I(U;X\mid Y). \] The 马尔可夫 condition makes \(I(U;Y\mid X)=0\). Hence \(I(U;X)-I(U;Y)=I(U;X\mid Y)\ge0\).

命题

命题. 表述. For a family \(\{P_\theta\}\), a statistic \(T(X)\) is information-lossless for pairwise discrimination if \[ D(P_\theta\|P_{\theta'})=D(P_\theta T^{-1}\|P_{\theta'}T^{-1}) \] for all \(\theta,\theta'\). In dominated finite models, this is equivalent to the usual 因子分解 form of sufficiency.
