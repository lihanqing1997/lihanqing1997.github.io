12. Finite-sample optimality

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/confidence-sets-pivots/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/linear-and-generalized-linear-models/" %}

This page records the classical exact optimality results that belong naturally to inference. Broader risk, admissibility, and minimax theory is treated in the statistical decision theory note.

Definition. An unbiased estimator \(T\) of \(g(\theta)\) satisfies \(E_\theta T=g(\theta)\). It is UMVUE if it has minimum variance among all unbiased estimators for every \(\theta\).

Lehmann-Scheffé theorem. If \(T\) is complete sufficient for \(\theta\), and \(U\) is any unbiased estimator of \(g(\theta)\), then \(E(U\mid T)\) is the unique UMVUE of \(g(\theta)\).

Proof. Rao-Blackwellization gives \(E_\theta\{E(U\mid T)\}=g(\theta)\) and \[ \operatorname{Var}_\theta(E(U\mid T))\le\operatorname{Var}_\theta(U). \] Thus \(E(U\mid T)\) is at least as good as every unbiased estimator after conditioning on \(T\). If \(V\) is another unbiased function of \(T\), then \(E_\theta(V-E(U\mid T))=0\) for all \(\theta\). Completeness implies \(V=E(U\mid T)\) a.s.

UMP and UMPU tests

Uniformly most powerful tests exist in special settings, especially one-parameter exponential families with monotone likelihood ratio. For two-sided problems, unbiasedness restrictions lead to UMPU tests. These exact results are elegant, but many modern problems rely instead on asymptotic likelihood theory.

12. Optimalité en échantillon fini

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/confidence-sets-pivots/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/linear-and-generalized-linear-models/" %}

Cette page rassemble les résultats classiques d’optimalité exacte qui relèvent directement de l’inférence. La théorie générale du risque, de l’admissibilité et du minimax appartient à la théorie de la décision statistique.

Définition. Un estimateur sans biais \(T\) de \(g(\theta)\) vérifie \(E_\theta T=g(\theta)\). Il est UMVUE s’il a variance minimale parmi tous les estimateurs sans biais, pour tout \(\theta\).

Théorème de Lehmann-Scheffé. Si \(T\) est complète suffisante pour \(\theta\), et si \(U\) est sans biais pour \(g(\theta)\), alors \(E(U\mid T)\) est l’unique UMVUE de \(g(\theta)\).

Preuve. Le théorème de Rao-Blackwell donne \(E_\theta E(U\mid T)=g(\theta)\) et \[ \operatorname{Var}_\theta(E(U\mid T))\le\operatorname{Var}_\theta(U). \] Si \(V\) est un autre estimateur sans biais fonction de \(T\), alors \(E_\theta(V-E(U\mid T))=0\) pour tout \(\theta\). La complétude force \(V=E(U\mid T)\) p.s.

UMP et UMPU

Les tests uniformément les plus puissants existent dans des cadres spéciaux, notamment les familles exponentielles à rapport de vraisemblance monotone. Les problèmes bilatéraux conduisent souvent aux tests UMPU.

12. 有限样本最优性

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/confidence-sets-pivots/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/linear-and-generalized-linear-models/" %}

本页记录自然属于统计推断的经典有限样本最优性结果。更一般的风险、admissibility 和极小极大理论放在 statistical decision theory 笔记中。

定义. 若 \(E_\theta T=g(\theta)\)，则 \(T\) 是 \(g(\theta)\) 的无偏估计量。若它在所有无偏估计量中对每个 \(\theta\) 都有最小方差，则称为 UMVUE。

Lehmann-Scheffé 定理. 若 \(T\) 对 \(\theta\) 完备充分，且 \(U\) 是 \(g(\theta)\) 的任意无偏估计，则 \(E(U\mid T)\) 是 \(g(\theta)\) 的唯一 UMVUE。

证明. 拉奥-布莱克韦尔化给出 \(E_\theta\{E(U\mid T)\}=g(\theta)\)，并且 \[ \operatorname{Var}_\theta(E(U\mid T))\le\operatorname{Var}_\theta(U). \] 若 \(V\) 是另一个作为 \(T\) 函数的无偏估计量，则 \(E_\theta(V-E(U\mid T))=0\) 对所有 \(\theta\) 成立。完备性推出 \(V=E(U\mid T)\) a.s.

UMP 与 UMPU 检验

一致最强检验只在特殊结构中存在，特别是一参数指数族和单调似然比情形。双侧问题通常需要无偏性限制，从而得到 UMPU 检验。现代复杂问题更多依赖渐近似然理论。
