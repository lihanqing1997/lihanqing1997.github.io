Local asymptotic normality and contiguity

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/censored-truncated-data/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/efficiency-theory/" %}

LAN describes the local geometry of a statistical experiment near a fixed parameter. It is the bridge between likelihood expansions and Gaussian limit experiments.

Definition. The experiments \(P_\theta^{(n)}\) are LAN at \(\theta_0\) with information \(I\) if, for each fixed \(h\), \[ \log\frac{dP_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}^{(n)}}{dP_{\theta_0}^{(n)}} =h^\top\Delta_n-\frac12h^\top Ih+o_{P_{\theta_0}}(1), \qquad \Delta_n\rightsquigarrow N(0,I). \]

Le Cam first lemma. If likelihood ratios \(dQ_n/dP_n\) converge in distribution under \(P_n\) to a positive random variable with mean \(1\), then \(Q_n\) is contiguous with respect to \(P_n\).

Proof. If \(P_n(A_n)\to0\), then for any \(M\), \[ Q_n(A_n)=E_{P_n}\left[1_{A_n}\frac{dQ_n}{dP_n}\right] \le M P_n(A_n)+E_{P_n}\left[\frac{dQ_n}{dP_n}1_{\{dQ_n/dP_n\gt M\}}\right]. \] The first term vanishes. Tightness plus convergence to a mean-one limit makes the tail term small for large \(M\).

Meaning

Under LAN, local alternatives \(\theta_0+h/\sqrt n\) cannot be separated perfectly from \(\theta_0\); they shift the Gaussian central sequence by \(Ih\). This is the basis for local power and efficiency calculations.

Normalité locale asymptotique et contiguïté

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/censored-truncated-data/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/efficiency-theory/" %}

La propriété LAN décrit la géométrie locale d’une expérience statistique près d’un paramètre fixé. Elle relie les développements de vraisemblance aux expériences gaussiennes limites.

Définition. Les expériences \(P_\theta^{(n)}\) sont LAN en \(\theta_0\), d’information \(I\), si pour tout \(h\) fixé, \[ \log\frac{dP_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}^{(n)}}{dP_{\theta_0}^{(n)}} =h^\top\Delta_n-\frac12h^\top Ih+o_{P_{\theta_0}}(1), \qquad \Delta_n\rightsquigarrow N(0,I). \]

Premier lemme de Le Cam. Si les rapports \(dQ_n/dP_n\) convergent sous \(P_n\) vers une variable positive de moyenne \(1\), alors \(Q_n\) est contiguë à \(P_n\).

Preuve. Si \(P_n(A_n)\to0\), alors \[ Q_n(A_n)\le M P_n(A_n)+E_{P_n}\left[\frac{dQ_n}{dP_n}1_{\{dQ_n/dP_n\gt M\}}\right]. \] Le premier terme disparaît, et le second devient petit pour \(M\) grand par convergence vers une limite intégrable de moyenne \(1\).

Sens statistique

Sous LAN, les alternatives \(\theta_0+h/\sqrt n\) déplacent la suite centrale gaussienne de \(Ih\), ce qui fonde les calculs de puissance locale et d’efficacité.

局部渐近正态性与 contiguity

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/censored-truncated-data/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/efficiency-theory/" %}

LAN 描述固定参数附近统计实验的局部几何。它连接 likelihood expansion 与 Gaussian limit experiment。

定义. 若对每个固定 \(h\)， \[ \log\frac{dP_{\theta_0+h/\sqrt n}^{(n)}}{dP_{\theta_0}^{(n)}} =h^\top\Delta_n-\frac12h^\top Ih+o_{P_{\theta_0}}(1), \qquad \Delta_n\rightsquigarrow N(0,I), \] 则称实验 \(P_\theta^{(n)}\) 在 \(\theta_0\) 处 LAN，信息为 \(I\)。

Le Cam 第一引理. 若 likelihood ratio \(dQ_n/dP_n\) 在 \(P_n\) 下依分布收敛到均值为 \(1\) 的正随机变量，则 \(Q_n\) 关于 \(P_n\) contiguous。

证明. 若 \(P_n(A_n)\to0\)，则对任意 \(M\)， \[ Q_n(A_n)\le M P_n(A_n)+E_{P_n}\left[\frac{dQ_n}{dP_n}1_{\{dQ_n/dP_n\gt M\}}\right]. \] 第一项趋于 \(0\)。由 likelihood ratio 收敛到均值为 \(1\) 的可积极限，第二项可通过取大 \(M\) 变小。

统计意义

LAN 下，局部备择 \(\theta_0+h/\sqrt n\) 不能与 \(\theta_0\) 完全分离；它们只把 Gaussian central sequence 平移 \(Ih\)。这是局部 power 和效率计算的基础。
