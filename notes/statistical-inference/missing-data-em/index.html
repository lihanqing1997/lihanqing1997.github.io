Missing data and EM algorithm

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/monte-carlo-for-inference/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/censored-truncated-data/" %}

Missing data change the likelihood because the observed data are a coarsening of complete data. EM turns incomplete-data likelihood maximization into repeated conditional expectation and maximization steps.

Definition. Let \(Y=(Y_{\mathrm{obs}},Y_{\mathrm{mis}})\) be complete data. The observed likelihood is \[ L_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta)=\int p_\theta(Y_{\mathrm{obs}},y_{\mathrm{mis}})\,dy_{\mathrm{mis}}. \] EM defines \[ Q(\theta\mid\theta^{(t)})=E_{\theta^{(t)}}\{\log p_\theta(Y)\mid Y_{\mathrm{obs}}\}. \]

EM monotonicity. If \(\theta^{(t+1)}\) maximizes \(Q(\theta\mid\theta^{(t)})\), then \(\ell_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta^{(t+1)})\ge \ell_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta^{(t)})\).

Proof. Write the observed log-likelihood as \[ \ell_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta)=Q(\theta\mid\theta^{(t)})-\operatorname{KL}\{p_{\theta^{(t)}}(Y_{\mathrm{mis}}\mid Y_{\mathrm{obs}}),p_\theta(Y_{\mathrm{mis}}\mid Y_{\mathrm{obs}})\}+C. \] The KL term is nonnegative and equals zero at \(\theta=\theta^{(t)}\). Increasing \(Q\) therefore cannot decrease \(\ell_{\mathrm{obs}}\).

Missingness assumptions

MCAR, MAR, and MNAR describe how missingness depends on data. Ignorability requires assumptions about both the missingness mechanism and parameter distinctness; otherwise the missingness process must be modeled.

Données manquantes et algorithme EM

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/monte-carlo-for-inference/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/censored-truncated-data/" %}

Les données manquantes modifient la vraisemblance car les données observées sont une version incomplète des données complètes. EM maximise la vraisemblance observée par espérance conditionnelle et maximisation répétées.

Définition. Soit \(Y=(Y_{\mathrm{obs}},Y_{\mathrm{mis}})\). La vraisemblance observée est \[ L_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta)=\int p_\theta(Y_{\mathrm{obs}},y_{\mathrm{mis}})\,dy_{\mathrm{mis}}. \] EM définit \[ Q(\theta\mid\theta^{(t)})=E_{\theta^{(t)}}\{\log p_\theta(Y)\mid Y_{\mathrm{obs}}\}. \]

Monotonie EM. Si \(\theta^{(t+1)}\) maximise \(Q(\theta\mid\theta^{(t)})\), alors \(\ell_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta^{(t+1)})\ge \ell_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta^{(t)})\).

Preuve. On décompose \[ \ell_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta)=Q(\theta\mid\theta^{(t)})-\operatorname{KL}\{p_{\theta^{(t)}}(Y_{\mathrm{mis}}\mid Y_{\mathrm{obs}}),p_\theta(Y_{\mathrm{mis}}\mid Y_{\mathrm{obs}})\}+C. \] Le terme KL est positif et nul en \(\theta^{(t)}\). Augmenter \(Q\) ne peut donc pas diminuer la vraisemblance observée.

Mécanismes de manque

MCAR, MAR et MNAR décrivent la dépendance du manque aux données. L’ignorabilité nécessite des hypothèses sur le mécanisme et sur la séparation des paramètres.

缺失数据与 EM 算法

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/monte-carlo-for-inference/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/censored-truncated-data/" %}

缺失数据改变似然，因为观测数据是完整数据的 coarsening。EM 算法通过反复做条件期望和最大化来处理 incomplete-data likelihood。

定义. 设完整数据 \(Y=(Y_{\mathrm{obs}},Y_{\mathrm{mis}})\)。观测似然为 \[ L_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta)=\int p_\theta(Y_{\mathrm{obs}},y_{\mathrm{mis}})\,dy_{\mathrm{mis}}. \] EM 定义 \[ Q(\theta\mid\theta^{(t)})=E_{\theta^{(t)}}\{\log p_\theta(Y)\mid Y_{\mathrm{obs}}\}. \]

EM 单调性. 若 \(\theta^{(t+1)}\) 最大化 \(Q(\theta\mid\theta^{(t)})\)，则 \(\ell_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta^{(t+1)})\ge \ell_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta^{(t)})\)。

证明. 可分解 \[ \ell_{\mathrm{obs}}(\theta)=Q(\theta\mid\theta^{(t)})-\operatorname{KL}\{p_{\theta^{(t)}}(Y_{\mathrm{mis}}\mid Y_{\mathrm{obs}}),p_\theta(Y_{\mathrm{mis}}\mid Y_{\mathrm{obs}})\}+C. \] KL 项非负，并在 \(\theta=\theta^{(t)}\) 为零。因此增加 \(Q\) 不会降低观测 log-likelihood。

缺失机制

MCAR、MAR、MNAR 描述缺失如何依赖数据。Ignorability 需要关于缺失机制和参数分离的假设；否则必须显式建模缺失过程。
