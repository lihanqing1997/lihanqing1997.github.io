15. Robust inference

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/misspecification-robust-covariance/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/rank-sign-permutation-inference/" %}

Robust inference studies procedures that remain stable under outliers, heavy tails, or small deviations from the ideal model.

Definition. For a statistical functional \(T(P)\), the influence function at \(P\) is \[ \operatorname{IF}(x;T,P)=\left.\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}\varepsilon}T\{(1-\varepsilon)P+\varepsilon\delta_x\}\right|_{\varepsilon=0}, \] when the derivative exists.

Asymptotic variance. If \(T\) is sufficiently smooth and \(T(P_n)-T(P)=P_n\operatorname{IF}(\cdot;T,P)+o_p(n^{-1/2})\), then \[ \sqrt n\{T(P_n)-T(P)\}\rightsquigarrow N(0,P\operatorname{IF}^2). \]

Proof. This is the asymptotic linearity theorem applied to \(\phi=\operatorname{IF}\). Smoothness gives the linear expansion; the CLT gives the normal limit.

Bounded influence

The mean has \(\operatorname{IF}(x)=x-\mu\), unbounded under large outliers. Median and Huber M-estimators have bounded or controlled influence under suitable models. Robust inference trades a little efficiency at the ideal model for much better stability nearby.

15. Inférence robuste

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/misspecification-robust-covariance/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/rank-sign-permutation-inference/" %}

L’inférence robuste étudie les procédures stables face aux valeurs aberrantes, queues lourdes ou petites déviations du modèle idéal.

Définition. Pour une fonctionnelle statistique \(T(P)\), la fonction d’influence est \[ \operatorname{IF}(x;T,P)=\left.\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}\varepsilon}T\{(1-\varepsilon)P+\varepsilon\delta_x\}\right|_{\varepsilon=0}, \] lorsque cette dérivée existe.

Variance asymptotique. Si \(T(P_n)-T(P)=P_n\operatorname{IF}(\cdot;T,P)+o_p(n^{-1/2})\), alors \[ \sqrt n\{T(P_n)-T(P)\}\rightsquigarrow N(0,P\operatorname{IF}^2). \]

Preuve. C’est le théorème de linéarité asymptotique avec \(\phi=\operatorname{IF}\). La régularité donne l’expansion linéaire; le TCL donne la limite normale.

Influence bornée

La moyenne a \(\operatorname{IF}(x)=x-\mu\), non bornée. La médiane et les M-estimateurs de Huber ont une influence bornée ou contrôlée. On échange un peu d’efficacité au modèle idéal contre plus de stabilité à proximité.

15. 稳健推断

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/misspecification-robust-covariance/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/rank-sign-permutation-inference/" %}

稳健推断研究的是：在离群值、厚尾或对理想模型的小偏离下仍保持稳定的程序。

定义. 对统计泛函 \(T(P)\)，其影响函数为 \[ \operatorname{IF}(x;T,P)=\left.\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}\varepsilon}T\{(1-\varepsilon)P+\varepsilon\delta_x\}\right|_{\varepsilon=0}, \] 前提是导数存在。

渐近方差. 若 \(T\) 足够光滑且 \[ T(P_n)-T(P)=P_n\operatorname{IF}(\cdot;T,P)+o_p(n^{-1/2}), \] 则 \[ \sqrt n\{T(P_n)-T(P)\}\rightsquigarrow N(0,P\operatorname{IF}^2). \]

证明. 这就是把渐近线性定理用于 \(\phi=\operatorname{IF}\)。光滑性给出线性展开，CLT 给出正态极限。

有界影响

均值的影响函数是 \(\operatorname{IF}(x)=x-\mu\)，对大离群值无界。中位数和 Huber M-估计量在合适模型下有有界或受控影响。稳健推断用理想模型下少量效率损失换取邻近模型下的稳定性。
