19. Bootstrap and jackknife

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/goodness-of-fit-chi-square/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/higher-order-asymptotics/" %}

Resampling estimates uncertainty by repeatedly recomputing the statistic under a data-based approximation to the sampling distribution.

Definition. The nonparametric bootstrap draws \(X_1^*,\ldots,X_n^*\) i.i.d. from the empirical distribution \(P_n\). The bootstrap statistic is \(T_n^*=T(X_1^*,\ldots,X_n^*)\), studied conditionally on the data.

Bootstrap consistency for smooth means. If \(T_n=g(\bar X_n)\), \(EX_i=\mu\), \(\operatorname{Var}(X_i)=\Sigma\), and \(g\) is differentiable at \(\mu\), then conditionally on the data, \[ \sqrt n\{T_n^*-T_n\}\rightsquigarrow N(0,\dot g_\mu\Sigma\dot g_\mu^\top) \] in probability.

Proof. Conditional on the data, \(X_i^*\) are i.i.d. from \(P_n\). The conditional mean is \(\bar X_n\), and the conditional covariance converges to \(\Sigma\). The conditional CLT gives the bootstrap limit for \(\sqrt n(\bar X_n^*-\bar X_n)\). The delta method transfers this to \(g(\bar X_n^*)-g(\bar X_n)\).

Intervals and jackknife

Bootstrap standard errors, percentile intervals, bootstrap-\(t\), BC and BCa intervals use different approximations to the distribution of the error. The jackknife recomputes leave-one-out estimates and is especially tied to influence-function approximations.

Le rééchantillonnage estime l’incertitude en recalculant la statistique sous une approximation empirique de la loi d’échantillonnage.

Définition. Le bootstrap non paramétrique tire \(X_1^*,\ldots,X_n^*\) i.i.d. de la loi empirique \(P_n\). On étudie \(T_n^*=T(X_1^*,\ldots,X_n^*)\) conditionnellement aux données.

Consistance pour moyennes lisses. Si \(T_n=g(\bar X_n)\), \(EX_i=\mu\), \(\operatorname{Var}(X_i)=\Sigma\), et \(g\) est différentiable en \(\mu\), alors conditionnellement aux données, \[ \sqrt n\{T_n^*-T_n\}\rightsquigarrow N(0,\dot g_\mu\Sigma\dot g_\mu^\top) \] en probabilité.

Preuve. Conditionnellement aux données, les \(X_i^*\) sont i.i.d. de loi \(P_n\). Leur moyenne conditionnelle est \(\bar X_n\), et leur covariance conditionnelle converge vers \(\Sigma\). Le TCL conditionnel donne la limite de \(\sqrt n(\bar X_n^*-\bar X_n)\), puis la méthode delta donne le résultat.

Intervalles et jackknife

Les intervalles percentile, bootstrap-\(t\), BC et BCa utilisent différentes approximations de la loi de l’erreur. Le jackknife recalcule des estimateurs leave-one-out et se relie aux fonctions d’influence.

19. Bootstrap 与 jackknife

重抽样通过在数据给出的抽样分布近似下反复计算统计量来估计不确定性。

定义. 非参数 bootstrap 从经验分布 \(P_n\) 中独立抽取 \(X_1^*,\ldots,X_n^*\)。bootstrap 统计量为 \(T_n^*=T(X_1^*,\ldots,X_n^*)\)，并在给定数据条件下研究。

光滑均值的 bootstrap 一致性. 若 \(T_n=g(\bar X_n)\)，\(EX_i=\mu\)，\(\operatorname{Var}(X_i)=\Sigma\)，且 \(g\) 在 \(\mu\) 可微，则给定数据条件下， \[ \sqrt n\{T_n^*-T_n\}\rightsquigarrow N(0,\dot g_\mu\Sigma\dot g_\mu^\top) \] 依概率成立。

证明. 给定数据后，\(X_i^*\) 独立同分布于 \(P_n\)。条件均值为 \(\bar X_n\)，条件协方差收敛到 \(\Sigma\)。条件 CLT 给出 \(\sqrt n(\bar X_n^*-\bar X_n)\) 的极限，再由 delta method 得到结论。

区间与 jackknife

bootstrap standard error、percentile、bootstrap-\(t\)、BC 和 BCa 区间使用不同方式近似误差分布。jackknife 重算 leave-one-out 估计量，与影响函数近似关系密切。
