6. Asymptotic tools for inference

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/maximum-likelihood-estimation/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/m-estimators-z-estimators/" %}

This page collects only the asymptotic tools used repeatedly in inference. Detailed probability limit theory belongs to the probability theory note.

Definition. \(X_n=o_p(a_n)\) means \(X_n/a_n\to0\) in probability. \(X_n=O_p(a_n)\) means \(X_n/a_n\) is tight. An estimator is asymptotically linear with influence function \(\phi\) if \[ T_n-\theta_0=P_n\phi+o_p(n^{-1/2}),\qquad P\phi=0,\quad P\|\phi\|^2\lt\infty. \]

Slutsky and delta method

If \(X_n\rightsquigarrow X\) and \(Y_n\to c\) in probability, then \(X_n+Y_n\rightsquigarrow X+c\) and \(X_nY_n\rightsquigarrow cX\). If \(\sqrt n(T_n-\theta)\rightsquigarrow Z\) and \(g\) is differentiable at \(\theta\), then \[ \sqrt n\{g(T_n)-g(\theta)\}\rightsquigarrow \dot g_\theta Z. \]

Asymptotic linearity implies normality. If \(T_n\) is asymptotically linear with influence function \(\phi\), then \[ \sqrt n(T_n-\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,P\phi\phi^\top). \]

Proof. Multiply the linear expansion by \(\sqrt n\): \[ \sqrt n(T_n-\theta_0)=n^{-1/2}\sum_{i=1}^n\phi(X_i)+o_p(1). \] The vector CLT gives the Gaussian limit for the sum, and Slutsky removes the negligible remainder.

Argmax and continuous mapping

The argmax theorem turns uniform convergence of random criteria into consistency of optimizers. The continuous mapping theorem turns convergence of estimators into convergence of transformed estimators. These two tools explain a large fraction of large-sample statistical inference.

6. Outils asymptotiques pour l’inférence

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/maximum-likelihood-estimation/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/m-estimators-z-estimators/" %}

Cette page rassemble seulement les outils asymptotiques utilisés constamment en inférence. La théorie probabiliste détaillée appartient à la note de probabilité.

Définition. \(X_n=o_p(a_n)\) signifie \(X_n/a_n\to0\) en probabilité. \(X_n=O_p(a_n)\) signifie que \(X_n/a_n\) est tendue. Un estimateur est asymptotiquement linéaire de fonction d’influence \(\phi\) si \[ T_n-\theta_0=P_n\phi+o_p(n^{-1/2}),\qquad P\phi=0,\quad P\|\phi\|^2\lt\infty. \]

Slutsky et méthode delta

Si \(X_n\rightsquigarrow X\) et \(Y_n\to c\) en probabilité, alors \(X_n+Y_n\rightsquigarrow X+c\) et \(X_nY_n\rightsquigarrow cX\). Si \(\sqrt n(T_n-\theta)\rightsquigarrow Z\) et si \(g\) est différentiable en \(\theta\), alors \[ \sqrt n\{g(T_n)-g(\theta)\}\rightsquigarrow \dot g_\theta Z. \]

Linéarité asymptotique. Si \(T_n\) est asymptotiquement linéaire de fonction d’influence \(\phi\), alors \[ \sqrt n(T_n-\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,P\phi\phi^\top). \]

Preuve. En multipliant l’expansion par \(\sqrt n\), \[ \sqrt n(T_n-\theta_0)=n^{-1/2}\sum_{i=1}^n\phi(X_i)+o_p(1). \] Le TCL vectoriel donne la limite normale de la somme, et Slutsky élimine le reste.

Argmax et application continue

Le théorème d’argmax transforme la convergence uniforme de critères aléatoires en consistance d’optimiseurs. Le théorème de l’application continue transporte la convergence à travers les transformations lisses.

6. 推断中的渐近工具

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/maximum-likelihood-estimation/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/m-estimators-z-estimators/" %}

本页只收集统计推断中反复使用的渐近工具。更完整的概率极限理论放在 probability theory 笔记中。

定义. \(X_n=o_p(a_n)\) 指 \(X_n/a_n\to0\) 依概率成立。\(X_n=O_p(a_n)\) 指 \(X_n/a_n\) 紧。若 \[ T_n-\theta_0=P_n\phi+o_p(n^{-1/2}),\qquad P\phi=0,\quad P\|\phi\|^2\lt\infty, \] 则称 \(T_n\) 以影响函数 \(\phi\) 渐近线性。

Slutsky 与 delta method

若 \(X_n\rightsquigarrow X\)，且 \(Y_n\to c\) 依概率，则 \(X_n+Y_n\rightsquigarrow X+c\)、\(X_nY_n\rightsquigarrow cX\)。若 \(\sqrt n(T_n-\theta)\rightsquigarrow Z\)，且 \(g\) 在 \(\theta\) 可微，则 \[ \sqrt n\{g(T_n)-g(\theta)\}\rightsquigarrow \dot g_\theta Z. \]

渐近线性推出正态性. 若 \(T_n\) 以影响函数 \(\phi\) 渐近线性，则 \[ \sqrt n(T_n-\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,P\phi\phi^\top). \]

证明. 将线性展开乘以 \(\sqrt n\)： \[ \sqrt n(T_n-\theta_0)=n^{-1/2}\sum_{i=1}^n\phi(X_i)+o_p(1). \] 向量 CLT 给出和的高斯极限，Slutsky 引理去掉 \(o_p(1)\) 余项。

Argmax 与连续映射

Argmax 定理把随机准则函数的一致收敛转化为优化点的一致性。Continuous mapping 定理把估计量的收敛传递到变换后的估计量。这两个工具解释了大部分大样本推断。
