Likelihood and MLE

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/asymptotic-tools/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/tests-and-confidence-sets/" %}

Likelihood inference starts from a parametric model \(\{p_\theta:\theta\in\Theta\subset\mathbb R^d\}\). For i.i.d. data \(X_1,\ldots,X_n\),

\[ \ell_n(\theta)=\sum_{i=1}^n\log p_\theta(X_i) \]

measures how well parameter \(\theta\) explains the observed data.

Score and Hessian

The score is \(S_n(\theta)=\partial_\theta\ell_n(\theta)\), and the Hessian is \(H_n(\theta)=\partial_\theta^2\ell_n(\theta)\). For one observation, write \(s_\theta(X)=\partial_\theta\log p_\theta(X)\).

Proposition. In a regular dominated model, \(E_\theta s_\theta(X)=0\) and

\[ I(\theta)=E_\theta\{s_\theta(X)s_\theta(X)^\top\} =-E_\theta\{\partial_\theta^2\log p_\theta(X)\}. \]

Proof. Since \(\int p_\theta(x)\,dx=1\), differentiating under the integral sign gives \(\int \partial_\theta p_\theta(x)\,dx=0\). But \(\partial_\theta p_\theta=p_\theta\partial_\theta\log p_\theta\), hence \(E_\theta s_\theta(X)=0\). Differentiating this identity once more gives \(0=E_\theta\{\partial_\theta s_\theta(X)\}+E_\theta\{s_\theta(X)s_\theta(X)^\top\}\).

Under regularity conditions allowing differentiation under the integral sign,

\[ E_\theta s_\theta(X) =\int \partial_\theta\log p_\theta(x)p_\theta(x)\,dx =\partial_\theta\int p_\theta(x)\,dx=0. \]

Differentiating again gives

\[ I(\theta)=E_\theta\{s_\theta(X)s_\theta(X)^\top\} =-E_\theta\{\partial_\theta^2\log p_\theta(X)\}. \]

The first expression says information is score variance; the second says information is expected negative curvature. A sharply curved likelihood carries more information.

Consistency through KL divergence

The MLE satisfies \(\hat\theta_n\in\arg\max_\theta\ell_n(\theta)\). If \(X_i\sim p_{\theta_0}\), then

\[ n^{-1}\ell_n(\theta)\to M(\theta):=E_{\theta_0}\log p_\theta(X) \]

for fixed \(\theta\). Moreover,

\[ M(\theta_0)-M(\theta)=\operatorname{KL}(p_{\theta_0},p_\theta)\ge0. \]

If the model is identifiable and \(n^{-1}\ell_n\) converges to \(M\) uniformly, the argmax theorem gives \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\) in probability.

Asymptotic normality

Theorem. Suppose the model is identifiable and regular, \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\), and \(I(\theta_0)\) is nonsingular. Then

\[ \sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,I(\theta_0)^{-1}). \]

Since \(S_n(\hat\theta_n)=0\), Taylor expansion around \(\theta_0\) gives

\[ 0=S_n(\theta_0)+H_n(\tilde\theta_n)(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0), \]

where \(\tilde\theta_n\) lies between \(\hat\theta_n\) and \(\theta_0\). Hence

\[ \sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0) = \{-n^{-1}H_n(\tilde\theta_n)\}^{-1}n^{-1/2}S_n(\theta_0). \]

By the CLT, \(n^{-1/2}S_n(\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,I(\theta_0))\). By a uniform law and consistency, \(-n^{-1}H_n(\tilde\theta_n)\to I(\theta_0)\) in probability. Slutsky gives

\[ \sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,I(\theta_0)^{-1}). \]

Observed information

In practice \(I(\theta_0)\) is unknown. The observed information is

\[ J_n(\hat\theta_n)=-n^{-1}H_n(\hat\theta_n). \]

Under the same regularity conditions, \(J_n(\hat\theta_n)\to I(\theta_0)\). Standard errors are the square roots of the diagonal entries of \(n^{-1}J_n(\hat\theta_n)^{-1}\).

Vraisemblance et Maximum de Vraisemblance

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/asymptotic-tools/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/tests-and-confidence-sets/" %}

L'inférence par vraisemblance part d'un modèle \(\{p_\theta:\theta\in\Theta\subset\mathbb R^d\}\). Pour des données i.i.d.,

\[ \ell_n(\theta)=\sum_{i=1}^n\log p_\theta(X_i) \]

mesure l'accord entre \(\theta\) et les données observées.

Score et Hessienne

Le score est \(S_n(\theta)=\partial_\theta\ell_n(\theta)\), et la Hessienne est \(H_n(\theta)=\partial_\theta^2\ell_n(\theta)\). Pour une observation, \(s_\theta(X)=\partial_\theta\log p_\theta(X)\).

Proposition. Dans un modèle dominé régulier, \(E_\theta s_\theta(X)=0\) et

\[ I(\theta)=E_\theta\{s_\theta(X)s_\theta(X)^\top\} =-E_\theta\{\partial_\theta^2\log p_\theta(X)\}. \]

Preuve. On différentie \(\int p_\theta(x)\,dx=1\) sous le signe intégral. Comme \(\partial_\theta p_\theta=p_\theta\partial_\theta\log p_\theta\), on obtient \(E_\theta s_\theta=0\). Une seconde différentiation donne l'identité entre variance du score et courbure moyenne négative.

Sous régularité,

\[ E_\theta s_\theta(X) =\int \partial_\theta\log p_\theta(x)p_\theta(x)\,dx =\partial_\theta\int p_\theta(x)\,dx=0. \]

En dérivant de nouveau,

\[ I(\theta)=E_\theta\{s_\theta(X)s_\theta(X)^\top\} =-E_\theta\{\partial_\theta^2\log p_\theta(X)\}. \]

L'information est à la fois variance du score et courbure moyenne négative.

Consistance par divergence KL

Le MLE vérifie \(\hat\theta_n\in\arg\max_\theta\ell_n(\theta)\). Si \(X_i\sim p_{\theta_0}\), alors

\[ n^{-1}\ell_n(\theta)\to M(\theta):=E_{\theta_0}\log p_\theta(X). \]

De plus,

\[ M(\theta_0)-M(\theta)=\operatorname{KL}(p_{\theta_0},p_\theta)\ge0. \]

Avec identifiabilité et convergence uniforme, le théorème d'argmax donne \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\) en probabilité.

Normalité asymptotique

Théorème. Si le modèle est identifiable et régulier, si \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\), et si \(I(\theta_0)\) est inversible, alors

\[ \sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,I(\theta_0)^{-1}). \]

Comme \(S_n(\hat\theta_n)=0\), un développement autour de \(\theta_0\) donne

\[ 0=S_n(\theta_0)+H_n(\tilde\theta_n)(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0). \]

Donc

\[ \sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0) = \{-n^{-1}H_n(\tilde\theta_n)\}^{-1}n^{-1/2}S_n(\theta_0). \]

Le TCL donne \(n^{-1/2}S_n(\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,I(\theta_0))\), et une loi uniforme donne \(-n^{-1}H_n(\tilde\theta_n)\to I(\theta_0)\). Slutsky implique

\[ \sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,I(\theta_0)^{-1}). \]

Information observée

L'information observée est

\[ J_n(\hat\theta_n)=-n^{-1}H_n(\hat\theta_n). \]

Elle converge vers \(I(\theta_0)\). Les erreurs standards sont les racines carrées des éléments diagonaux de \(n^{-1}J_n(\hat\theta_n)^{-1}\).

似然与最大似然估计

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/asymptotic-tools/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/tests-and-confidence-sets/" %}

似然推断从参数模型 \(\{p_\theta:\theta\in\Theta\subset\mathbb R^d\}\) 出发。对独立同分布数据，

\[ \ell_n(\theta)=\sum_{i=1}^n\log p_\theta(X_i) \]

衡量参数 \(\theta\) 对观测数据的解释程度。

Score 与 Hessian

score 为 \(S_n(\theta)=\partial_\theta\ell_n(\theta)\)，Hessian 为 \(H_n(\theta)=\partial_\theta^2\ell_n(\theta)\)。对单个观测，记 \(s_\theta(X)=\partial_\theta\log p_\theta(X)\)。

命题. 在正则 dominated 模型中，\(E_\theta s_\theta(X)=0\)，并且

\[ I(\theta)=E_\theta\{s_\theta(X)s_\theta(X)^\top\} =-E_\theta\{\partial_\theta^2\log p_\theta(X)\}. \]

证明. 对恒等式 \(\int p_\theta(x)\,dx=1\) 在积分号下求导，得 \(\int \partial_\theta p_\theta(x)\,dx=0\)。又 \(\partial_\theta p_\theta=p_\theta\partial_\theta\log p_\theta\)，所以 \(E_\theta s_\theta(X)=0\)。再次求导即可得到 score 方差等于平均负 Hessian。

在允许积分号下求导的正则条件下，

\[ E_\theta s_\theta(X) =\int \partial_\theta\log p_\theta(x)p_\theta(x)\,dx =\partial_\theta\int p_\theta(x)\,dx=0. \]

再次求导可得

\[ I(\theta)=E_\theta\{s_\theta(X)s_\theta(X)^\top\} =-E_\theta\{\partial_\theta^2\log p_\theta(X)\}. \]

第一个表达说明 Fisher 信息是 score 的方差；第二个表达说明它是平均负曲率。似然越尖，信息越多。

通过 KL 散度得到一致性

MLE 满足 \(\hat\theta_n\in\arg\max_\theta\ell_n(\theta)\)。若 \(X_i\sim p_{\theta_0}\)，则

\[ n^{-1}\ell_n(\theta)\to M(\theta):=E_{\theta_0}\log p_\theta(X). \]

并且

\[ M(\theta_0)-M(\theta)=\operatorname{KL}(p_{\theta_0},p_\theta)\ge0. \]

若模型可识别，且 \(n^{-1}\ell_n\) 一致收敛到 \(M\)，则 argmax 定理给出 \(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\) 依概率成立。

渐近正态性

定理. 若模型可识别且正则，\(\hat\theta_n\to\theta_0\)，并且 \(I(\theta_0)\) 非奇异，则

\[ \sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,I(\theta_0)^{-1}). \]

由于 \(S_n(\hat\theta_n)=0\)，在 \(\theta_0\) 附近 Taylor 展开得到

\[ 0=S_n(\theta_0)+H_n(\tilde\theta_n)(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0). \]

因此

\[ \sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0) = \{-n^{-1}H_n(\tilde\theta_n)\}^{-1}n^{-1/2}S_n(\theta_0). \]

由 CLT，\(n^{-1/2}S_n(\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,I(\theta_0))\)。由一致大数定律和一致性，\(-n^{-1}H_n(\tilde\theta_n)\to I(\theta_0)\) 依概率成立。Slutsky 引理给出

\[ \sqrt n(\hat\theta_n-\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow N(0,I(\theta_0)^{-1}). \]

观测信息

实践中 \(I(\theta_0)\) 未知。观测信息定义为

\[ J_n(\hat\theta_n)=-n^{-1}H_n(\hat\theta_n). \]

它在正则条件下收敛到 \(I(\theta_0)\)。标准误差来自 \(n^{-1}J_n(\hat\theta_n)^{-1}\) 的对角元平方根。
