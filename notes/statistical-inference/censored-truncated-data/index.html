23. Censored and truncated data

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/missing-data-em/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/local-asymptotic-normality-contiguity/" %}

Censoring and truncation are not ordinary missingness: the observation mechanism changes which likelihood contribution is appropriate.

Definition. If event time \(T\) is right-censored by \(C\), one observes \(Y=\min(T,C)\) and \(\Delta=1_{\{T\le C\}}\). Under independent censoring, the likelihood contribution for \(T\) is \[ f_\theta(Y)^\Delta S_\theta(Y)^{1-\Delta}. \]

Kaplan-Meier form. With ordered event times \(t_j\), deaths \(d_j\), and risk sets \(r_j\), the nonparametric MLE of the survival function is \[ \hat S(t)=\prod_{t_j\le t}\left(1-\frac{d_j}{r_j}\right). \]

Proof. Put mass only at observed event times. Conditional on surviving to \(t_j\), the likelihood for the hazard jump \(q_j\) is \(q_j^{d_j}(1-q_j)^{r_j-d_j}\). Maximizing gives \(\hat q_j=d_j/r_j\). Survival is the product of conditional survival factors \(1-\hat q_j\).

Truncation

Truncation means some units are unobservable unless they fall in a sampling window. The likelihood must condition on being observable; otherwise estimates are biased toward observable regions.

23. Données censurées et tronquées

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/missing-data-em/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/local-asymptotic-normality-contiguity/" %}

La censure et la troncature ne sont pas de simples données manquantes : le mécanisme d’observation modifie la contribution de vraisemblance.

Définition. Si un temps \(T\) est censuré à droite par \(C\), on observe \(Y=\min(T,C)\) et \(\Delta=1_{\{T\le C\}}\). Sous censure indépendante, la contribution de vraisemblance est \[ f_\theta(Y)^\Delta S_\theta(Y)^{1-\Delta}. \]

Kaplan-Meier. Avec temps d’événement \(t_j\), décès \(d_j\), et ensembles à risque \(r_j\), \[ \hat S(t)=\prod_{t_j\le t}\left(1-\frac{d_j}{r_j}\right). \]

Preuve. On place la masse aux temps d’événement observés. Conditionnellement à la survie jusqu’à \(t_j\), la vraisemblance du saut de risque \(q_j\) est \(q_j^{d_j}(1-q_j)^{r_j-d_j}\). Le maximum est \(\hat q_j=d_j/r_j\), et la survie est le produit des facteurs \(1-\hat q_j\).

Troncature

La troncature signifie que certaines unités sont inobservables hors d’une fenêtre. La vraisemblance doit conditionner sur l’observabilité.

23. 删失与截断数据

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/missing-data-em/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/local-asymptotic-normality-contiguity/" %}

删失和截断不是普通缺失：观测机制会改变正确的似然贡献。

定义. 若事件时间 \(T\) 被 \(C\) 右删失，观测到 \(Y=\min(T,C)\) 和 \(\Delta=1_{\{T\le C\}}\)。在独立删失下，关于 \(T\) 的似然贡献为 \[ f_\theta(Y)^\Delta S_\theta(Y)^{1-\Delta}. \]

Kaplan-Meier 形式. 设有序事件时刻为 \(t_j\)，事件数为 \(d_j\)，风险集大小为 \(r_j\)，则 survival function 的非参数 MLE 为 \[ \hat S(t)=\prod_{t_j\le t}\left(1-\frac{d_j}{r_j}\right). \]

证明. 只在观测事件时刻放质量。条件于存活到 \(t_j\)，hazard jump \(q_j\) 的似然为 \(q_j^{d_j}(1-q_j)^{r_j-d_j}\)，最大化得 \(\hat q_j=d_j/r_j\)。生存函数是条件生存因子 \(1-\hat q_j\) 的乘积。

截断

截断表示只有落在抽样窗口内的个体才可观察。似然必须条件于“可被观察”这一事件，否则估计会偏向可观察区域。
