20. Higher-order asymptotics

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/bootstrap-jackknife/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/monte-carlo-for-inference/" %}

First-order asymptotics describe the main Gaussian limit. Higher-order asymptotics study the error of that approximation and how to improve it.

Definition. An Edgeworth expansion approximates a distribution function by \[ P\{Z_n\le x\}=\Phi(x)+n^{-1/2}p_1(x)\phi(x)+n^{-1}p_2(x)\phi(x)+\cdots+o(n^{-1}), \] where the polynomials \(p_j\) depend on cumulants of the summands or statistic.

Cornish-Fisher idea. If an Edgeworth expansion is available, approximate quantiles can be corrected by solving the expansion for \(x\). This gives Cornish-Fisher corrections to normal critical values.

Proof. Write the desired quantile as \(x=z_\alpha+n^{-1/2}a+n^{-1}b+\cdots\). Substitute into the Edgeworth expansion and match coefficients of powers of \(n^{-1/2}\). The first coefficient \(a\) cancels the \(n^{-1/2}\) distributional error at \(z_\alpha\); later coefficients cancel higher-order errors.

Bartlett correction

Likelihood ratio statistics often have expectation \(r+c/n+o(n^{-1})\) instead of \(r\). Multiplying by \(1-c/(rn)\) can improve chi-square approximation from first-order to second-order accuracy.

20. Asymptotique d’ordre supérieur

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/bootstrap-jackknife/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/monte-carlo-for-inference/" %}

L’asymptotique de premier ordre décrit la limite gaussienne principale. L’asymptotique d’ordre supérieur étudie l’erreur de cette approximation et comment l’améliorer.

Définition. Un développement d’Edgeworth approxime une fonction de répartition par \[ P\{Z_n\le x\}=\Phi(x)+n^{-1/2}p_1(x)\phi(x)+n^{-1}p_2(x)\phi(x)+\cdots+o(n^{-1}), \] où les polynômes \(p_j\) dépendent des cumulants.

Idée de Cornish-Fisher. Si un développement d’Edgeworth est disponible, on corrige les quantiles en inversant l’expansion. On obtient des corrections aux valeurs critiques normales.

Preuve. On écrit le quantile sous la forme \(x=z_\alpha+n^{-1/2}a+n^{-1}b+\cdots\). En substituant dans l’expansion et en identifiant les puissances de \(n^{-1/2}\), on choisit \(a,b,\ldots\) pour annuler successivement les erreurs.

Correction de Bartlett

Les statistiques de rapport de vraisemblance ont souvent espérance \(r+c/n+o(n^{-1})\). Une correction multiplicative améliore l’approximation \(\chi^2\).

20. 高阶渐近理论

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/bootstrap-jackknife/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/monte-carlo-for-inference/" %}

一阶渐近理论描述主要 Gaussian 极限。高阶渐近理论研究这种近似的误差，以及如何改进近似。

定义. Edgeworth 展开用 \[ P\{Z_n\le x\}=\Phi(x)+n^{-1/2}p_1(x)\phi(x)+n^{-1}p_2(x)\phi(x)+\cdots+o(n^{-1}) \] 近似分布函数，其中多项式 \(p_j\) 由 cumulants 决定。

Cornish-Fisher 思路. 若有 Edgeworth 展开，可以反解该展开来修正分位数，从而得到 normal critical values 的 Cornish-Fisher 修正。

证明. 将所求分位数写为 \(x=z_\alpha+n^{-1/2}a+n^{-1}b+\cdots\)。代入 Edgeworth 展开，并逐次匹配 \(n^{-1/2}\) 的幂次。选择 \(a,b,\ldots\) 使各阶误差依次抵消。

Bartlett 修正

似然比统计量的期望常为 \(r+c/n+o(n^{-1})\)，而不是 \(r\)。乘上 \(1-c/(rn)\) 一类修正可把 \(\chi^2\) 近似从一阶改进到二阶。
