8. Empirical likelihood and estimating equations

{% include note-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/notes/statistical-inference/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/statistical-inference/m-estimators-z-estimators/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/statistical-inference/hypothesis-testing-foundations/" %}

Empirical likelihood builds likelihood-type inference from moment equations without choosing a parametric distribution.

Estimating equations

Definition. Let \(g(x,\theta)\in\mathbb R^r\) satisfy \(P g(X,\theta_0)=0\). The parameter \(\theta_0\) is identified by the moment condition when no other \(\theta\) satisfies it in the relevant model.

Empirical likelihood

Definition. For fixed \(\theta\), empirical likelihood maximizes \(\prod_{i=1}^n p_i\) over weights \(p_i\ge0\), \(\sum_i p_i=1\), and \(\sum_i p_i g(X_i,\theta)=0\).

Lagrange multiplier representation

Proposition. When the constrained optimum exists, the weights have the form \(p_i(\theta)=\{n[1+\lambda(\theta)^\top g(X_i,\theta)]\}^{-1}\), where \(\lambda(\theta)\) solves \(\sum_i g(X_i,\theta)/(1+\lambda^\top g(X_i,\theta))=0\).

Wilks theorem

Theorem. Under regularity and correct specification, the empirical likelihood ratio satisfies \(-2\log R(\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow\chi_r^2\) for a fixed \(r\)-dimensional moment restriction. With parameters estimated under constraints, the degrees of freedom match the number of tested restrictions.

Relation to GMM and bootstrap

Remark. Empirical likelihood is a likelihood-shaped alternative to GMM. Its automatic studentization explains the chi-square limit, while bootstrap calibration is useful for small samples, overidentification, and boundary cases.

8. 经验似然与估计方程

经验似然从矩条件构造似然型推断，而不需要指定参数分布族。

Estimating equations

定义. Let \(g(x,\theta)\in\mathbb R^r\) satisfy \(P g(X,\theta_0)=0\). The parameter \(\theta_0\) is identified by the moment condition when no other \(\theta\) satisfies it in the relevant model.

经验似然

定义. For fixed \(\theta\), 经验似然 maximizes \(\prod_{i=1}^n p_i\) over weights \(p_i\ge0\), \(\sum_i p_i=1\), and \(\sum_i p_i g(X_i,\theta)=0\).

命题. When the constrained optimum exists, the weights have the form \(p_i(\theta)=\{n[1+\lambda(\theta)^\top g(X_i,\theta)]\}^{-1}\), where \(\lambda(\theta)\) solves \(\sum_i g(X_i,\theta)/(1+\lambda^\top g(X_i,\theta))=0\).

定理. Under regularity and correct specification, the 经验似然 ratio satisfies \(-2\log R(\theta_0)\rightsquigarrow\chi_r^2\) for a fixed \(r\)-dimensional moment restriction. With parameters estimated under constraints, the degrees of freedom match the number of tested restrictions.

说明. 经验似然 is a 似然-shaped alternative to GMM. Its automatic studentization explains the chi-square limit, while 自助法 calibration is useful for small samples, overidentification, and boundary cases.
