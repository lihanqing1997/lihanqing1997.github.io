Weak law of large numbers

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/characteristic-functions/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/borel-cantelli-lemma/" %}

Definition. For random variables \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\), the weak law of large numbers concerns convergence in probability of averages \(\bar X_n=n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^nX_k\).

Theorem. If \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\) are i.i.d. with \(\mathbb E X_1=\mu\) and \(\operatorname{Var}(X_1)=\sigma^2\lt \infty\), then \(\bar X_n\to\mu\) in probability.

Proof. Linearity gives \(\mathbb E\bar X_n=\mu\). Independence gives \[ \operatorname{Var}(\bar X_n)=\operatorname{Var}\left(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^nX_k\right) =n^{-2}\sum_{k=1}^n\operatorname{Var}(X_k)=\frac{\sigma^2}{n}. \] Chebyshev’s inequality yields \[ P(|\bar X_n-\mu|\gt \varepsilon)\le \frac{\sigma^2}{n\varepsilon^2}\to0. \]

Convergence in probability versus sample-path stabilization

The weak law says that averages stabilize in probability; it does not by itself give almost sure stabilization of a single infinite sample path.

Loi faible des grands nombres

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/characteristic-functions/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/borel-cantelli-lemma/" %}

Définition. Pour des variables \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\), la loi faible des grands nombres étudie la convergence en probabilité des moyennes \(\bar X_n=n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^nX_k\).

Théorème. Si \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\) sont i.i.d., avec \(\mathbb E X_1=\mu\) et \(\operatorname{Var}(X_1)=\sigma^2\lt \infty\), alors \(\bar X_n\to\mu\) en probabilité.

Preuve. Par linéarité, \(\mathbb E\bar X_n=\mu\). Par indépendance, \[ \operatorname{Var}(\bar X_n)=\operatorname{Var}\left(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^nX_k\right) =n^{-2}\sum_{k=1}^n\operatorname{Var}(X_k)=\frac{\sigma^2}{n}. \] L’inégalité de Chebyshev donne \[ P(|\bar X_n-\mu|\gt \varepsilon)\le \frac{\sigma^2}{n\varepsilon^2}\to0. \]

Convergence en probabilité et stabilisation des trajectoires

La loi faible affirme une stabilisation en probabilité ; elle ne donne pas encore la stabilisation presque sûre d’une trajectoire infinie.

弱大数定律

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/characteristic-functions/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/borel-cantelli-lemma/" %}

定义. 对随机变量 \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\)，弱大数定律研究均值 \(\bar X_n=n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^nX_k\) 的依概率收敛。

定理. 若 \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\) 独立同分布，且 \(\mathbb E X_1=\mu\)、\(\operatorname{Var}(X_1)=\sigma^2\lt \infty\)，则 \(\bar X_n\to\mu\) 依概率收敛。

证明. 由线性性，\(\mathbb E\bar X_n=\mu\)。由独立性， \[ \operatorname{Var}(\bar X_n)=\operatorname{Var}\left(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^nX_k\right) =n^{-2}\sum_{k=1}^n\operatorname{Var}(X_k)=\frac{\sigma^2}{n}. \] Chebyshev 不等式给出 \[ P(|\bar X_n-\mu|\gt \varepsilon)\le \frac{\sigma^2}{n\varepsilon^2}\to0. \]

依概率收敛与样本路径稳定

弱大数定律给出的是依概率稳定；它本身还没有说明单条无限样本路径上的几乎处处稳定。
