Weak Law of Large Numbers

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/independence/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/borel-cantelli-lemma/" %}

The weak law says that sample averages converge to the mean in probability.

Definition

\(X_n\to X\) in probability if for every \(\varepsilon>0\),

\[P(|X_n-X|>\varepsilon)\to0.\]

Theorem: finite-variance weak law

Let \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\) be i.i.d. with \(EX_1=\mu\) and \(\operatorname{Var}(X_1)=\sigma^2<\infty\). If \(\bar X_n=n^{-1}\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\), then \(\bar X_n\to\mu\) in probability.

Proof

By independence, \(E\bar X_n=\mu\) and \(\operatorname{Var}(\bar X_n)=n^{-2}\sum_{i=1}^n\operatorname{Var}(X_i)=\sigma^2/n\).

Chebyshev's inequality gives, for every \(\varepsilon>0\),

\[P(|\bar X_n-\mu|>\varepsilon)\le \frac{\operatorname{Var}(\bar X_n)}{\varepsilon^2}=\frac{\sigma^2}{n\varepsilon^2}\to0.\]

This is exactly convergence in probability.

Intuition

The average has the same center \(\mu\), but its variance is divided by \(n\), so large deviations from \(\mu\) become rare.

Loi faible des grands nombres

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/independence/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/borel-cantelli-lemma/" %}

La loi faible dit que les moyennes empiriques convergent vers la moyenne en probabilité.

Définition

\(X_n\to X\) en probabilité si, pour tout \(\varepsilon>0\),

\[P(|X_n-X|>\varepsilon)\to0.\]

Théorème : loi faible avec variance finie

Soient \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\) i.i.d. avec \(EX_1=\mu\) et \(\operatorname{Var}(X_1)=\sigma^2<\infty\). Si \(\bar X_n=n^{-1}\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\), alors \(\bar X_n\to\mu\) en probabilité.

Preuve

Par indépendance, \(E\bar X_n=\mu\) et \(\operatorname{Var}(\bar X_n)=n^{-2}\sum_{i=1}^n\operatorname{Var}(X_i)=\sigma^2/n\).

L'inégalité de Chebyshev donne, pour tout \(\varepsilon>0\),

\[P(|\bar X_n-\mu|>\varepsilon)\le \frac{\operatorname{Var}(\bar X_n)}{\varepsilon^2}=\frac{\sigma^2}{n\varepsilon^2}\to0.\]

C'est précisément la convergence en probabilité.

Intuition

La moyenne garde le même centre \(\mu\), mais sa variance est divisée par \(n\), donc les grands écarts deviennent rares.

Guide Durrett : section 2.2.

弱大数定律

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/independence/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/borel-cantelli-lemma/" %}

弱大数定律说明样本均值依概率收敛到总体均值。

定义

若对任意 \(\varepsilon>0\)，

\[P(|X_n-X|>\varepsilon)\to0,\]

则称 \(X_n\to X\) 依概率收敛。

定理：有限方差形式

设 \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\) 独立同分布，\(EX_1=\mu\)，\(\operatorname{Var}(X_1)=\sigma^2<\infty\)。若 \(\bar X_n=n^{-1}\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\)，则 \(\bar X_n\to\mu\) 依概率收敛。

证明

由独立性，\(E\bar X_n=\mu\)，且 \(\operatorname{Var}(\bar X_n)=n^{-2}\sum_{i=1}^n\operatorname{Var}(X_i)=\sigma^2/n\)。

由 Chebyshev 不等式，对任意 \(\varepsilon>0\)，

\[P(|\bar X_n-\mu|>\varepsilon)\le \frac{\operatorname{Var}(\bar X_n)}{\varepsilon^2}=\frac{\sigma^2}{n\varepsilon^2}\to0.\]

这正是依概率收敛的定义。

直观解释

样本均值的中心仍是 \(\mu\)，但方差缩小为原来的 \(1/n\)，所以偏离 \(\mu\) 的概率趋于零。

Durrett 参考：第 2.2 节。
