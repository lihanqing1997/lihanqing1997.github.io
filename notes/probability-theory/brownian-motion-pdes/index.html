43. Brownian motion and PDEs

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/multidimensional-brownian-motion/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/continuous-time-martingales-local-martingales/" %}

Definition. For Brownian motion started at \(x\), write \(\mathbb E_x\). The heat semigroup is \[ P_tf(x)=\mathbb E_x f(B_t)=\int_{\mathbb R^d}f(y)p_t(x-y)\,\mathrm{d}y, \] where \(p_t(z)=(2\pi t)^{-d/2}e^{-|z|^2/(2t)}\).

Theorem. If \(f\) is bounded and regular enough, then \(u(t,x)=P_tf(x)\) solves \[ \partial_tu=\frac12\Delta u,\qquad u(0,x)=f(x). \]

Proof. Differentiate the convolution under the integral sign. The heat kernel satisfies \(\partial_t p_t(z)=\frac12\Delta_zp_t(z)\). Therefore \[ \partial_tu(t,x)=\int f(y)\partial_t p_t(x-y)\,\mathrm{d}y =\frac12\int f(y)\Delta_xp_t(x-y)\,\mathrm{d}y =\frac12\Delta_xu(t,x). \] As \(t\downarrow0\), \(p_t\) is an approximate identity, so \(u(t,x)\to f(x)\) at continuity points of \(f\).

Generator and heat equation

Brownian motion represents the heat equation because its generator is \(\frac12\Delta\).

43. Mouvement brownien et EDP

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/multidimensional-brownian-motion/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/continuous-time-martingales-local-martingales/" %}

Définition. Pour un mouvement brownien partant de \(x\), on écrit \(\mathbb E_x\). Le semi-groupe de la chaleur est \[ P_tf(x)=\mathbb E_x f(B_t)=\int_{\mathbb R^d}f(y)p_t(x-y)\,\mathrm{d}y, \] où \(p_t(z)=(2\pi t)^{-d/2}e^{-|z|^2/(2t)}\).

Théorème. Si \(f\) est bornée et suffisamment régulière, alors \(u(t,x)=P_tf(x)\) résout \[ \partial_tu=\frac12\Delta u,\qquad u(0,x)=f(x). \]

Preuve. On dérive la convolution sous le signe intégral. Le noyau de chaleur vérifie \(\partial_t p_t(z)=\frac12\Delta_zp_t(z)\). Donc \[ \partial_tu(t,x)=\int f(y)\partial_t p_t(x-y)\,\mathrm{d}y =\frac12\int f(y)\Delta_xp_t(x-y)\,\mathrm{d}y =\frac12\Delta_xu(t,x). \] Lorsque \(t\downarrow0\), \(p_t\) est une approximation de l’identité, donc \(u(t,x)\to f(x)\) aux points de continuité de \(f\).

Générateur et équation de la chaleur

Le mouvement brownien représente l’équation de la chaleur parce que son générateur est \(\frac12\Delta\).

43. 布朗运动与偏微分方程

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/multidimensional-brownian-motion/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/continuous-time-martingales-local-martingales/" %}

定义. 对从 \(x\) 出发的 Brownian motion，记期望为 \(\mathbb E_x\)。热半群为 \[ P_tf(x)=\mathbb E_x f(B_t)=\int_{\mathbb R^d}f(y)p_t(x-y)\,\mathrm{d}y, \] 其中 \(p_t(z)=(2\pi t)^{-d/2}e^{-|z|^2/(2t)}\)。

定理. 若 \(f\) 有界且足够光滑，则 \(u(t,x)=P_tf(x)\) 解 \[ \partial_tu=\frac12\Delta u,\qquad u(0,x)=f(x). \]

证明. 在积分号下对卷积求导。热核满足 \(\partial_t p_t(z)=\frac12\Delta_zp_t(z)\)。因此 \[ \partial_tu(t,x)=\int f(y)\partial_t p_t(x-y)\,\mathrm{d}y =\frac12\int f(y)\Delta_xp_t(x-y)\,\mathrm{d}y =\frac12\Delta_xu(t,x). \] 当 \(t\downarrow0\) 时，\(p_t\) 是近似恒等核，所以在 \(f\) 的连续点有 \(u(t,x)\to f(x)\)。

生成元与热方程

Brownian motion 表示热方程，是因为它的生成元是 \(\frac12\Delta\)。
