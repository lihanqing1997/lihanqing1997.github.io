Conditional expectation

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/conditional-probability-regular-conditional-distributions/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/modes-of-convergence/" %}

Definition. For \(X\in L^1(\Omega,\mathcal F,P)\) and a sub-\(\sigma\)-field \(\mathcal G\subset\mathcal F\), a conditional expectation \(\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]\) is a \(\mathcal G\)-measurable integrable random variable \(Y\) such that \[ \int_GY\,\mathrm{d}P=\int_GX\,\mathrm{d}P\qquad\text{for every }G\in\mathcal G. \]

Theorem. Conditional expectation is unique almost surely. It is linear, positive, satisfies \(\mathbb E[\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]]=\mathbb E X\), and if \(\mathcal H\subset\mathcal G\), then \[ \mathbb E[\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]\mid\mathcal H]=\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal H]. \]

Proof. If \(Y_1,Y_2\) both satisfy the defining identity, then \(\int_G(Y_1-Y_2)\mathrm{d}P=0\) for every \(G\in\mathcal G\). Taking \(G=\{Y_1\gt Y_2\}\) and \(G=\{Y_2\gt Y_1\}\), both in \(\mathcal G\), gives \(Y_1=Y_2\) almost surely. Linearity and positivity follow by testing the defining identity on every \(G\in\mathcal G\). For the tower property, \(\mathbb E[\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]\mid\mathcal H]\) is \(\mathcal H\)-measurable and has the same integrals as \(X\) over every \(H\in\mathcal H\); uniqueness identifies it with \(\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal H]\).

Remark

Conditional expectation is an \(L^1\)-projection onto the information contained in \(\mathcal G\), though in \(L^2\) this projection interpretation becomes literal orthogonal projection.

Espérance conditionnelle

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/conditional-probability-regular-conditional-distributions/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/modes-of-convergence/" %}

Définition. Pour \(X\in L^1(\Omega,\mathcal F,P)\) et une sous-\(\sigma\)-algèbre \(\mathcal G\subset\mathcal F\), une espérance conditionnelle \(\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]\) est une variable \(Y\), intégrable et \(\mathcal G\)-mesurable, telle que \[ \int_GY\,\mathrm{d}P=\int_GX\,\mathrm{d}P\qquad\text{pour tout }G\in\mathcal G. \]

Théorème. L’espérance conditionnelle est unique presque sûrement. Elle est linéaire, positive, vérifie \(\mathbb E[\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]]=\mathbb E X\), et si \(\mathcal H\subset\mathcal G\), alors \[ \mathbb E[\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]\mid\mathcal H]=\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal H]. \]

Preuve. Si \(Y_1,Y_2\) vérifient la définition, alors \(\int_G(Y_1-Y_2)\mathrm{d}P=0\) pour tout \(G\in\mathcal G\). En prenant \(G=\{Y_1\gt Y_2\}\) puis \(G=\{Y_2\gt Y_1\}\), on obtient \(Y_1=Y_2\) presque sûrement. La linéarité et la positivité se vérifient directement dans l’identité définissante. Pour la propriété de tour, \(\mathbb E[\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]\mid\mathcal H]\) est \(\mathcal H\)-mesurable et a les mêmes intégrales que \(X\) sur tout \(H\in\mathcal H\). L’unicité donne donc \(\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal H]\).

Remarque

L’espérance conditionnelle est une projection de \(X\) sur l’information contenue dans \(\mathcal G\) ; dans \(L^2\), cette image devient exactement une projection orthogonale.

条件期望

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/conditional-probability-regular-conditional-distributions/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/modes-of-convergence/" %}

定义. 设 \(X\in L^1(\Omega,\mathcal F,P)\)，且 \(\mathcal G\subset\mathcal F\) 为子 \(\sigma\)-代数。条件期望 \(\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]\) 是一个 \(\mathcal G\)-可测且可积的随机变量 \(Y\)，满足 \[ \int_GY\,\mathrm{d}P=\int_GX\,\mathrm{d}P\qquad\text{对每个 }G\in\mathcal G. \]

定理. 条件期望在几乎处处意义下唯一。它具有线性、保正性，满足 \(\mathbb E[\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]]=\mathbb E X\)，并且若 \(\mathcal H\subset\mathcal G\)，则 \[ \mathbb E[\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]\mid\mathcal H]=\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal H]. \]

证明. 若 \(Y_1,Y_2\) 都满足定义，则对每个 \(G\in\mathcal G\)，有 \(\int_G(Y_1-Y_2)\mathrm{d}P=0\)。取 \(G=\{Y_1\gt Y_2\}\) 和 \(G=\{Y_2\gt Y_1\}\)，得到 \(Y_1=Y_2\) 几乎处处成立。线性和保正性可直接代入定义验证。对塔性质，\(\mathbb E[\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal G]\mid\mathcal H]\) 是 \(\mathcal H\)-可测，并且在每个 \(H\in\mathcal H\) 上与 \(X\) 有相同积分；由唯一性，它等于 \(\mathbb E[X\mid\mathcal H]\)。

说明

条件期望可以看作把 \(X\) 投影到 \(\mathcal G\) 所包含的信息上；在 \(L^2\) 中，这个说法就是严格的正交投影。
