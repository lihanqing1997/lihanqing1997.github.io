Strong Law of Large Numbers

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/borel-cantelli-lemma/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/central-limit-theorem/" %}

The strong law upgrades convergence in probability to convergence of the whole sample path outside a null set.

Definition. \(X_n\to X\) almost surely if \(P(\{\omega:X_n(\omega)\to X(\omega)\})=1\).

Theorem: i.i.d. strong law. Let \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\) be i.i.d. with \(E|X_1|<\infty\) and \(EX_1=\mu\). If \(S_n=\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\), then \(S_n/n\to\mu\) almost surely.

Proof. Tool. If independent centered variables \(Z_n\) satisfy \(\sum_n\operatorname{Var}(Z_n)<\infty\), then \(\sum_n Z_n\) converges almost surely. Indeed, Kolmogorov's maximal inequality gives, for \(N\ge m\),

\[P\left(\max_{m\le k\le N}\left|\sum_{n=m}^kZ_n\right|>\varepsilon\right)\le \varepsilon^{-2}\sum_{n=m}^N\operatorname{Var}(Z_n).\]

Letting \(N\to\infty\), choosing \(m_j\) with tail variance at most \(2^{-j}\varepsilon^2\), and applying Borel-Cantelli shows that the tails are eventually \(\varepsilon\)-small. Taking rational \(\varepsilon>0\), the partial sums are Cauchy almost surely.

Kronecker's lemma. If \(\sum_na_n/n\) converges, then \(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^na_k\to0\). Let \(t_n=\sum_{k=1}^na_k/k\). Since \(a_k=k(t_k-t_{k-1})\), summation by parts gives \(\sum_{k=1}^na_k=nt_n-\sum_{k=1}^{n-1}t_k\). Dividing by \(n\), both terms tend to the same limit by Cesàro averaging.

Set \(Y_n=X_n1_{\{|X_n|\le n\}}\). Since

\[\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P(X_n\ne Y_n)=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P(|X_1|>n)\le E|X_1|<\infty,\]

Borel-Cantelli gives \(X_n=Y_n\) eventually almost surely. Hence \(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^n(X_k-Y_k)\to0\) almost surely.

Also,

\[\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{\operatorname{Var}(Y_n)}{n^2}\le \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{E[Y_n^2]}{n^2}=E\left[X_1^2\sum_{n\ge |X_1|}\frac1{n^2}\right]<\infty.\]

Apply the tool to \(Z_n=(Y_n-EY_n)/n\). Then \(\sum_n(Y_n-EY_n)/n\) converges almost surely, and Kronecker's lemma gives \(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^n(Y_k-EY_k)\to0\) almost surely.

Finally, \(EY_n\to EX_1=\mu\) by dominated convergence, so \(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^nEY_k\to\mu\) by Cesàro averaging. Combining the three terms yields \(S_n/n\to\mu\) almost surely.

Loi forte des grands nombres

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/borel-cantelli-lemma/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/central-limit-theorem/" %}

La loi forte transforme une convergence en probabilité en convergence de toute la trajectoire hors d'un ensemble nul.

Définition. \(X_n\to X\) presque sûrement si \(P(\{\omega:X_n(\omega)\to X(\omega)\})=1\).

Théorème : loi forte i.i.d. Soient \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\) i.i.d. avec \(E|X_1|<\infty\) et \(EX_1=\mu\). Si \(S_n=\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\), alors \(S_n/n\to\mu\) presque sûrement.

Preuve. Outil. Si des variables indépendantes centrées \(Z_n\) vérifient \(\sum_n\operatorname{Var}(Z_n)<\infty\), alors \(\sum_nZ_n\) converge presque sûrement. En effet, l'inégalité maximale de Kolmogorov donne, pour \(N\ge m\),

\[P\left(\max_{m\le k\le N}\left|\sum_{n=m}^kZ_n\right|>\varepsilon\right)\le \varepsilon^{-2}\sum_{n=m}^N\operatorname{Var}(Z_n).\]

En faisant tendre \(N\) vers l'infini, puis en choisissant \(m_j\) tel que la variance de queue soit au plus \(2^{-j}\varepsilon^2\), le lemme de Borel-Cantelli montre que les queues sont finalement plus petites que \(\varepsilon\). Pour les \(\varepsilon\) rationnels positifs, les sommes partielles sont donc de Cauchy p.s.

Lemme de Kronecker. Si \(\sum_na_n/n\) converge, alors \(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^na_k\to0\). Posons \(t_n=\sum_{k=1}^na_k/k\). Comme \(a_k=k(t_k-t_{k-1})\), une sommation par parties donne \(\sum_{k=1}^na_k=nt_n-\sum_{k=1}^{n-1}t_k\). Après division par \(n\), les deux termes tendent vers la même limite par moyenne de Cesàro.

Posons \(Y_n=X_n1_{\{|X_n|\le n\}}\). Comme

\[\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P(X_n\ne Y_n)=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P(|X_1|>n)\le E|X_1|<\infty,\]

Borel-Cantelli donne \(X_n=Y_n\) finalement p.s. Ainsi \(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^n(X_k-Y_k)\to0\) p.s.

De plus,

\[\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{\operatorname{Var}(Y_n)}{n^2}\le \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{E[Y_n^2]}{n^2}=E\left[X_1^2\sum_{n\ge |X_1|}\frac1{n^2}\right]<\infty.\]

Appliquons l'outil à \(Z_n=(Y_n-EY_n)/n\). Alors \(\sum_n(Y_n-EY_n)/n\) converge p.s., et le lemme de Kronecker donne \(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^n(Y_k-EY_k)\to0\) p.s.

Enfin, \(EY_n\to EX_1=\mu\) par convergence dominée, donc \(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^nEY_k\to\mu\) par moyenne de Cesàro. En combinant les trois termes, \(S_n/n\to\mu\) p.s.

强大数定律

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/borel-cantelli-lemma/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/central-limit-theorem/" %}

强大数定律把依概率收敛加强为：除去一个零概率集合后，整条样本路径都收敛。

定义. 若 \(P(\{\omega:X_n(\omega)\to X(\omega)\})=1\)，则称 \(X_n\to X\) 几乎必然收敛。

定理：独立同分布强大数定律. 设 \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\) 独立同分布，\(E|X_1|<\infty\)，且 \(EX_1=\mu\)。若 \(S_n=\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\)，则 \(S_n/n\to\mu\) 几乎必然。

证明. 工具。 若独立中心化随机变量 \(Z_n\) 满足 \(\sum_n\operatorname{Var}(Z_n)<\infty\)，则 \(\sum_nZ_n\) 几乎必然收敛。事实上，由 Kolmogorov 最大不等式，对 \(N\ge m\)，

\[P\left(\max_{m\le k\le N}\left|\sum_{n=m}^kZ_n\right|>\varepsilon\right)\le \varepsilon^{-2}\sum_{n=m}^N\operatorname{Var}(Z_n).\]

令 \(N\to\infty\)，再取 \(m_j\) 使尾部方差不超过 \(2^{-j}\varepsilon^2\)，由 Borel-Cantelli 引理可知尾部最终都小于 \(\varepsilon\)。令 \(\varepsilon\) 取遍正有理数，部分和几乎必然为 Cauchy 列。

Kronecker 引理。 若 \(\sum_na_n/n\) 收敛，则 \(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^na_k\to0\)。令 \(t_n=\sum_{k=1}^na_k/k\)。由于 \(a_k=k(t_k-t_{k-1})\)，分部求和给出 \(\sum_{k=1}^na_k=nt_n-\sum_{k=1}^{n-1}t_k\)。两边除以 \(n\)，由 Cesàro 平均可知两项趋于同一极限。

令 \(Y_n=X_n1_{\{|X_n|\le n\}}\)。因为

\[\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P(X_n\ne Y_n)=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}P(|X_1|>n)\le E|X_1|<\infty,\]

由 Borel-Cantelli 引理，\(X_n=Y_n\) 最终几乎必然成立。因此 \(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^n(X_k-Y_k)\to0\) 几乎必然。

另一方面，

\[\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{\operatorname{Var}(Y_n)}{n^2}\le \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{E[Y_n^2]}{n^2}=E\left[X_1^2\sum_{n\ge |X_1|}\frac1{n^2}\right]<\infty.\]

对 \(Z_n=(Y_n-EY_n)/n\) 应用上面的工具，得到 \(\sum_n(Y_n-EY_n)/n\) 几乎必然收敛。由 Kronecker 引理，\(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^n(Y_k-EY_k)\to0\) 几乎必然。

最后，由控制收敛定理 \(EY_n\to EX_1=\mu\)，所以 Cesàro 平均给出 \(n^{-1}\sum_{k=1}^nEY_k\to\mu\)。合并这三部分，得到 \(S_n/n\to\mu\) 几乎必然。
