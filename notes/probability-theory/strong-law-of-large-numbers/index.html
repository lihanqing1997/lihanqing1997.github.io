Strong Law of Large Numbers

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/borel-cantelli-lemma/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/central-limit-theorem/" %}

The strong law strengthens convergence in probability to pathwise convergence outside a null set.

Definition

\(X_n\to X\) almost surely if \(P(\{\omega:X_n(\omega)\to X(\omega)\})=1\).

Theorem: finite-variance strong law

Let \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\) be i.i.d. with \(EX_1=\mu\) and \(\operatorname{Var}(X_1)=\sigma^2<\infty\). If \(S_n=\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\), then \(S_n/n\to\mu\) almost surely.

Proof

It is enough to prove the result for \(EX_i=0\); apply that to \(X_i-\mu\). Write \(S_n=\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\).

First prove a maximal estimate. For independent mean-zero variables and \(M_n=\max_{1\le k\le n}|S_k|\), define \(B_k=\{M_{k-1}<\lambda,\ |S_k|\ge\lambda\}\). The \(B_k\) are disjoint and \(\{M_n\ge\lambda\}=\bigcup_{k=1}^nB_k\). Since \(B_k\in\sigma(X_1,\ldots,X_k)\) and \(E(S_n-S_k\mid X_1,\ldots,X_k)=0\),

\[E[S_n^2 1_{B_k}]\ge E[S_k^2 1_{B_k}]\ge \lambda^2P(B_k).\]

Summing in \(k\) gives \(P(M_n\ge\lambda)\le ES_n^2/\lambda^2=n\sigma^2/\lambda^2\).

Now use dyadic blocks. By Chebyshev, \(\sum_mP(|S_{2^m}|>\varepsilon 2^m)\le\sum_m\sigma^2/(\varepsilon^2 2^m)<\infty\), so \(S_{2^m}/2^m\to0\) almost surely by Borel-Cantelli.

The maximal estimate gives

\[\sum_mP\left(\max_{2^m\varepsilon 2^m\right)\le\sum_m\frac{\sigma^2}{\varepsilon^2 2^m}<\infty.\]

Loi forte des grands nombres

La loi forte renforce la convergence en probabilité en convergence trajectorielle hors d'un ensemble nul.

Définition

\(X_n\to X\) presque sûrement si \(P(\{\omega:X_n(\omega)\to X(\omega)\})=1\).

强大数定律

强大数定律把依概率收敛加强为除去零概率集合后的逐点收敛。

定义

若 \(P(\{\omega:X_n(\omega)\to X(\omega)\})=1\)，则称 \(X_n\to X\) 几乎处处收敛。

定理：有限方差形式

设 \(X_1,X_2,\ldots\) 独立同分布，\(EX_1=\mu\)，\(\operatorname{Var}(X_1)=\sigma^2<\infty\)。若 \(S_n=\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\)，则 \(S_n/n\to\mu\) 几乎处处。

证明

只需证明 \(EX_i=0\) 的情形；一般情形对 \(X_i-\mu\) 应用即可。记 \(S_n=\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\)。

\[E[S_n^2 1_{B_k}]\ge E[S_k^2 1_{B_k}]\ge \lambda^2P(B_k).\]

对 \(k\) 求和得到 \(P(M_n\ge\lambda)\le ES_n^2/\lambda^2=n\sigma^2/\lambda^2\)。
