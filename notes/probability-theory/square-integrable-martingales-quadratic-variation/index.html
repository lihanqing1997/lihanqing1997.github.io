26. Square-integrable martingales and quadratic variation

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/uniform-integrability-martingale-convergence/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/martingale-clt/" %}

Definition. For a square-integrable martingale \(M_n\), the predictable quadratic variation is \[ \langle M\rangle_n=\sum_{k=1}^n\mathbb E[(M_k-M_{k-1})^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}]. \] The optional quadratic variation is \([M]_n=\sum_{k=1}^n(M_k-M_{k-1})^2\).

Proposition. If \(M_n\) is square-integrable, then \(M_n^2-\langle M\rangle_n\) is a martingale.

Proof. Let \(D_k=M_k-M_{k-1}\). Since \(M_k=M_{k-1}+D_k\) and \(\mathbb E[D_k\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}]=0\), \[ \mathbb E[M_k^2-M_{k-1}^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}] =\mathbb E[2M_{k-1}D_k+D_k^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}] =\mathbb E[D_k^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}]. \] Subtracting the increment of \(\langle M\rangle\) gives conditional mean zero, so \(M_n^2-\langle M\rangle_n\) is a martingale.

Quadratic variation as conditional variance

Quadratic variation measures the accumulated conditional variance of martingale increments.

26. Martingales de carré intégrable et variation quadratique

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/uniform-integrability-martingale-convergence/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/martingale-clt/" %}

Définition. Pour une martingale de carré intégrable \(M_n\), la variation quadratique prévisible est \[ \langle M\rangle_n=\sum_{k=1}^n\mathbb E[(M_k-M_{k-1})^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}]. \] La variation quadratique optionnelle est \([M]_n=\sum_{k=1}^n(M_k-M_{k-1})^2\).

Proposition. Si \(M_n\) est de carré intégrable, alors \(M_n^2-\langle M\rangle_n\) est une martingale.

Preuve. Posons \(D_k=M_k-M_{k-1}\). Comme \(M_k=M_{k-1}+D_k\) et \(\mathbb E[D_k\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}]=0\), \[ \mathbb E[M_k^2-M_{k-1}^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}] =\mathbb E[2M_{k-1}D_k+D_k^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}] =\mathbb E[D_k^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}]. \] En soustrayant l’accroissement de \(\langle M\rangle\), on obtient une espérance conditionnelle nulle, donc \(M_n^2-\langle M\rangle_n\) est une martingale.

Variation quadratique comme variance conditionnelle

La variation quadratique mesure la variance conditionnelle accumulée des accroissements de martingale.

26. 平方可积鞅与二次变差

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/uniform-integrability-martingale-convergence/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/martingale-clt/" %}

定义. 对平方可积鞅 \(M_n\)，可预测二次变差定义为 \[ \langle M\rangle_n=\sum_{k=1}^n\mathbb E[(M_k-M_{k-1})^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}]. \] 可选二次变差为 \([M]_n=\sum_{k=1}^n(M_k-M_{k-1})^2\)。

命题. 若 \(M_n\) 平方可积，则 \(M_n^2-\langle M\rangle_n\) 是鞅。

证明. 令 \(D_k=M_k-M_{k-1}\)。因为 \(M_k=M_{k-1}+D_k\)，且 \(\mathbb E[D_k\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}]=0\)， \[ \mathbb E[M_k^2-M_{k-1}^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}] =\mathbb E[2M_{k-1}D_k+D_k^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}] =\mathbb E[D_k^2\mid\mathcal F_{k-1}]. \] 减去 \(\langle M\rangle\) 的对应增量后，条件期望为零，因此 \(M_n^2-\langle M\rangle_n\) 是鞅。

作为条件方差的二次变差

二次变差刻画鞅增量条件方差的累积。
