52. Stochastic differential equations

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/girsanov-theorem-change-of-measure/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/local-time-tanaka-formula/" %}

Stochastic differential equations define processes through drift and diffusion coefficients. They combine ordinary dynamics with Brownian noise.

Strong solutions

A strong solution is adapted to the Brownian filtration and satisfies the integral equation.

Definition. For coefficients \(b,\sigma\), an SDE \[ \mathrm{d}X_t=b(t,X_t)\,\mathrm{d}t+\sigma(t,X_t)\,\mathrm{d}B_t \] means \[ X_t=X_0+\int_0^tb(s,X_s)\,\mathrm{d}s+\int_0^t\sigma(s,X_s)\,\mathrm{d}B_s. \]

Existence and uniqueness

Lipschitz and linear-growth conditions give well-posedness.

Theorem. If \(b,\sigma\) are globally Lipschitz in \(x\) and satisfy linear growth, then the SDE has a unique strong solution, and solutions depend continuously on the initial condition in \(L^2\) on compact time intervals.

Proof. Use Picard iteration: start with \(X^0_t=X_0\) and define \(X^{n+1}\) by inserting \(X^n\) into the drift and diffusion integrals. Itô isometry and Cauchy-Schwarz give an \(L^2\) bound for \(\sup_{s\le t}|X^{n+1}_s-X^n_s|\) controlled by an integral of the previous difference. Iteration and Gronwall-type estimates give convergence. The same estimates applied to two solutions give uniqueness.

Generator

The SDE generator is the differential operator governing expectations.

Proposition. For \(f\in C_b^2\), the generator of the diffusion is \[ Lf=b\cdot\nabla f+\frac12(\sigma\sigma^\top):D^2f. \]

Proof. Apply Itô's formula to \(f(X_t)\). The drift term is exactly \(Lf(X_t)\,\mathrm{d}t\), and the remaining stochastic integral has mean zero after localization. Therefore \(\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t}\mathbb E_x f(X_t)|_{t=0}=Lf(x)\).

Remark. Weak solutions specify the law of the process; strong solutions specify the process as a functional of a given Brownian motion.
