Brownian martingales

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/brownian-strong-markov-property/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/donsker-theorem-brownian-bridge/" %}

Definition. Brownian martingales are martingales built from Brownian motion and its natural filtration \(\mathcal F_t=\sigma(B_s:s\le t)\).

Proposition. The processes \(B_t\), \(B_t^2-t\), and \(\exp(\theta B_t-\theta^2t/2)\) are martingales.

Proof. For \(s\lt t\), write \(B_t=B_s+(B_t-B_s)\). The increment is independent of \(\mathcal F_s\) and has mean zero, so \(\mathbb E[B_t\mid\mathcal F_s]=B_s\). Also \[ \mathbb E[B_t^2-t\mid\mathcal F_s] =B_s^2+\mathbb E(B_t-B_s)^2-t =B_s^2+(t-s)-t=B_s^2-s. \] Finally, using the Gaussian moment generating function, \[ \mathbb E[e^{\theta(B_t-B_s)-\theta^2(t-s)/2}]=1, \] which gives the exponential martingale by independence.

Remark

These martingales are the entry point to optional stopping, hitting-time computations, and Girsanov transforms.

Martingales browniennes

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/brownian-strong-markov-property/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/donsker-theorem-brownian-bridge/" %}

Définition. Les martingales browniennes sont des martingales construites à partir du mouvement brownien et de sa filtration naturelle \(\mathcal F_t=\sigma(B_s:s\le t)\).

Proposition. Les processus \(B_t\), \(B_t^2-t\), et \(\exp(\theta B_t-\theta^2t/2)\) sont des martingales.

Preuve. Pour \(s\lt t\), écrivons \(B_t=B_s+(B_t-B_s)\). L’accroissement est indépendant de \(\mathcal F_s\) et de moyenne nulle, donc \(\mathbb E[B_t\mid\mathcal F_s]=B_s\). De plus, \[ \mathbb E[B_t^2-t\mid\mathcal F_s] =B_s^2+\mathbb E(B_t-B_s)^2-t =B_s^2+(t-s)-t=B_s^2-s. \] Enfin, la fonction génératrice gaussienne donne \[ \mathbb E[e^{\theta(B_t-B_s)-\theta^2(t-s)/2}]=1, \] ce qui donne la martingale exponentielle par indépendance.

Remarque

Ces martingales ouvrent la porte à l’arrêt optionnel, aux calculs de temps d’atteinte et aux transformations de Girsanov.

布朗鞅

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/brownian-strong-markov-property/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/donsker-theorem-brownian-bridge/" %}

定义. Brownian 鞅是由 Brownian motion 及其自然滤过 \(\mathcal F_t=\sigma(B_s:s\le t)\) 构造的鞅。

命题. 过程 \(B_t\)、\(B_t^2-t\)、\(\exp(\theta B_t-\theta^2t/2)\) 都是鞅。

证明. 对 \(s\lt t\)，写 \(B_t=B_s+(B_t-B_s)\)。增量独立于 \(\mathcal F_s\)，且均值为零，所以 \(\mathbb E[B_t\mid\mathcal F_s]=B_s\)。并且 \[ \mathbb E[B_t^2-t\mid\mathcal F_s] =B_s^2+\mathbb E(B_t-B_s)^2-t =B_s^2+(t-s)-t=B_s^2-s. \] 最后，由 Gaussian 矩母函数， \[ \mathbb E[e^{\theta(B_t-B_s)-\theta^2(t-s)/2}]=1, \] 再用独立性得到指数鞅。

说明

这些鞅是 optional stopping、击中时刻计算和 Girsanov 变换的入口。
