Filtrations, adapted processes, and stopping times

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/stable-infinitely-divisible-laws/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/discrete-time-martingales/" %}

Definition. A filtration is an increasing family of \(\sigma\)-fields \((\mathcal F_n)_{n\ge0}\), meaning \(\mathcal F_n\subset\mathcal F_{n+1}\). A process \(X_n\) is adapted if \(X_n\) is \(\mathcal F_n\)-measurable. A random time \(\tau:\Omega\to\{0,1,2,\ldots,\infty\}\) is a stopping time if \(\{\tau\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\) for every \(n\).

Proposition. If \(\tau\) and \(\sigma\) are stopping times, then \(\tau\wedge\sigma\), \(\tau\vee\sigma\), and \(\tau+\sigma\) are stopping times.

Proof. For the minimum, \(\{\tau\wedge\sigma\le n\}=\{\tau\le n\}\cup\{\sigma\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\). For the maximum, \(\{\tau\vee\sigma\le n\}=\{\tau\le n\}\cap\{\sigma\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\). For the sum, \[ \{\tau+\sigma\le n\}=\bigcup_{k=0}^n\{\tau=k\}\cap\{\sigma\le n-k\}. \] Here \(\{\tau=k\}\in\mathcal F_k\subset\mathcal F_n\) and \(\{\sigma\le n-k\}\in\mathcal F_{n-k}\subset\mathcal F_n\), so the finite union is in \(\mathcal F_n\).

Deciding whether a stopping time occurred

The condition \(\{\tau\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\) says that by time \(n\) one can decide whether the time has already occurred.

Filtrations, processus adaptés et temps d’arrêt

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/stable-infinitely-divisible-laws/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/discrete-time-martingales/" %}

Définition. Une filtration est une famille croissante de \(\sigma\)-algèbres \((\mathcal F_n)_{n\ge0}\), c’est-à-dire \(\mathcal F_n\subset\mathcal F_{n+1}\). Un processus \(X_n\) est adapté si \(X_n\) est \(\mathcal F_n\)-mesurable. Un temps aléatoire \(\tau:\Omega\to\{0,1,2,\ldots,\infty\}\) est un temps d’arrêt si \(\{\tau\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\) pour tout \(n\).

Proposition. Si \(\tau\) et \(\sigma\) sont des temps d’arrêt, alors \(\tau\wedge\sigma\), \(\tau\vee\sigma\), et \(\tau+\sigma\) sont des temps d’arrêt.

Preuve. Pour le minimum, \(\{\tau\wedge\sigma\le n\}=\{\tau\le n\}\cup\{\sigma\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\). Pour le maximum, \(\{\tau\vee\sigma\le n\}=\{\tau\le n\}\cap\{\sigma\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\). Pour la somme, \[ \{\tau+\sigma\le n\}=\bigcup_{k=0}^n\{\tau=k\}\cap\{\sigma\le n-k\}. \] On a \(\{\tau=k\}\in\mathcal F_k\subset\mathcal F_n\) et \(\{\sigma\le n-k\}\in\mathcal F_{n-k}\subset\mathcal F_n\), donc l’union finie appartient à \(\mathcal F_n\).

Décider si un temps d’arrêt est arrivé

La condition \(\{\tau\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\) signifie qu’au temps \(n\), on peut savoir si le temps aléatoire s’est déjà produit.

滤过、适应过程与停时

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/stable-infinitely-divisible-laws/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/discrete-time-martingales/" %}

定义. 滤过是递增的 \(\sigma\)-代数族 \((\mathcal F_n)_{n\ge0}\)，即 \(\mathcal F_n\subset\mathcal F_{n+1}\)。过程 \(X_n\) 称为适应的，若 \(X_n\) 是 \(\mathcal F_n\)-可测。随机时间 \(\tau:\Omega\to\{0,1,2,\ldots,\infty\}\) 称为停时，若对每个 \(n\)，\(\{\tau\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\)。

命题. 若 \(\tau\) 与 \(\sigma\) 是停时，则 \(\tau\wedge\sigma\)、\(\tau\vee\sigma\)、\(\tau+\sigma\) 都是停时。

证明. 对最小值，\(\{\tau\wedge\sigma\le n\}=\{\tau\le n\}\cup\{\sigma\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\)。对最大值，\(\{\tau\vee\sigma\le n\}=\{\tau\le n\}\cap\{\sigma\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\)。对和， \[ \{\tau+\sigma\le n\}=\bigcup_{k=0}^n\{\tau=k\}\cap\{\sigma\le n-k\}. \] 其中 \(\{\tau=k\}\in\mathcal F_k\subset\mathcal F_n\)，且 \(\{\sigma\le n-k\}\in\mathcal F_{n-k}\subset\mathcal F_n\)，故有限并属于 \(\mathcal F_n\)。

判断停时是否已经发生

条件 \(\{\tau\le n\}\in\mathcal F_n\) 的意思是：到时间 \(n\) 为止，我们已经能判断该随机时间是否已经发生。
