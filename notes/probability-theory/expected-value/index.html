5. Lebesgue integration and expectation

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/independence/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/lp-spaces-inequalities-uniform-integrability/" %}

Definition. For \(X\ge0\), the expectation is the Lebesgue integral \[ \mathbb E X=\int_\Omega X\,\mathrm{d}P =\sup\left\{\int_\Omega s\,\mathrm{d}P:0\le s\le X,\ s\text{ simple}\right\}. \] For a real \(X\), write \(X=X^+-X^-\). If \(\mathbb E X^+\lt \infty\) and \(\mathbb E X^-\lt \infty\), then \(X\in L^1\) and \(\mathbb E X=\mathbb E X^+-\mathbb E X^-\).

Theorem. If \(0\le X_n\uparrow X\) almost surely, then \(\mathbb E X_n\uparrow \mathbb E X\). This is the monotone convergence theorem.

Proof. The sequence \(\mathbb E X_n\) is increasing and bounded above by \(\mathbb E X\). Let \(s\) be a simple function with \(0\le s\le X\), and fix \(0\lt c\lt 1\). The sets \(A_n=\{X_n\ge cs\}\) increase to \(\Omega\) on \(\{s\gt 0\}\). Hence \(\int cs\,1_{A_n}\,\mathrm{d}P\to\int cs\,\mathrm{d}P\). Since \(cs\,1_{A_n}\le X_n\), we get \(\lim_n\mathbb E X_n\ge c\int s\,\mathrm{d}P\). Letting \(c\uparrow1\) and taking the supremum over all such \(s\) gives \(\lim_n\mathbb E X_n\ge\mathbb E X\). The reverse inequality is immediate from \(X_n\le X\).

Monotone convergence as the base theorem

Fatou’s lemma and dominated convergence are best understood as refinements of this theorem for limits that are not monotone.

5. Intégration de Lebesgue et espérance

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/independence/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/lp-spaces-inequalities-uniform-integrability/" %}

Définition. Pour \(X\ge0\), l’espérance est l’intégrale de Lebesgue \[ \mathbb E X=\int_\Omega X\,\mathrm{d}P =\sup\left\{\int_\Omega s\,\mathrm{d}P:0\le s\le X,\ s\text{ simple}\right\}. \] Pour une variable réelle \(X\), on écrit \(X=X^+-X^-\). Si \(\mathbb E X^+\lt \infty\) et \(\mathbb E X^-\lt \infty\), alors \(X\in L^1\) et \(\mathbb E X=\mathbb E X^+-\mathbb E X^-\).

Théorème. Si \(0\le X_n\uparrow X\) presque sûrement, alors \(\mathbb E X_n\uparrow \mathbb E X\). C’est le théorème de convergence monotone.

Preuve. La suite \(\mathbb E X_n\) est croissante et majorée par \(\mathbb E X\). Soit \(s\) simple, \(0\le s\le X\), et soit \(0\lt c\lt 1\). Les ensembles \(A_n=\{X_n\ge cs\}\) croissent vers \(\Omega\) sur \(\{s\gt 0\}\). Donc \(\int cs\,1_{A_n}\,\mathrm{d}P\to\int cs\,\mathrm{d}P\). Comme \(cs\,1_{A_n}\le X_n\), on obtient \(\lim_n\mathbb E X_n\ge c\int s\,\mathrm{d}P\). En faisant \(c\uparrow1\), puis en prenant le supremum sur \(s\), on a \(\lim_n\mathbb E X_n\ge\mathbb E X\). L’inégalité inverse vient de \(X_n\le X\).

Convergence monotone comme théorème de base

Le lemme de Fatou et la convergence dominée sont des raffinements de ce résultat lorsque la convergence n’est pas monotone.

5. 勒贝格积分与期望

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/independence/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/lp-spaces-inequalities-uniform-integrability/" %}

定义. 对 \(X\ge0\)，期望定义为勒贝格积分 \[ \mathbb E X=\int_\Omega X\,\mathrm{d}P =\sup\left\{\int_\Omega s\,\mathrm{d}P:0\le s\le X,\ s\text{ 为简单函数}\right\}. \] 对实值 \(X\)，写 \(X=X^+-X^-\)。若 \(\mathbb E X^+\lt \infty\) 且 \(\mathbb E X^-\lt \infty\)，则 \(X\in L^1\)，并定义 \(\mathbb E X=\mathbb E X^+-\mathbb E X^-\)。

定理. 若 \(0\le X_n\uparrow X\) 几乎处处成立，则 \(\mathbb E X_n\uparrow \mathbb E X\)。这就是单调收敛定理。

证明. \(\mathbb E X_n\) 递增且不超过 \(\mathbb E X\)。取简单函数 \(s\)，满足 \(0\le s\le X\)，并取 \(0\lt c\lt 1\)。令 \(A_n=\{X_n\ge cs\}\)，则在 \(\{s\gt 0\}\) 上有 \(A_n\uparrow\Omega\)。因此 \(\int cs\,1_{A_n}\,\mathrm{d}P\to\int cs\,\mathrm{d}P\)。由于 \(cs\,1_{A_n}\le X_n\)，得到 \(\lim_n\mathbb E X_n\ge c\int s\,\mathrm{d}P\)。令 \(c\uparrow1\)，再对所有这样的 \(s\) 取上确界，得 \(\lim_n\mathbb E X_n\ge\mathbb E X\)。反向不等式由 \(X_n\le X\) 直接得到。

作为基础定理的单调收敛

Fatou 引理和控制收敛定理可以看成单调收敛定理在非单调极限情形下的改进。
