Poisson convergence and Poisson processes

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/berry-esseen-rates/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/stable-infinitely-divisible-laws/" %}

Definition. A Poisson random variable with parameter \(\lambda\gt 0\) satisfies \(P(N=k)=e^{-\lambda}\lambda^k/k!\). A Poisson process \((N_t)_{t\ge0}\) has \(N_0=0\), independent increments, and \(N_t-N_s\sim\operatorname{Poisson}(\lambda(t-s))\) for \(0\le s\lt t\).

Theorem. If \(X_n\sim\operatorname{Binomial}(n,p_n)\) and \(np_n\to\lambda\), then \(X_n\Rightarrow\operatorname{Poisson}(\lambda)\).

Proof. For each fixed \(k\), \[ P(X_n=k)=\binom nkp_n^k(1-p_n)^{n-k}. \] Since \(p_n\to0\) and \(np_n\to\lambda\), we have \(\binom nkp_n^k\to\lambda^k/k!\) and \((1-p_n)^{n-k}\to e^{-\lambda}\). Thus point probabilities converge to the Poisson probabilities. Since the limiting probabilities sum to \(1\), this gives convergence in distribution.

Rare-event limits

Poisson limits describe many rare nearly independent events observed over a large population or long time interval.

Convergence de Poisson et processus de Poisson

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/berry-esseen-rates/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/stable-infinitely-divisible-laws/" %}

Définition. Une variable de Poisson de paramètre \(\lambda\gt 0\) vérifie \(P(N=k)=e^{-\lambda}\lambda^k/k!\). Un processus de Poisson \((N_t)_{t\ge0}\) vérifie \(N_0=0\), a des accroissements indépendants, et \(N_t-N_s\sim\operatorname{Poisson}(\lambda(t-s))\) pour \(0\le s\lt t\).

Théorème. Si \(X_n\sim\operatorname{Binomial}(n,p_n)\) et \(np_n\to\lambda\), alors \(X_n\Rightarrow\operatorname{Poisson}(\lambda)\).

Preuve. Pour \(k\) fixé, \[ P(X_n=k)=\binom nkp_n^k(1-p_n)^{n-k}. \] Comme \(p_n\to0\) et \(np_n\to\lambda\), on a \(\binom nkp_n^k\to\lambda^k/k!\) et \((1-p_n)^{n-k}\to e^{-\lambda}\). Les probabilités ponctuelles convergent donc vers celles de la loi de Poisson. Comme la somme des probabilités limites vaut \(1\), on obtient la convergence en loi.

Limites d’événements rares

Les limites de Poisson décrivent des événements rares presque indépendants observés dans une grande population ou sur un long intervalle de temps.

Poisson 收敛与 Poisson 过程

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/berry-esseen-rates/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/stable-infinitely-divisible-laws/" %}

定义. 参数为 \(\lambda\gt 0\) 的 Poisson 随机变量满足 \(P(N=k)=e^{-\lambda}\lambda^k/k!\)。Poisson 过程 \((N_t)_{t\ge0}\) 满足 \(N_0=0\)，具有独立增量，并且对 \(0\le s\lt t\)，\(N_t-N_s\sim\operatorname{Poisson}(\lambda(t-s))\)。

定理. 若 \(X_n\sim\operatorname{Binomial}(n,p_n)\) 且 \(np_n\to\lambda\)，则 \(X_n\Rightarrow\operatorname{Poisson}(\lambda)\)。

证明. 对固定 \(k\)， \[ P(X_n=k)=\binom nkp_n^k(1-p_n)^{n-k}. \] 因为 \(p_n\to0\) 且 \(np_n\to\lambda\)，有 \(\binom nkp_n^k\to\lambda^k/k!\)，并且 \((1-p_n)^{n-k}\to e^{-\lambda}\)。所以点概率收敛到 Poisson 概率。由于极限概率总和为 \(1\)，得到依分布收敛。

稀有事件极限

Poisson 极限刻画大量或长时间区间中罕见、近似独立的事件。
