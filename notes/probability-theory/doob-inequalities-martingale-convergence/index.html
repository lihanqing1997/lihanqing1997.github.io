Doob inequalities and martingale convergence

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/discrete-time-martingales/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/optional-stopping-sampling/" %}

Definition. For a submartingale \(X_n\), define the running maximum \(X_n^*=\max_{0\le k\le n}|X_k|\). Doob inequalities control this maximum using the terminal variable.

Theorem. If \(M_n\) is a nonnegative submartingale, then for \(\lambda\gt 0\), \[ \lambda P\left(\max_{k\le n}M_k\ge\lambda\right)\le \mathbb E[M_n1_{\{\max_{k\le n}M_k\ge\lambda\}}]\le \mathbb E M_n. \] If \(M_n\) is an \(L^p\)-martingale, \(p\gt 1\), then \(\|M_n^*\|_p\le \frac{p}{p-1}\|M_n\|_p\).

Proof. Let \(\tau=\inf\{k\le n:M_k\ge\lambda\}\), with \(\tau=n+1\) if the set is empty. On \(\{\tau\le n\}\), \(M_\tau\ge\lambda\). Optional sampling for the bounded stopping time \(\tau\wedge n\) and the submartingale property give \(\mathbb E[M_n\mid\mathcal F_\tau]\ge M_\tau\) on \(\{\tau\le n\}\). Hence \[ \lambda P(\tau\le n)\le\mathbb E[M_\tau1_{\{\tau\le n\}}]\le\mathbb E[M_n1_{\{\tau\le n\}}]. \] The \(L^p\) inequality follows by applying this tail bound to the nonnegative submartingale \(|M_k|\) and integrating \(p\lambda^{p-1}P(M_n^*\gt \lambda)\,\mathrm{d}\lambda\), then using Hölder’s inequality.

Maximal control of martingale paths

Maximal inequalities are the bridge from pointwise \(L^p\) bounds to pathwise control of the whole martingale history.

Inégalités de Doob et convergence des martingales

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/discrete-time-martingales/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/optional-stopping-sampling/" %}

Définition. Pour une sous-martingale \(X_n\), on note \(X_n^*=\max_{0\le k\le n}|X_k|\). Les inégalités de Doob contrôlent ce maximum à partir de la variable terminale.

Théorème. Si \(M_n\) est une sous-martingale positive, alors, pour \(\lambda\gt 0\), \[ \lambda P\left(\max_{k\le n}M_k\ge\lambda\right)\le \mathbb E[M_n1_{\{\max_{k\le n}M_k\ge\lambda\}}]\le \mathbb E M_n. \] Si \(M_n\) est une martingale dans \(L^p\), \(p\gt 1\), alors \(\|M_n^*\|_p\le \frac{p}{p-1}\|M_n\|_p\).

Preuve. Soit \(\tau=\inf\{k\le n:M_k\ge\lambda\}\), avec \(\tau=n+1\) si l’ensemble est vide. Sur \(\{\tau\le n\}\), \(M_\tau\ge\lambda\). L’échantillonnage optionnel pour le temps borné \(\tau\wedge n\), avec la propriété de sous-martingale, donne \(\mathbb E[M_n\mid\mathcal F_\tau]\ge M_\tau\) sur \(\{\tau\le n\}\). Donc \[ \lambda P(\tau\le n)\le\mathbb E[M_\tau1_{\{\tau\le n\}}]\le\mathbb E[M_n1_{\{\tau\le n\}}]. \] L’inégalité \(L^p\) s’obtient en appliquant cette borne de queue à la sous-martingale positive \(|M_k|\), puis en intégrant \(p\lambda^{p-1}P(M_n^*\gt \lambda)\,\mathrm{d}\lambda\) et en utilisant Hölder.

Contrôle maximal des trajectoires de martingales

Les inégalités maximales transforment un contrôle de la variable terminale en contrôle de toute l’histoire de la martingale.

Doob 不等式与鞅收敛

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/discrete-time-martingales/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/optional-stopping-sampling/" %}

定义. 对下鞅 \(X_n\)，记运行最大值 \(X_n^*=\max_{0\le k\le n}|X_k|\)。Doob 不等式用终端变量控制这个最大值。

定理. 若 \(M_n\) 是非负下鞅，则对 \(\lambda\gt 0\)， \[ \lambda P\left(\max_{k\le n}M_k\ge\lambda\right)\le \mathbb E[M_n1_{\{\max_{k\le n}M_k\ge\lambda\}}]\le \mathbb E M_n. \] 若 \(M_n\) 是 \(L^p\)-鞅，\(p\gt 1\)，则 \(\|M_n^*\|_p\le \frac{p}{p-1}\|M_n\|_p\)。

证明. 令 \(\tau=\inf\{k\le n:M_k\ge\lambda\}\)，若集合为空则令 \(\tau=n+1\)。在 \(\{\tau\le n\}\) 上，\(M_\tau\ge\lambda\)。对有界停时 \(\tau\wedge n\) 用 optional sampling，并用下鞅性质得到在 \(\{\tau\le n\}\) 上 \(\mathbb E[M_n\mid\mathcal F_\tau]\ge M_\tau\)。因此 \[ \lambda P(\tau\le n)\le\mathbb E[M_\tau1_{\{\tau\le n\}}]\le\mathbb E[M_n1_{\{\tau\le n\}}]. \] \(L^p\) 不等式由此尾界作用于非负下鞅 \(|M_k|\)，再积分 \(p\lambda^{p-1}P(M_n^*\gt \lambda)\,\mathrm{d}\lambda\)，最后用 Hölder 不等式得到。

鞅路径的最大控制

最大不等式把终端时刻的 \(L^p\) 控制转化为整条鞅路径的控制。
