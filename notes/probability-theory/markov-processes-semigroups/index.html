31. Markov processes and semigroups

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/markov-chains-transition-kernels/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/recurrence-transience-invariant-measures/" %}

Definition. A Markov semigroup is a family \(P_t\) of kernels or operators satisfying \(P_0=I\) and \(P_{s+t}=P_sP_t\). For a Markov process, \(P_tf(x)=\mathbb E_x f(X_t)\).

Proposition. If \(X_t\) is Markov with transition operators \(P_t\), then \(P_{s+t}=P_sP_t\).

Proof. For bounded measurable \(f\), \[ P_{s+t}f(x)=\mathbb E_x f(X_{s+t}) =\mathbb E_x[\mathbb E(f(X_{s+t})\mid\mathcal F_s)]. \] By the Markov property, the conditional expectation equals \(P_tf(X_s)\). Taking expectation gives \(\mathbb E_xP_tf(X_s)=P_s(P_tf)(x)\).

Semigroup as time evolution

The semigroup viewpoint turns probabilistic time evolution into operator composition.

31. Processus de Markov et semi-groupes

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/markov-chains-transition-kernels/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/recurrence-transience-invariant-measures/" %}

Définition. Un semi-groupe de Markov est une famille \(P_t\) de noyaux ou d’opérateurs telle que \(P_0=I\) et \(P_{s+t}=P_sP_t\). Pour un processus de Markov, \(P_tf(x)=\mathbb E_x f(X_t)\).

Proposition. Si \(X_t\) est markovien de transition \(P_t\), alors \(P_{s+t}=P_sP_t\).

Preuve. Pour \(f\) mesurable bornée, \[ P_{s+t}f(x)=\mathbb E_x f(X_{s+t}) =\mathbb E_x[\mathbb E(f(X_{s+t})\mid\mathcal F_s)]. \] Par la propriété de Markov, l’espérance conditionnelle vaut \(P_tf(X_s)\). En prenant l’espérance, on obtient \(\mathbb E_xP_tf(X_s)=P_s(P_tf)(x)\).

Semi-groupe comme évolution temporelle

Le point de vue semi-groupe transforme l’évolution probabiliste en composition d’opérateurs.

31. 马尔可夫过程与半群

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/markov-chains-transition-kernels/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/recurrence-transience-invariant-measures/" %}

定义. 马尔可夫半群是核或算子族 \(P_t\)，满足 \(P_0=I\) 和 \(P_{s+t}=P_sP_t\)。对马尔可夫过程，\(P_tf(x)=\mathbb E_x f(X_t)\)。

命题. 若 \(X_t\) 是转移算子为 \(P_t\) 的马尔可夫过程，则 \(P_{s+t}=P_sP_t\)。

证明. 对有界可测 \(f\)， \[ P_{s+t}f(x)=\mathbb E_x f(X_{s+t}) =\mathbb E_x[\mathbb E(f(X_{s+t})\mid\mathcal F_s)]. \] 由马尔可夫性，条件期望等于 \(P_tf(X_s)\)。再取期望得到 \(\mathbb E_xP_tf(X_s)=P_s(P_tf)(x)\)。

作为时间演化的半群

半群观点把概率时间演化转化为算子的复合。
