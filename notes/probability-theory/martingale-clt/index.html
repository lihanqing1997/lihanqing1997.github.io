Martingale CLT and applications

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Back" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/square-integrable-martingales-quadratic-variation/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/kolmogorov-extension/" %}

Definition. A martingale difference array is a triangular array \(D_{n,k}\) with \(\mathbb E[D_{n,k}\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]=0\). Its conditional variance process is \(V_n=\sum_k\mathbb E[D_{n,k}^2\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]\).

Theorem. If \(V_n\to\sigma^2\) in probability and the conditional Lindeberg condition \[ \sum_k\mathbb E[D_{n,k}^2 1_{\{|D_{n,k}|\gt \varepsilon\}}\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]\to0 \] holds in probability for every \(\varepsilon\gt 0\), then \(\sum_kD_{n,k}\Rightarrow N(0,\sigma^2)\).

Proof. Use conditional characteristic functions. For small \(u\), \[ \mathbb E[e^{iuD_{n,k}}\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}] =1-\frac{u^2}{2}\mathbb E[D_{n,k}^2\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]+r_{n,k}. \] The conditional Lindeberg condition makes \(\sum_k r_{n,k}\to0\) in probability. Multiplying the conditional characteristic functions gives \[ \exp\left(-\frac{u^2}{2}V_n+o_P(1)\right)\to e^{-u^2\sigma^2/2}. \] Lévy’s theorem gives the normal limit.

Remark

The martingale CLT replaces independence by conditional mean zero plus conditional variance convergence.

TCL pour martingales et applications

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="Retour" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/square-integrable-martingales-quadratic-variation/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/kolmogorov-extension/" %}

Définition. Un tableau de différences de martingale est un tableau \(D_{n,k}\) tel que \(\mathbb E[D_{n,k}\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]=0\). Son processus de variance conditionnelle est \(V_n=\sum_k\mathbb E[D_{n,k}^2\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]\).

Théorème. Si \(V_n\to\sigma^2\) en probabilité et si la condition de Lindeberg conditionnelle \[ \sum_k\mathbb E[D_{n,k}^2 1_{\{|D_{n,k}|\gt \varepsilon\}}\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]\to0 \] vaut en probabilité pour tout \(\varepsilon\gt 0\), alors \(\sum_kD_{n,k}\Rightarrow N(0,\sigma^2)\).

Preuve. On utilise les fonctions caractéristiques conditionnelles. Pour \(u\) petit, \[ \mathbb E[e^{iuD_{n,k}}\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}] =1-\frac{u^2}{2}\mathbb E[D_{n,k}^2\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]+r_{n,k}. \] La condition de Lindeberg conditionnelle donne \(\sum_k r_{n,k}\to0\) en probabilité. Le produit des fonctions caractéristiques conditionnelles devient \[ \exp\left(-\frac{u^2}{2}V_n+o_P(1)\right)\to e^{-u^2\sigma^2/2}. \] Le théorème de Lévy donne la limite normale.

Remarque

Le théorème central limite pour martingales remplace l’indépendance par une moyenne conditionnelle nulle et une convergence de la variance conditionnelle.

鞅中心极限定理及应用

{% include probability-note-nav.html back_label="返回" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/notes/probability-theory/square-integrable-martingales-quadratic-variation/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/notes/probability-theory/kolmogorov-extension/" %}

定义. 鞅差三角阵是满足 \(\mathbb E[D_{n,k}\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]=0\) 的三角阵 \(D_{n,k}\)。它的条件方差过程为 \(V_n=\sum_k\mathbb E[D_{n,k}^2\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]\)。

定理. 若 \(V_n\to\sigma^2\) 依概率收敛，并且对每个 \(\varepsilon\gt 0\)，条件 Lindeberg 条件 \[ \sum_k\mathbb E[D_{n,k}^2 1_{\{|D_{n,k}|\gt \varepsilon\}}\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]\to0 \] 依概率成立，则 \(\sum_kD_{n,k}\Rightarrow N(0,\sigma^2)\)。

证明. 使用条件特征函数。对小的 \(u\)， \[ \mathbb E[e^{iuD_{n,k}}\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}] =1-\frac{u^2}{2}\mathbb E[D_{n,k}^2\mid\mathcal F_{n,k-1}]+r_{n,k}. \] 条件 Lindeberg 条件保证 \(\sum_k r_{n,k}\to0\) 依概率成立。条件特征函数相乘得到 \[ \exp\left(-\frac{u^2}{2}V_n+o_P(1)\right)\to e^{-u^2\sigma^2/2}. \] 由 Lévy 连续性定理得到正态极限。

说明

鞅中心极限定理用条件均值为零和条件方差收敛来替代独立性。
