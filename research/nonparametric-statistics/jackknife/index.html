Jackknife

{% include research-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/research/nonparametric-statistics/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/research/nonparametric-statistics/bootstrapping/" %}

The jackknife studies the effect of deleting one observation at a time. It is a simple tool for bias correction and variance estimation.

Leave-one-out statistics

Let \(T_n=T(X_1,\ldots,X_n)\), and let \(T_{(i)}\) be the same statistic computed after removing \(X_i\). The jackknife average is

\[\bar T_{(\cdot)}=\frac1n\sum_{i=1}^n T_{(i)}.\]

Bias correction

If \(E T_n=\theta+a/n+o(n^{-1})\), then

\[T_{\mathrm{jack}}=nT_n-(n-1)\bar T_{(\cdot)}\]

removes the leading \(a/n\) bias term.

Variance estimate

The jackknife variance estimate is

\[\widehat{\operatorname{Var}}_{\mathrm{jack}}(T_n)=\frac{n-1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^n\{T_{(i)}-\bar T_{(\cdot)}\}^2.\]

When it works

The jackknife works well for smooth statistics such as sample means and smooth functions of means. It can fail for non-smooth functionals such as sample quantiles, where deleting one point may not approximate the statistic's full sampling variability.

Jackknife

{% include research-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/research/nonparametric-statistics/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/research/nonparametric-statistics/bootstrapping/" %}

Le jackknife étudie l'effet de supprimer une observation à la fois. C'est un outil simple pour corriger le biais et estimer la variance.

Statistiques leave-one-out

Soit \(T_n=T(X_1,\ldots,X_n)\), et soit \(T_{(i)}\) la même statistique calculée sans \(X_i\). La moyenne jackknife est

\[\bar T_{(\cdot)}=\frac1n\sum_{i=1}^n T_{(i)}.\]

Correction de biais

Si \(E T_n=\theta+a/n+o(n^{-1})\), alors

\[T_{\mathrm{jack}}=nT_n-(n-1)\bar T_{(\cdot)}\]

supprime le terme principal de biais \(a/n\).

Estimateur de variance

L'estimateur jackknife de variance est

\[\widehat{\operatorname{Var}}_{\mathrm{jack}}(T_n)=\frac{n-1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^n\{T_{(i)}-\bar T_{(\cdot)}\}^2.\]

Quand cela fonctionne

Le jackknife fonctionne bien pour les statistiques lisses, comme les moyennes et les fonctions lisses de moyennes. Il peut échouer pour des fonctionnelles non lisses comme les quantiles empiriques.

刀切法

{% include research-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/research/nonparametric-statistics/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/research/nonparametric-statistics/bootstrapping/" %}

刀切法每次删除一个观测，考察统计量的变化。它是偏差校正和方差估计的简单工具。

留一统计量

设 \(T_n=T(X_1,\ldots,X_n)\)，令 \(T_{(i)}\) 为删除 \(X_i\) 后计算出的同一统计量。刀切法平均为

\[\bar T_{(\cdot)}=\frac1n\sum_{i=1}^n T_{(i)}.\]

偏差校正

若 \(E T_n=\theta+a/n+o(n^{-1})\)，则

\[T_{\mathrm{jack}}=nT_n-(n-1)\bar T_{(\cdot)}\]

可以消去主导的 \(a/n\) 偏差项。

方差估计

刀切法方差估计量为

\[\widehat{\operatorname{Var}}_{\mathrm{jack}}(T_n)=\frac{n-1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^n\{T_{(i)}-\bar T_{(\cdot)}\}^2.\]

适用范围

刀切法对光滑统计量效果好，例如样本均值和均值的光滑函数。对样本分位数等非光滑泛函，它可能无法捕捉完整的抽样波动。
