Curse of Dimensionality

{% include research-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/blessing-of-dimensionality/" %}

The curse of dimensionality means that many geometric and statistical tasks become exponentially harder as the ambient dimension \(d\) grows.

Grid intuition

To place \(m\) grid points on each coordinate of \([0,1]^d\), one needs \(m^d\) total points. If a Lipschitz function \(f\) is approximated by nearest-neighbor interpolation with mesh width \(h\), then \(|f(x)-f(x_i)|\le Lh\). Achieving error \(\varepsilon\) therefore requires roughly \(h\le\varepsilon/L\), hence \(n\asymp \varepsilon^{-d}\) points.

Volume effect

The volume of a Euclidean ball is

\[\operatorname{Vol}(B_d(r))=\frac{\pi^{d/2}}{\Gamma(d/2+1)}r^d.\]

The factor \(r^d\) means small radius reductions are severe in high dimension. Local neighborhoods become sparse unless the sample size grows very quickly.

Statistical consequence

For nonparametric estimation of a \(\beta\)-smooth function in \(d\) dimensions, the typical minimax mean-square rate has the form

\[n^{-2\beta/(2\beta+d)}.\]

As \(d\) increases, the exponent decreases. This is why structure such as sparsity, additivity, low rank, or manifold assumptions is essential in high-dimensional statistics.

Malédiction de la dimension

{% include research-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/blessing-of-dimensionality/" %}

La malédiction de la dimension désigne le fait que beaucoup de problèmes géométriques et statistiques deviennent exponentiellement plus difficiles lorsque la dimension \(d\) augmente.

Idée de grille

Mettre \(m\) points par coordonnée dans \([0,1]^d\) demande \(m^d\) points. Si une fonction lipschitzienne \(f\) est approchée par le plus proche voisin avec pas \(h\), alors \(|f(x)-f(x_i)|\le Lh\). Pour obtenir l'erreur \(\varepsilon\), il faut donc environ \(h\le\varepsilon/L\), donc \(n\asymp \varepsilon^{-d}\) points.

Effet de volume

Le volume d'une boule euclidienne vaut

\[\operatorname{Vol}(B_d(r))=\frac{\pi^{d/2}}{\Gamma(d/2+1)}r^d.\]

Le facteur \(r^d\) rend les petits voisinages très pauvres en grande dimension, sauf si la taille d'échantillon augmente très vite.

Conséquence statistique

Pour estimer une fonction \(\beta\)-régulière en dimension \(d\), le taux minimax quadratique typique est

\[n^{-2\beta/(2\beta+d)}.\]

Quand \(d\) augmente, l'exposant diminue. Il faut donc exploiter de la structure : parcimonie, additivité, rang faible ou hypothèse de variété.

维数灾难

{% include research-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/blessing-of-dimensionality/" %}

维数灾难指许多几何和统计任务会随环境维数 \(d\) 增大而指数级变难。

网格直观

若在 \([0,1]^d\) 的每个坐标方向放 \(m\) 个网格点，总点数为 \(m^d\)。若用网格最近邻近似 Lipschitz 函数 \(f\)，网格宽度为 \(h\)，则 \(|f(x)-f(x_i)|\le Lh\)。要达到误差 \(\varepsilon\)，大约需要 \(h\le\varepsilon/L\)，因此样本量 \(n\asymp \varepsilon^{-d}\)。

体积效应

欧氏球体积为

\[\operatorname{Vol}(B_d(r))=\frac{\pi^{d/2}}{\Gamma(d/2+1)}r^d.\]

其中 \(r^d\) 说明半径稍微变小，体积在高维中会急剧变小。因此局部邻域会非常稀疏，除非样本量增长很快。

统计后果

估计 \(d\) 维 \(\beta\)-光滑函数时，典型 minimax 均方误差速度为

\[n^{-2\beta/(2\beta+d)}.\]

当 \(d\) 增大，指数变小。因此高维统计必须利用结构，例如稀疏性、可加性、低秩或流形假设。
