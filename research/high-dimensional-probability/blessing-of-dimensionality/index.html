Blessing of Dimensionality

{% include research-topic-nav.html back_label="Back" back_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/" prev_label="Last page" prev_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/curse-of-dimensionality/" next_label="Next page" next_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/lasso-theory/" %}

High dimension is not only a difficulty. Under randomness, independence, or low-complexity structure, high-dimensional objects often become more regular.

Concentration

If \(X=(X_1,\ldots,X_d)\) has independent \(N(0,1)\) coordinates, then \(\|X\|_2^2\sim\chi_d^2\) and

\[\frac{\|X\|_2^2}{d}\to1\quad\text{in probability}.\]

Thus most points from a high-dimensional Gaussian lie near a thin shell of radius \(\sqrt d\). Random fluctuations average out.

Johnson-Lindenstrauss principle

For \(N\) fixed points in a high-dimensional Euclidean space and \(0<\varepsilon<1\), a random projection to

\[m\gtrsim \varepsilon^{-2}\log N\]

dimensions approximately preserves all pairwise distances with high probability. The required dimension depends logarithmically on the number of points, not on the original dimension.

Statistical lesson

High dimension can help when the effective complexity is small. Concentration inequalities, random projections, and sparse structures allow procedures whose error depends on \(\log p\) rather than \(p\).

Bénédiction de la dimension

{% include research-topic-nav.html back_label="Retour" back_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/" prev_label="Page précédente" prev_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/curse-of-dimensionality/" next_label="Page suivante" next_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/lasso-theory/" %}

La grande dimension n'est pas seulement une difficulté. Avec hasard, indépendance ou structure de faible complexité, les objets en grande dimension deviennent souvent plus réguliers.

Concentration

Si \(X=(X_1,\ldots,X_d)\) a des coordonnées indépendantes \(N(0,1)\), alors \(\|X\|_2^2\sim\chi_d^2\) et

\[\frac{\|X\|_2^2}{d}\to1\quad\text{en probabilité}.\]

Les points gaussiens se trouvent donc près d'une coquille fine de rayon \(\sqrt d\). Les fluctuations se moyennent.

Principe de Johnson-Lindenstrauss

Pour \(N\) points fixes dans un espace euclidien et \(0<\varepsilon<1\), une projection aléatoire vers

\[m\gtrsim \varepsilon^{-2}\log N\]

dimensions préserve approximativement toutes les distances deux à deux avec grande probabilité. La dimension requise dépend de \(\log N\), pas de la dimension originale.

Leçon statistique

La grande dimension aide lorsque la complexité effective est petite. Les inégalités de concentration, les projections aléatoires et la parcimonie donnent des erreurs dépendant de \(\log p\) plutôt que de \(p\).

维数祝福

{% include research-topic-nav.html back_label="返回" back_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/" prev_label="上一页" prev_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/curse-of-dimensionality/" next_label="下一页" next_url="/research/high-dimensional-probability/lasso-theory/" %}

高维并不总是坏事。在随机性、独立性或低复杂度结构下，高维对象常常表现出更强的规律性。

集中现象

若 \(X=(X_1,\ldots,X_d)\) 的坐标独立且服从 \(N(0,1)\)，则 \(\|X\|_2^2\sim\chi_d^2\)，并且

\[\frac{\|X\|_2^2}{d}\to1\quad\text{依概率收敛}.\]

因此高维高斯点大多落在半径约为 \(\sqrt d\) 的薄壳附近，随机波动会被平均掉。

Johnson-Lindenstrauss 原理

对高维欧氏空间中的 \(N\) 个固定点和 \(0<\varepsilon<1\)，随机投影到

\[m\gtrsim \varepsilon^{-2}\log N\]

维后，可以以高概率近似保持所有两两距离。所需维数依赖于 \(\log N\)，而不是原始维数。

统计启示

当有效复杂度较低时，高维可以带来优势。集中不等式、随机投影和稀疏结构可以让误差依赖 \(\log p\)，而不是直接依赖 \(p\)。
